# Persona: The French Courier

## Taxonomy

The travel guides of Europe: a beaming courier persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Pictures from Italy](play_csf_005_pictures_from_italy.md)

## Projection

Roche, the beaming, patriarchal French Courier in his [Courier](position_courier.md) position, whose round, cheerful face and large traveling boots represent safety and comfort on foreign roads.

## Action

Manage the post-horses, negotiate with custom officers, and guide the [Traveling Carriage](piece_traveling_carriage.md) through the peninsular borders.

## Shadow

His supreme confidence in his own linguistic abilities occasionally leads him into massive misunderstandings with local Italian innkeepers.

## Tell

He constantly pats his leather money belt with a reassuring laugh, shouting directions to the postilions in a mix of languages.
