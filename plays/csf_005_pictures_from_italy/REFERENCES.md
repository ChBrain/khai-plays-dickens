# Pictures from Italy: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's shorter work _Pictures from Italy_ (1846). A series of travel sketches, recording personal impressions of Italian cities, ruins, and ordinary social life.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _Pictures from Italy_, published in 1846, one of Shorter Fiction and Sketches in the house catalog (`csf_005`, [PI]). The text is in the public domain.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **The catalog is the index**: this warrant answers for one staged work; the full Dickens catalogue and its `csf_005` numbering live in the house register, not here.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
