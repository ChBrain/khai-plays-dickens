# Position: Traveller

## Taxonomy

The observers of foreign customs: a foreign traveller role.

## Owner

- Project: [Pictures from Italy](play_csf_005_pictures_from_italy.md)

## Has

A traveler notebook, letters of introduction, a purse of foreign currency, and a mandate to record impressions.

## Orders

Observe local scenery and customs, write letters and diary entries, inspect historical ruins, and avoid standard guidebook definitions.

## Loses

His travel notebook during a sudden storm, his patience at municipal custom houses, and his preconceptions of classical grandeur.

## Drives

The pursuit of human observation and emotional truth, looking past historical monuments to study the daily life of ordinary citizens.
