# Plot: Plantagenet Struggles

## Taxonomy

The fytte of medieval kings: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [A Child's History of England](play_csf_010_childs_history.md)

## Cue

A fresh log is added to the fire as the lessons continue.

## Action

[Charles Dickens](persona_dickens.md) turns a page of the [History Manuscript](piece_history_manuscript.md) to narrate the reign of King John, explaining how the nobles forced the monarch to sign the Magna Carta.

## Stage

[Charles Dickens](persona_dickens.md) reads to the [Child Reader](persona_child_reader.md) by the [Study Hearth](place_study_hearth.md), using the story of the lost [Royal Crown](piece_royal_crown.md) in the Wash to illustrate the vanity of corrupt rulers.

## Tension

The constant wars of the medieval barons and the suffering of the common people raise deep questions in the mind of the young listener about the justice of kingship.
