# Piece: History Manuscript

## Taxonomy

The written records of the nation: a history manuscript.

## Owner

- Project: [A Child's History of England](play_csf_010_childs_history.md)

## Place

[Study Hearth](place_study_hearth.md)

## Load Bearing

The capability to narrate and evaluate the past. Without this manuscript containing the moral evaluations of each reign, the father cannot conduct his lessons.

## Apparent

A stack of paper pages covered in close handwriting with many ink crossings out and marginal notes.

## Yearbook

- Yesterday, the writer drafted the account of the Civil War at his desk.
- Today, the father turns the pages as he reads to the children by the hearth.
- Tomorrow, the manuscript travels to the publishing office for serial printing.
