# Position: Pupil

## Taxonomy

The students of history: a listening pupil role.

## Owner

- Project: [A Child's History of England](play_csf_010_childs_history.md)

## Has

A small footstool by the fire, a notebook for sketches, and a quiet attention.

## Orders

Listen to the historical stories, ask questions about past customs, and reflect on the moral lessons of the narrative.

## Loses

His naive belief in the absolute goodness of kings as he learns about their political ambitions and crimes.

## Drives

Curiosity about the origins of his country and how its modern freedom was won through past struggles.
