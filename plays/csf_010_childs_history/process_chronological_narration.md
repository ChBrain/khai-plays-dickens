# Process: Chronological Narration

## Taxonomy

The analysis of historical progress: a chronological narration process.

## Owner

- Project: [A Child's History of England](play_csf_010_childs_history.md)

## Initiated by

The father starting to read the historical manuscript to his children by the study hearth.

## Direction

The retelling of national events in a sequential order while applying moral judgments to the actions of past rulers.

## Lever

The instructor voice and historical records meeting the child interest and willingness to learn.

## Echo

The completion of the narrative timeline, leaving the listener with a deep respect for constitutional progress and liberty.
