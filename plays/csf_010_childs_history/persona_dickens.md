# Persona: Charles Dickens

## Taxonomy

The writers of Victorian England: a father and history narrator persona.

## Owner

- Project: [A Child's History of England](play_csf_010_childs_history.md)

## Projection

Charles Dickens, an expressive father in his [Instructor](position_instructor.md) position, whose storytelling voice and vivid vocabulary bring dead monarchs to life.

## Action

Read the handwritten pages, interpret the causes of historical wars, and answer the questions of the children.

## Shadow

He can let his strong personal dislikes of historical figures lead to highly biased descriptions.

## Tell

He gestures dramatically with his reading spectacles while describing famous battles or court intrigues.
