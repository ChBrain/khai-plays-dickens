# Place: Tower of London

## Taxonomy

The historic prisons of London: a stone fortress.

## Owner

- Project: [A Child's History of England](play_csf_010_childs_history.md)

## Shown

The dark and massive stone towers of the fortress, reflecting in the muddy waters of the Thames river.

## Holds

Damp prison cells, heavy iron chains, executioner blocks, and the royal crown jewels.

## Offers

A grim setting that illustrates the absolute power and cruelty of ancient monarchs.

## Withheld

Comfort, justice, and the common rights of the citizens imprisoned within the walls.
