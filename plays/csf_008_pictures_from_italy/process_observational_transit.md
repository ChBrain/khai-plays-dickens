# Process: Observational Transit

## Taxonomy

The writer's translation of scenery to record: an observational transit process.

## Owner

- Project: [Pictures from Italy](play_csf_008_pictures_from_italy.md)

## Initiated by

The departure of the traveling carriage from Paris, beginning the journey across France and the Italian borders.

## Direction

The translation of visual impressions and local conversations into written sketches, showing how a transient traveller records the spirit of a place.

## Lever

The traveler notebook and traveling carriage meeting Charles Dickens's traveler role and the courier's logistical guide.

## Echo

The return of the carriage across the Swiss mountain passes, leaving behind a finished record of southern impressions.
