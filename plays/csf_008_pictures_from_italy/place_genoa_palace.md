# Place: Genoa Palace

## Taxonomy

The grand decaying villas of Genoa: a pink-washed palace.

## Owner

- Project: [Pictures from Italy](play_csf_008_pictures_from_italy.md)

## Shown

The pink-washed Villa Bagnerello in the suburbs of Genoa, with grand, drafty rooms, frescoed ceilings peeling from dampness, and a wild garden filled with grapevines and fig trees.

## Holds

An observational desk, traveler correspondence, folding chairs, and local wine flasks.

## Offers

A quiet, semi-permanent refuge for observation, smelling of garden earth and old plaster.

## Withheld

Northern English comforts and coal fires, which the drafty stone corridors refuse to hold.
