# Position: Courier

## Taxonomy

The managers of peninsular travel: a professional courier role.

## Owner

- Project: [Pictures from Italy](play_csf_008_pictures_from_italy.md)

## Has

A leather travel belt, a set of passport documents, a whip for post-horses, and a mandate to manage the carriage transit.

## Orders

Negotiate with postilions and innkeepers, settle custom house disputes, manage travel luggage, and guide the travelers safely.

## Loses

His booming voice when arguing with border guards, and his patriarchal dignity when a post-horse collapses on the road.

## Drives

The preservation of traveling efficiency and safety, resolving every logistical problem with good humor and energetic conversation.
