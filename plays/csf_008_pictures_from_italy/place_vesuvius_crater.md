# Place: Vesuvius Crater

## Taxonomy

The volcanic summits of Naples: a volcanic crater.

## Owner

- Project: [Pictures from Italy](play_csf_008_pictures_from_italy.md)

## Shown

The dark, ash-covered summit of Mt. Vesuvius at night, lit by the red glow of bubbling lava and surrounded by thick clouds of sulfurous steam.

## Holds

Volcanic ash, sulfur beds, iron-tipped walking staffs, and flickering lanterns.

## Offers

A dramatic, terrifying view of subterranean power, smelling of hot iron and brimstone.

## Withheld

Stable, solid footing and cool air, which the shaking ash slopes refuse to provide.
