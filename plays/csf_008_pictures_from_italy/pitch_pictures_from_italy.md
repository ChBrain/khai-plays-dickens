## Tenor

A sunlit yet dusty peninsular landscape where ancient white marble ruins crumble next to red tiled roofs, and the scent of wild grapes and dry stone mixes with the smell of carriage leather and Swiss mountain pine.

## Undertow

The rhythmic clatter of iron carriage wheels on stone roads, the distant bells of mountain churches, the shouting of Italian postilions, and the low hiss of volcanic gas on the mountain slopes.

## Nerve

The insatiable curiosity of a northern traveller who seeks to look past standard historical commentary to find the human reality of the southern streets.

## Echo

A persistent realization that the grandest historical empires pass away into ruin, leaving only the simple comedy and tragedy of the common people.
