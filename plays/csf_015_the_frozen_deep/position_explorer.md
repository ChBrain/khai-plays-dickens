# Position: Explorer

## Taxonomy

The polar explorers of the search: an explorer role.

## Owner

- Project: [The Frozen Deep](play_csf_015_the_frozen_deep.md)

## Has

An expedition journal, a compass, a thick winter coat, and a ration tin.

## Orders

Endure the extreme Arctic cold, maintain discipline inside the frozen shelter, and trek across the ice fields to find safety.

## Loses

His hope when the food supplies run low, and his life if abandoned in the deep snow.

## Drives

The search for survival and navigation through the polar wastes despite despair and rivalry.
