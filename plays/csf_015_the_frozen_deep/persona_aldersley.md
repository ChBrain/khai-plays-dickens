# Persona: Frank Aldersley

## Taxonomy

The innocent, successful lovers: a young explorer and betrothed suitor persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Frozen Deep](play_csf_015_the_frozen_deep.md)

## Projection

Frank Aldersley, Clara's betrothed lover operating in his [Explorer](position_explorer.md) position, who remains unaware of Wardour's hatred until they are stranded on the ice.

## Action

Accompany the Arctic expedition, wear Clara's ring, fall ill in the extreme cold, and rely on Wardour's strength for rescue.

## Shadow

His youthful innocence and physical weakness make him vulnerable to both the harsh Arctic environment and Wardour's vengeful impulses.

## Tell

He looks at his betrothal ring for comfort and speaks warmly of Clara to his companion.
