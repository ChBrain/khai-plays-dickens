## Taxonomy

## Owner

## Cue

A misty morning on the shores of Newfoundland.

## Action

[Richard Wardour](persona_wardour.md) executes his [Rescue Plan](plan_rescue.md), bringing [Frank Aldersley](persona_aldersley.md) to safety at [The Newfoundland Coast](place_newfoundland_coast.md) where [Clara Burnham](persona_clara.md) and [Lieutenant Crayford](persona_crayford.md) are waiting.

## Stage

[Richard Wardour](persona_wardour.md) lays the saved [Frank Aldersley](persona_aldersley.md) at the feet of [Clara Burnham](persona_clara.md), presenting her with the betrothal ring before collapsing and dying of exhaustion in her arms.

## Tension

The emotional climax as Wardour audits his own bitterness and achieves redemption through self-sacrifice, securing the lovers' future.
