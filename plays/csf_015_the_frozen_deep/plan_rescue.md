# Plan: Rescue Plan

## Taxonomy

The design of a self-sacrificing rescue: a rescue plan.

## Owner

- Owner: [Richard Wardour](persona_wardour.md)

## Direction

To save the life of Frank Aldersley and return him to Clara Burnham, overcoming his own jealousy and sacrificing his own life in the process.

## Orders

Wardour is ordered to find the lost Aldersley in the snowstorm, carry him across the ice fields, protect him from freezing, and deliver him to the survivors.

## Implementation

The snow blankets, the search compass, and the last remaining energy of Wardour's body.

## Targets

- [ ] Richard Wardour overcomes his bitter anger and sets out to search for Frank Aldersley
- [ ] Wardour finds the freezing Aldersley in a snowdrift on the ice field
- [ ] Wardour carries Aldersley through the polar storm toward the Newfoundland coast
- [ ] Wardour delivers Aldersley to Clara Burnham and dies of exhaustion
