# Persona: Richard Wardour

## Taxonomy

The bitter, vengeful rivals: a rejected lover and polar explorer persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Frozen Deep](play_csf_015_the_frozen_deep.md)

## Projection

Richard Wardour, a rejected lover operating in his [Explorer](position_explorer.md) position, who harbors a deep hatred for his unknown rival until he discovers it is Frank Aldersley.

## Action

Join the Arctic expedition, discover his rival's identity through Clara's ring, struggle with the temptation of murder on the ice, and execute his rescue plan.

## Shadow

His obsessive bitterness and thirst for vengeance threaten to turn him into a murderer when isolated in the polar waste.

## Tell

He glares silently at the ice, mutters Clara's name, and clenches his fists when looking at Frank.
