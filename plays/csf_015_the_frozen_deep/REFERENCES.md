# The Frozen Deep: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens and Wilkie Collins's shorter work _The Frozen Deep_ (1856). A theatrical drama of self-sacrifice, staging an Arctic expedition, romantic rivalry, and a redemptive rescue.

## Origin

Charles Dickens and Wilkie Collins, _The Frozen Deep_, published in 1856, one of Shorter Fiction and Sketches in the house catalog (`csf_015`, [TFD]). The text is in the public domain.
