# Persona: Lieutenant Crayford

## Taxonomy

The rational officers of the expedition: an officer persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Frozen Deep](play_csf_015_the_frozen_deep.md)

## Projection

Lieutenant Crayford, a rational and steady naval officer operating in his [Explorer](position_explorer.md) position, who tries to maintain hope among the shipwrecked crew.

## Action

Keep the expedition journal, ration the food supplies, lead the trek across the ice, and welcome Wardour's redemptive return on the coast.

## Shadow

His reliance on discipline and reason makes it difficult for him to understand the dark, emotional rivalry brewing between Wardour and Aldersley.

## Tell

He writes in his logbook with steady strokes and checks the food crates with a serious expression.
