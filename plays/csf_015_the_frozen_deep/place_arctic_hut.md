# Place: Arctic Hut

## Taxonomy

The desperate survival shelters of the ice: a polar hut place.

## Owner

- Project: [The Frozen Deep](play_csf_015_the_frozen_deep.md)

## Shown

A drafty wooden hut built from ship timbers, surrounded by towering blocks of ice under the dark polar sky.

## Holds

Expedition journals, empty ration crates, blankets, a small stove, and frozen tools.

## Offers

A temporary shield against the freezing wind, and a space where rivalries boil over.
