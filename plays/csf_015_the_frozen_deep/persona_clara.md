# Persona: Clara Burnham

## Taxonomy

The dread-filled watchers: a young lady with premonitions persona.

## Owner

## Projection

Clara Burnham, a young lady operating in her [Watcher](position_watcher.md) position, who feels a supernatural dread that her two suitors will meet in the Arctic.

## Action

Give her ring to Frank, wait anxiously in England for news of the expedition, and suffer from vivid premonitions of the ice.

Her fear and guilt over rejecting Wardour paralyze her, leaving her helpless to do anything but watch and wait for fate to unfold.
