# Plot: The Trek

## Taxonomy

The fytte of the ice march: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [The Frozen Deep](play_csf_015_the_frozen_deep.md)

## Cue

A blizzard howling across the open polar ice pack.

## Action

[Richard Wardour](persona_wardour.md) and [Frank Aldersley](persona_aldersley.md) in their [Explorer](position_explorer.md) positions march across the ice fields during the [Arctic Survival](process_arctic_survival.md) trek, becoming separated from the main party.

## Stage

[Richard Wardour](persona_wardour.md) stands over the exhausted, collapsing [Frank Aldersley](persona_aldersley.md), struggling with the dark temptation to leave his rival to freeze to death in the snow.

## Tension

The moral struggle of Wardour as he decides between murder by abandonment and the difficult call of self-sacrifice.
