# Process: Arctic Survival

## Taxonomy

The process of polar exploration and survival: an Arctic survival process.

## Owner

- Project: [The Frozen Deep](play_csf_015_the_frozen_deep.md)

## Initiated by

The stranding of the expedition ships in the polar ice pack, forcing the crew to build a shelter.

## Direction

The step-by-step decline of food rations, dropping temperatures, abandonment of the wooden hut, and the long trek across the ice.

## Lever

The Expedition Journal and the leadership of the officers trying to preserve life.

## Echo

The arrival on the Newfoundland Coast and the redemptive rescue of Frank Aldersley by Richard Wardour.
