# Plot: Association Meeting

## Taxonomy

The fytte of scientific invasion: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [The Mudfog Papers](play_csf_002_the_mudfog_papers.md)

## Cue

A foggy afternoon at the Mudfog docks, where steamships arrive from London carrying the illustrious members of the scientific society.

## Action

[Professor Snore](persona_snore.md) leads the incoming professors to [Association Hall](place_association_hall.md), assuming his [Academic](position_academic.md) position, and delivers a tedious opening address on the progress of everything.

## Stage

[Professor Snore](persona_snore.md) occupies the speaker's lectern with his notebooks, while [Nicholas Tulrumble](persona_tulrumble.md) sits on the stage as the [Mayor](position_mayor.md) to welcome the visitors.

## Tension

[Professor Snore](persona_snore.md) details absurd, abstract scientific equations, while the practical townspeople grow increasingly bored and restless under the academic jargon.
