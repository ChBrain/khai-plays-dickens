# The Mudfog Papers: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's shorter work _The Mudfog Papers_ (1837). A parody of scientific vanity, staging a mayor's disastrous pageant and the absurd scientific exhibitions.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _The Mudfog Papers_, published from 1837 to 1838, one of Shorter Fiction and Sketches in the house catalog (`csf_002`, [MP]). The text is in the public domain.
