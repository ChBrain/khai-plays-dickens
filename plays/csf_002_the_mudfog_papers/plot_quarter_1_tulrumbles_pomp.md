# Plot: Tulrumble's Pomp

## Taxonomy

The fytte of municipal vanity: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [The Mudfog Papers](play_csf_002_the_mudfog_papers.md)

## Cue

A damp, chilly morning in [Mudfog Town](place_mudfog_town.md), where the streets are wet and a crowd gathers outside the municipal offices.

## Action

[Nicholas Tulrumble](persona_tulrumble.md) assumes his [Mayor](position_mayor.md) gown and tries to execute his [Grandiose Pageant](plan_grandiose_pageant.md) with the [Mayoral Mace](piece_mayoral_mace.md) under the key of the [Mudfog Papers Pitch](pitch_the_mudfog_papers.md), but the parade descends into chaos when he becomes stuck in his heavy brass armor.

## Stage

[Nicholas Tulrumble](persona_tulrumble.md) stands on the balcony in his heavy brass armor, while the town council and local citizens watch from the muddy cobblestones below.

## Tension

[Nicholas Tulrumble](persona_tulrumble.md) tries to maintain a dignified, majestic posture, while the weight of his brass armor prevents him from moving and exposes him to public ridicule.
