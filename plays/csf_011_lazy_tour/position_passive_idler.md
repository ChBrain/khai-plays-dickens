# Position: Passive Idler

## Taxonomy

The passive travelers of Cumberland: a passive idler role.

## Owner

- Project: [The Lazy Tour of Two Idle Apprentices](play_csf_011_lazy_tour.md)

## Has

A comfortable sofa, a bandage for his sprained ankle, a smoking pipe, and a deep dislike of exercise.

## Orders

Avoid all physical effort, rest his sprained ankle in the inn, and tell dark stories to pass the hours of recovery.

## Loses

His physical mobility during the fell climb, and his quiet comfort when forced to travel.

## Drives

The pursuit of complete relaxation, defending the belief that avoiding work is the most sensible way to live.
