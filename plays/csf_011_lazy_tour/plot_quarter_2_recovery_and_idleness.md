# Plot: Recovery and Idleness

## Taxonomy

The fytte of recovery and reminiscence: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [The Lazy Tour of Two Idle Apprentices](play_csf_011_lazy_tour.md)

## Cue

A quiet inn room at Allonby, where the sound of the sea comes through the window.

## Action

[Francis Goodchild](persona_francis_goodchild.md) sits at the table, leaning his [Climbing Staff](piece_climbing_staff.md) against the wall, while [Thomas Idle](persona_thomas_idle.md) rests on the sofa and dictates his own comic history of idleness for their journal under the process of [Collaborative Writing](process_collaborative_writing.md).

## Stage

[Thomas Idle](persona_thomas_idle.md) in his [Passive Idler](position_passive_idler.md) position recounts how every disaster of his life sprang from being lured into activity - the school prize that made him an outcast, the single game of cricket that gave him a fever.

## Tension

The physical helplessness of the injured traveler set against his cheerful proof that idleness is the only safe course in life.
