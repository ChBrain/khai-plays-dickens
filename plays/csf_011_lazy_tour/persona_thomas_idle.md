# Persona: Thomas Idle

## Taxonomy

The apprentices of literature: a lazy, passive traveler persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Lazy Tour of Two Idle Apprentices](play_csf_011_lazy_tour.md)

## Projection

Thomas Idle, a born and bred passive apprentice in his [Passive Idler](position_passive_idler.md) position, whose chief ambition is to avoid all physical activity.

## Action

Sprain his ankle on the mountain side, lie on the sofa during recovery, and write dark stories for their journal.

## Shadow

He can be stubborn and lazy, leaving all the practical duties and arrangements of the journey to his companion.

## Tell

He sighs loudly whenever travel is mentioned and rests his bandaged ankle on a pile of cushions.
