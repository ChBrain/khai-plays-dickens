# Piece: Race Card

## Taxonomy

The booklets of chance: a horse-race card.

## Owner

- Project: [The Lazy Tour of Two Idle Apprentices](play_csf_011_lazy_tour.md)

## Place

[Doncaster Racecourse](place_doncaster_racecourse.md)

## Load Bearing

The information needed for betting. Without this card showing the horse names and race times, the active traveler cannot place his bets.

## Apparent

A thin printed paper booklet containing lists of racing horses, jockey colors, and owners.

## Yearbook

- Yesterday, the race card was printed by the local Doncaster press.
- Today, the card is purchased in the crowd and marked with pencil notes on the St. Leger race.
- Tomorrow, the card is left behind on the racecourse grass as trash.
