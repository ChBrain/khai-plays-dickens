# Position: Active Wanderer

## Taxonomy

The energetic travelers of northern roads: an active wanderer role.

## Owner

- Project: [The Lazy Tour of Two Idle Apprentices](play_csf_011_lazy_tour.md)

## Has

A climbing staff, a pocket notebook, a compass, and a restless physical energy.

## Orders

Plan the walking routes, encourage active travel, climb the local fells, and record observations in the travel journal.

## Loses

His direction on the misty mountain slopes, his patience with his passive partner, and his money at the Doncaster races.

## Drives

The constant urge to remain active, turning every simple holiday excursion into a productive writing task.
