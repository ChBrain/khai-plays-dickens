# Plot: Escape and the Fell

## Taxonomy

The fytte of the mountain climb: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [The Lazy Tour of Two Idle Apprentices](play_csf_011_lazy_tour.md)

## Cue

A damp morning in Cumberland, where the steep hills rise into the clouds.

## Action

[Francis Goodchild](persona_francis_goodchild.md) in his [Active Wanderer](position_active_wanderer.md) position uses his [Climbing Staff](piece_climbing_staff.md) to lead the reluctant [Thomas Idle](persona_thomas_idle.md) up the wet slopes of [Carrock Fell](place_carrock_fell.md), executing the initial stages of the [Walking Excursion Plan](plan_walking_excursion.md) under the direction of the [Lazy Tour Pitch](pitch_lazy_tour.md).

## Stage

[Francis Goodchild](persona_francis_goodchild.md) gains the mist-blind summit of [Carrock Fell](place_carrock_fell.md), but on the streaming descent [Thomas Idle](persona_thomas_idle.md) turns his ankle among the wet stones by the running water.

## Tension

The thick mist and the sudden injury of the passive partner turn a holiday escape into a difficult rescue struggle.
