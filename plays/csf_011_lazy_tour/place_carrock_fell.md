# Place: Carrock Fell

## Taxonomy

The mountains of Cumberland: a steep, misty fell.

## Owner

- Project: [The Lazy Tour of Two Idle Apprentices](play_csf_011_lazy_tour.md)

## Shown

The steep and grassy slopes of the mountain rising into a thick, wet mist that covers the trail and blinds the hikers.

## Holds

Loose wet stones, slippery grass paths, cold driving rain, and hidden peat bogs.

## Offers

A wild and exhausting challenge to the climber, testing his strength and resolution.

## Withheld

Safe pathways, clear signs, and any shelter from the cold mountain wind.
