# Place: Lancaster Inn

## Taxonomy

The old coaching inns of Lancaster: a haunted lodging house.

## Owner

- Project: [The Lazy Tour of Two Idle Apprentices](play_csf_011_lazy_tour.md)

## Shown

A great, dark, panelled old inn at Lancaster whose gloomy upper room - the Bride's Chamber - broods over the travelers as night falls.

## Holds

Heavy four-poster beds, creaking staircases, a shuttered window, and the century-old presence of a hanged man.

## Offers

A night's uneasy lodging and the Gothic tale of the bride killed by her husband's will.

## Withheld

Sound sleep, plain daylight comfort, and any easy dismissal of the haunting.
