# Pitch: Lazy Tour Pitch

## Taxonomy

The tuning of tone for the whole play: a production directive.

## Owner

- Owner: [The Lazy Tour of Two Idle Apprentices](play_csf_011_lazy_tour.md)

## Tenor

A damp, windy landscape of northern fells and coastal villages, where heavy rains and grey mists contrast with the bright, noisy confusion of racecourses and crowded inns.

## Undertow

The slow, quiet ticking of hours spent resting on a sofa, the soft scratch of collaborative writing pens, and the sudden, distant roar of race spectators at Doncaster.

## Nerve

The humorous clash between two writing partners, where one insists on constant, energetic movement and the other fiercely defends the absolute right to do nothing.

## Echo

A persistent reminder that fleeing from literature and work only brings the traveler back to the noise of the world, and that true idleness is difficult to find.
