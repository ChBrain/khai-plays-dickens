# The Lazy Tour of Two Idle Apprentices: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's shorter work _The Lazy Tour of Two Idle Apprentices_ (1857). A satirical travelogue, tracing two idle apprentices fleeing literature through Cumberland and Doncaster.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _The Lazy Tour of Two Idle Apprentices_, published in 1857, one of Shorter Fiction and Sketches in the house catalog (`csf_011`, [LT]). The text is in the public domain.
