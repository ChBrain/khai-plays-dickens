# Process: Collaborative Writing

## Taxonomy

The process of shared literary creation: a collaborative writing process.

## Owner

- Project: [The Lazy Tour of Two Idle Apprentices](play_csf_011_lazy_tour.md)

## Initiated by

The decision of the two travelers to write down their travel adventures, blending the active partner's descriptions and ghost story with the idler's comic history.

## Direction

The combination of different styles, where the active partner writes energetic descriptions and the dark tale of the Bride's Chamber, and the idler drafts his comic history of doing nothing.

## Lever

The active wanderer observations meeting the passive partner imagination and the pages of their joint journal.

## Echo

The publication of the completed chapters in Household Words, establishing a shared record of their Cumberland tour.
