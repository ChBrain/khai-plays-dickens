# Plot: The Bride's Chamber

## Taxonomy

The fytte of the haunted lodging: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [The Lazy Tour of Two Idle Apprentices](play_csf_011_lazy_tour.md)

## Cue

A shuttered upper room at the [Lancaster Inn](place_lancaster_inn.md), as night comes on.

## Action

[Francis Goodchild](persona_francis_goodchild.md) in his [Active Wanderer](position_active_wanderer.md) position keeps a restless vigil in the haunted [Lancaster Inn](place_lancaster_inn.md) while [Thomas Idle](persona_thomas_idle.md) sleeps, and sets down the ghost story he receives there for their journal under [Collaborative Writing](process_collaborative_writing.md).

## Stage

[Francis Goodchild](persona_francis_goodchild.md) describes the apparition of the old man - hanged at Lancaster a hundred years before - who tells the tale of a bride killed by the sheer will of her husband.

## Tension

The restless watcher's Gothic imagining presses against [Thomas Idle](persona_thomas_idle.md)'s single wish only to sleep undisturbed.
