# Plot: Injury and Ghosts

## Taxonomy

The fytte of recovery and stories: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [The Lazy Tour of Two Idle Apprentices](play_csf_011_lazy_tour.md)

## Cue

A quiet inn room at Allonby, where the sound of the sea comes through the window.

## Action

[Francis Goodchild](persona_francis_goodchild.md) sits at the table, leaning his [Climbing Staff](piece_climbing_staff.md) against the wall, while [Thomas Idle](persona_thomas_idle.md) rests on the sofa and begins to write down a dark ghost story for their journal under the process of [Collaborative Writing](process_collaborative_writing.md).

## Stage

[Thomas Idle](persona_thomas_idle.md) in his [Passive Idler](position_passive_idler.md) position describes the chilling tale of the dead hand to [Francis Goodchild](persona_francis_goodchild.md).

## Tension

The physical helplessness of the injured traveler and the dark themes of the ghost story create a gloomy atmosphere inside the small inn room.
