# George Silverman's Explanation: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's shorter work _George Silverman's Explanation_ (1868). A melancholy monologue of cellar childhood, preacher hypocrisy, and quiet misunderstandings of self-sacrifice.
