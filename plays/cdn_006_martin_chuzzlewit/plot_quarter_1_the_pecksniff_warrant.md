# Plot: The Pecksniff Warrant

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Martin Chuzzlewit](play_cdn_006_martin_chuzzlewit.md)

## Cue

A winter Wiltshire morning, starting inside [Pecksniff House](place_pecksniff_home.md).

## Action

[Young Martin](persona_young_martin.md) is apprenticed to [Pecksniff](persona_pecksniff.md) in his [Architect](position_architect.md) position, before [Old Martin](persona_old_martin.md) demands his dismissal under the key of the [Selfish Reform Pitch](pitch_selfish_reform.md).

## Stage

Young Martin draws the grammar school elevation in the studio, which Pecksniff claims as his own [Pecksniff Elevation](piece_pecksniff_elevation.md) before dismissing the boy.

## Tension

Pecksniff bows to the patriarch's demands to secure his favor, turning Young Martin out into the cold streets.
