# Persona: Pecksniff

## Taxonomy

The hypocrites of Wiltshire: an oily architect persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Martin Chuzzlewit](play_cdn_006_martin_chuzzlewit.md)

## Projection

Seth Pecksniff, a tall man in his [Architect](position_architect.md) position, with a high standing collar, hair that stands up, and a face that beams with fake benevolence.

## Action

Dismiss Young Martin, flatter old Martin to secure his fortune, invest in the Anglo-Bengalee, and end his days exposed as a beggar.

## Shadow

His constant talk of virtue and gratitude is a tool of deception, hiding a cold avarice that exploits both pupils and relatives.

## Tell

He strokes his chest, raises his hands, and speaks in oily, soft tones about the beauty of virtue.
