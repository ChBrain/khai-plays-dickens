# Plot: The American Swamp

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Martin Chuzzlewit](play_cdn_006_martin_chuzzlewit.md)

## Cue

A damp, foggy evening along the Mississippi, starting inside [Eden Swamp](place_eden_swamp.md).

## Action

[Young Martin](persona_young_martin.md) and [Mark Tapley](persona_mark_tapley.md) execute the [American Emigration Plan](plan_american_emigration.md), purchasing a useless plot of land in the swamp and falling ill with fever.

## Stage

Young Martin lies on a rough bed in a decaying log cabin, clutching his useless [Eden Deed](piece_eden_deed.md), while Mark Tapley nurses him.

## Tension

The fever illness and Mark's selfless care purge Martin of his selfish pride, reforming his character before they return to England.
