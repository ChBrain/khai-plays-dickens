# Plot: The Murder and Ruin

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Martin Chuzzlewit](play_cdn_006_martin_chuzzlewit.md)

## Cue

A dark, stormy night in the Wiltshire woods, shifting to [Pecksniff House](place_pecksniff_home.md).

## Action

[Jonas Chuzzlewit](persona_jonas.md) murders [Montague Tigg](persona_tigg.md) in the woods, ending the [Speculative Bubble](process_speculative_bubble.md) process as [Old Martin](persona_old_martin.md) exposes [Pecksniff](persona_pecksniff.md) and reconciles with [Young Martin](persona_young_martin.md) after his [American Emigration Plan](plan_american_emigration.md).

## Stage

Old Martin stands in the home, raising his cane to strike Pecksniff, while Jonas is arrested and takes poison.

## Tension

The exposure of Pecksniff's plagiarism and Jonas's murder resolves the family's fortunes, ending the usurer's schemes and blessing the marriage.
