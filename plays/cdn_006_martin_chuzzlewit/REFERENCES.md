# Martin Chuzzlewit: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's novel _The Life and Adventures of Martin Chuzzlewit_ (1843). A story of selfishness and hypocrisy, staging architectural deception, speculative bubbles, and swampy moral reform.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _The Life and Adventures of Martin Chuzzlewit_, published in monthly parts from 1843 to 1844, one of The Novels in the house catalog (`cdn_006`, [MC]). The text is in the public domain.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **The catalog is the index**: this warrant answers for one staged work; the full Dickens catalogue and its `cdn_006` numbering live in the house register, not here.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
