# Persona: Jonas Chuzzlewit

## Taxonomy

The greedy relatives of London: a murderous nephew persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Martin Chuzzlewit](play_cdn_006_martin_chuzzlewit.md)

## Projection

A mean, narrow-chested young man, with a surly, suspicious face and a calculating eye, who grudges every penny spent.

## Action

Poison his father's medicine, marry Pecksniff's daughter Mercy, invest in Anglo-Bengalee, murder Tigg, and commit suicide.

## Shadow

His greed and fear drive him to crime, eventually leading to his exposure by Nadgett and his self-destruction.

## Tell

He counts his pocket-money, scowls at his wife, and mutters about legal costs.
