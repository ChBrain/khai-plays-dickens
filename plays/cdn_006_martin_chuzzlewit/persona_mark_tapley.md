# Persona: Mark Tapley

## Taxonomy

The jolly helpers of England: a selfless companion persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Martin Chuzzlewit](play_cdn_006_martin_chuzzlewit.md)

## Projection

A broad-shouldered, active young man, with a jolly, laughing face and a constant desire to find credit in being jolly under adversity.

## Action

Serve Young Martin, sail to America, nurse Martin through the swamp fever, and return to manage the Blue Dragon Inn.

## Shadow

His selfless devotion is the moral balance to Martin's selfishness, acting as the agent of Martin's ethical conversion.

## Tell

He winks, whistles a lively tune, and declares: "There's some credit in being jolly here!"
