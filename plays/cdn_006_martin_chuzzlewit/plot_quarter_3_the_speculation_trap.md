# Plot: The Speculation Trap

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Martin Chuzzlewit](play_cdn_006_martin_chuzzlewit.md)

## Cue

A grand London morning, starting inside [Anglo-Bengalee Office](place_anglo_bengalee.md).

## Action

[Montague Tigg](persona_tigg.md) in his [Speculator](position_speculator.md) position inflates the [Speculative Bubble](process_speculative_bubble.md) process, drawing in [Pecksniff](persona_pecksniff.md) and [Jonas Chuzzlewit](persona_jonas.md) to purchase shares.

## Stage

Tigg displays the gold-rimmed [Assurance Share](piece_assurance_share.md) certificates on his desk, while Jonas agrees to join the board to secure his fortune.

## Tension

Jonas's poison secrets make him a target for Tigg's blackmail, forcing him deeper into the fraudulent bubble company's trap.
