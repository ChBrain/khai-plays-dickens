# Persona: Young Martin

## Taxonomy

The disinherited sons of England: a young protagonist persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Martin Chuzzlewit](play_cdn_006_martin_chuzzlewit.md)

## Projection

A handsome, well-educated young man, who carries a proud, selfish spirit and treats his companions' assistance as his natural right.

## Action

Apprentice to Pecksniff, sail to America, buy the Eden deed, fall ill in the swamp, realize his own selfishness, and reconcile with his grandfather.

## Shadow

His selfishness blinds him to the devotion of Mark Tapley, before the hardships of the swamp purge his pride and reform his character.

## Tell

He commands Mark with an air of superiority and sighs over his disinheritance.
