# Persona: Old Martin

## Taxonomy

The wealthy patriarchs of England: a suspicious patriarch persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Martin Chuzzlewit](play_cdn_006_martin_chuzzlewit.md)

## Projection

A wealthy, grey-haired old man, whose suspicious face reflects a life spent guarding his fortune against greedy relatives.

## Action

Disinherit his grandson Young Martin, pretend to fall under Pecksniff's influence to test him, and eventually expose the hypocrite.

## Shadow

His wealth has made him suspicious of all affection, leading him to test his family by elaborate deceptions.

## Tell

He leans heavily on his cane and watches Pecksniff with a silent, calculating gaze.
