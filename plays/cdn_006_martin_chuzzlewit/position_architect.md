# Position: Architect

## Taxonomy

The masters of design: a hypocritical architect role.

## Owner

- Project: [Martin Chuzzlewit](play_cdn_006_martin_chuzzlewit.md)

## Has

An Wiltshire office, rolls of architectural drawings, a collection of moral pamphlets, and a mandate to collect heavy premiums from pupils.

## Orders

Take architectural pupils, pocket their premiums, claim credit for their drawings, lecture them on morals and gratitude, and seek wealthy patrons.

## Loses

The reputation of honest design, the standing of an honorable master, and the respect of his pupils who discover his plagiarism.

## Drives

The accumulation of wealth through architectural premiums and oily sycophancy, converting his pupils' labor into personal credit.
