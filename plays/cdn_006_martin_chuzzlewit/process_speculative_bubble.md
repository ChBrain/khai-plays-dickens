# Process: Speculative Bubble

## Taxonomy

The financial operations of bubble companies: a speculative bubble.

## Owner

- Project: [Martin Chuzzlewit](play_cdn_006_martin_chuzzlewit.md)

## Initiated by

Montague Tigg's establishment of the Anglo-Bengalee Assurance Company, drawing in Seth Pecksniff and Jonas Chuzzlewit to invest.

## Direction

The progressive inflation of company deposits and public reputation, showing how false shares turn greed into financial leverage and entrap investors.

## Lever

The deposits of Pecksniff and Jonas Chuzzlewit, meeting the blackmail demands of Montague Tigg.

## Echo

Montague Tigg is murdered, Jonas commits suicide, and the bubble company collapses in bankruptcy, ruining Pecksniff.
