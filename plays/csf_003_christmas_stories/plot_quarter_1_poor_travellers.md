# Plot: Poor Travellers

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Christmas Stories](play_csf_003_christmas_stories.md)

## Cue

A warm, glowing dining hall of Watts's Charity in Rochester, starting inside [place_watts_charity.md](place_watts_charity.md).

## Action

A traveller in his [Storyteller](position_storyteller.md) position visits [place_watts_charity.md](place_watts_charity.md) on Christmas Eve, hosting a festive dinner of beef and punch for six poor travellers under the [Christmas Stories Pitch](pitch_christmas_stories.md), and listening to a soldier's story of family reconciliation.

## Stage

The poor travellers sit at the dining table eating beef, while the host pours hot punch and the private soldier relates his story of forgiveness.

## Tension

The cold winter night outside is balanced by the warm charity and shared stories inside the old hall.
