## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Christmas Stories](play_csf_003_christmas_stories.md)

## Cue

The parlor dining room in Norfolk Street, London.

## Action

[Mrs Lirriper](persona_lirriper.md) executes her [Orphan Adoption Plan](plan_orphan_adoption.md) in [place_lirriper_lodgings.md](place_lirriper_lodgings.md) to raise the orphaned Jemmy, while her loyal [Major Jackman](persona_jackman.md) in his [Lodger](position_lodger.md) position teaches the boy arithmetic lessons and protects the home from London's cold streets.

## Stage

Mrs. Lirriper carries keys, while Major Jackman sits in the parlor teaching Jemmy and drawing chalk marks on a slate.

## Tension

Mrs. Lirriper defends her lodgings from debt and gossip, while they raise the orphan with fatherly and motherly devotion.
