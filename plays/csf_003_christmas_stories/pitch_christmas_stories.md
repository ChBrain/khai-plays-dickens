# Pitch: Christmas Stories

## Taxonomy

The tuning of tone for the whole play: a production directive.

## Owner

- Owner: [Christmas Stories](play_csf_003_christmas_stories.md)

## Tenor

A warm, festive, and collaborative winter atmosphere: the crackling of wood fires inside snowy inns, the rich steam of hot Christmas punch, the sharp clashing of railway signals at Mugby, the calling of street auctioneers, and the rustling of lodging-house keys.

## Undertow

The low chanting of Christmas carols, the clatter of plates at a charity feast, the quiet scratching of a pen on a handwritten child's book, the hiss of locomotive steam, and the gentle laughter of Mrs. Lirriper's lodgers.

## Nerve

The festive warmth of family reconciliation and the quiet power of personal charity in the face of isolation and poverty.

## Echo

A persistent warning that true comfort and family are not bought with money, but found in the simple acts of adopting the orphan, welcoming the traveller, and listening to the neglected.
