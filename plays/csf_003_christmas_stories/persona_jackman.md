# Persona: Major Jackman

## Taxonomy

The lodging-house residents of London: a loyal lodger persona.

## Owner

## Projection

Major Jemmy Jackman, a polite, retired military gentleman in his [Lodger](position_lodger.md) position, who lives in Mrs. Lirriper's Norfolk Street house.

## Action

Rent parlor rooms, assist Mrs. Lirriper, educate the orphan Jemmy in math, and participate in Norfolk Street events.

## Shadow

His grand, military title and politeness cover a lonely bachelor's heart, adopting Mrs. Lirriper's lodgings and Jemmy as his own family.

## Tell

He bows formally, twirling his grey mustache and quoting mathematical rules to Jemmy.
