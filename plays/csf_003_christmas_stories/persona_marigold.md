# Persona: Doctor Marigold

## Taxonomy

The street auctioneers of England: a cheap jack persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Christmas Stories](play_csf_003_christmas_stories.md)

## Projection

Doctor Marigold, a kind-hearted, loud-voiced street auctioneer (cheap jack) in his [Storyteller](position_storyteller.md) position, whose colorful cart contains household goods.

## Action

Hawk goods to street crowds, stand on his cart footboard, adopt the deaf Sophy, write a book, and teach Sophy to read.

## Shadow

His energetic, joking street auction manner is a cover for his deep grief over the loss of his wife and child, dedicating his life to Sophy.

## Tell

He rings a brass auction bell, shouting: "Here is a pair of boots, worth five shillings, selling for one!"
