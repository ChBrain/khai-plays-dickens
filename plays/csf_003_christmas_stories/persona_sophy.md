# Persona: Sophy

## Taxonomy

The deaf-and-dumb children of England: an adopted child persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Christmas Stories](play_csf_003_christmas_stories.md)

## Projection

Sophy, a beautiful deaf-and-dumb girl adopted by Doctor Marigold, whose bright face reflects a quick intelligence.

## Action

Live in the auction cart, study Doctor Marigold's handwritten book, learn sign language, and return as a mother.

## Shadow

Her silent isolation is broken by Doctor Marigold's patient, fatherly education, unlocking her mind to the world.

## Tell

She looks at Marigold, repeating his hand signs with a quick smile.
