# Plot: Pickwicks Return

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Master Humphrey's Clock](play_csf_004_master_humphreys_clock.md)

## Cue

A lively, larger clubroom decorated with portraits.

## Action

[Pickwick](persona_pickwick.md) joins the clock club in [place_pickwick_clubroom.md](place_pickwick_clubroom.md), wearing his [Pickwicks Spectacles](piece_pickwicks_spectacles.md), while introducing his servant [Sam Weller](persona_sam_weller.md) and Sam's coachman father [Tony Weller](persona_tony_weller.md) who form their own branch club in the kitchen to debate club rules.

## Stage

Mr. Pickwick adjusts his spectacles to read a manuscript, while in the kitchen Sam Weller winks at Tony, and Tony puffs his pipe in debate.

## Tension

The Pickwickian humor and club debates bring a lively warmth, before Master Humphrey's passing stops the clock forever.
