# Position: Clock Keeper

## Taxonomy

The study hosts of London: a clock keeper role.

## Owner

- Project: [Master Humphrey's Clock](play_csf_004_master_humphreys_clock.md)

## Has

A grandfather clock, study keys, manuscript papers, and a mandate to host the night meetings.

## Orders

Wind the grandfather clock, store manuscripts inside the clock case, host the night meetings, and read stories to the club.

## Loses

His solitary routine, when his studies are filled with the laughter and stories of his new club friends.

## Drives

The preservation of stories and the creation of a warm sanctuary for lonely souls.
