# Persona: Humphrey

## Taxonomy

The study hosts of old London: a clock keeper persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Master Humphrey's Clock](play_csf_003_master_humphreys_clock.md)

## Projection

Master Humphrey, a deformed, solitary, kind-hearted old man in his [Clock Keeper](position_clock_keeper.md) position, whose study contains an ancient grandfather clock.

## Action

Wind the grandfather clock, write manuscripts, host the night meetings, read stories to the club, and welcome Mr. Pickwick.

## Shadow

His deformity and solitary life drove him to seek comfort in the study, founding the club to find late-life friendship and share stories.

## Tell

He winds the ancient grandfather clock with a large brass key, smiling at his friends.
