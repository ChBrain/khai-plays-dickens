# Plot: Giant Chronicles

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Master Humphrey's Clock](play_csf_003_master_humphreys_clock.md)

## Cue

A cold winter midnight in London as the church bells chime.

## Action

[Redburn](persona_redburn.md) executes the [Manuscript Reading](process_manuscript_reading.md) process, reading the "Giant Chronicles" manuscripts from the [Clock Case](place_clock_case.md) to [Humphrey](persona_humphrey.md) and the [Deaf Gentleman](persona_deaf_gentleman.md), describing the ancient legends told by the Guildhall giants Gog and Magog.

## Stage

Redburn stands by the grandfather clock reading aloud, while Master Humphrey and the Deaf Gentleman listen intently, and the clock ticks heavily.

## Tension

The ancient, dark legends of London's past contrast with the quiet safety of the modern study.
