# Persona: Deaf Gentleman

## Taxonomy

The silent observers of London: a silent listener persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Master Humphrey's Clock](play_csf_003_master_humphreys_clock.md)

## Projection

The Deaf Gentleman, a quiet, lonely, benevolent elderly man in his [Listener](position_listener.md) position, who joins the clock club.

## Action

Attend the night meetings, listen to stories through sign or expression, and participate silently in study conversations.

## Shadow

His deaf isolation is broken by the warm friendship of Master Humphrey's study, finding a quiet family among the club members.

## Tell

He cup his hand behind his ear, smiling benevolently as he watches the readers.
