# Master Humphrey's Clock: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's shorter work _Master Humphrey's Clock_ (1840). A framing narrative of clock club meetings, stored manuscripts, and the return of Samuel Pickwick and the Wellers.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _Master Humphrey's Clock_, published from 1840 to 1841, one of Shorter Fiction and Sketches in the house catalog (`csf_003`, [MHC]). The text is in the public domain.
