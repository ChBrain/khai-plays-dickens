# Plot: The Deaf Gentleman

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Master Humphrey's Clock](play_csf_003_master_humphreys_clock.md)

## Cue

A quiet, rainy night outside the study window, shifting to the warm hearth inside.

## Action

The lonely [Deaf Gentleman](persona_deaf_gentleman.md) in his [Listener](position_listener.md) position arrives at the study, where [Humphrey](persona_humphrey.md) and [Redburn](persona_redburn.md) welcome him into the clock club, sharing tea and silent friendship.

## Stage

The Deaf Gentleman sits in a high-backed armchair by the fire, cupping his hand to listen, while Jack Redburn pours tea and Master Humphrey nods warmly.

## Tension

The Deaf Gentleman's silent isolation is overcome by the quiet, welcoming friendship of the study.
