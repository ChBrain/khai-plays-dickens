# Position: Listener

## Taxonomy

The silent observers of London: a silent listener role.

## Owner

- Project: [Master Humphrey's Clock](play_csf_003_master_humphreys_clock.md)

## Has

A high-backed chair, a cup of tea, a notebook, and a mandate to keep the club's secrets.

## Orders

Attend night meetings in Master Humphrey's study, listen to manuscripts read aloud, and keep the club's stories secret.

## Loses

His silence, when he is moved to share his own quiet thoughts and observations with the club.

## Drives

The support of the clock keeper and the silent observation of human interest stories.
