## Tenor

A cozy, warm, and nostalgic atmosphere of a quiet London study: the slow, heavy ticking of a grandfather clock, the crackling of wood fire in the hearth, the soft rustling of old yellowed manuscripts, and the scratch of a pen.

## Undertow

The rhythmic chiming of the hours, the clinking of teacups, the low, friendly conversation of a small group of old friends, and the winter wind outside the window.

## Nerve

The quiet warmth of late-life friendship and the preservation of stories in the face of passing time and aging.

## Echo

A persistent reminder that time passes steadily for all, but that stories shared among loyal friends around a warm hearth can preserve the memory of the past.
