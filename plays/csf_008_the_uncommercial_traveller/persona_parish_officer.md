# Persona: Parish Officer

## Taxonomy

The parish officials of England: an official persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Uncommercial Traveller](play_csf_008_the_uncommercial_traveller.md)

## Projection

The Parish Officer, a self-satisfied, respectable parish official who manages the local workhouse and parish charity.

## Action

Inspect parish boundaries, attend vestry meetings, manage workhouse expenses, and defend parish decisions to visitors.

## Shadow

His respectable, self-satisfied manner covers a complete indifference to the real sufferings of the workhouse inmates, prioritizing low costs.

## Tell

He pats his pockets, declaring: "Our parish is managed with the highest efficiency, sir."
