# Place: Nocturnal London

## Taxonomy

The silent city at midnight: a silent nocturnal scene.

## Owner

- Project: [The Uncommercial Traveller](play_csf_008_the_uncommercial_traveller.md)

## Shown

A silent, dark, fog-covered London at midnight, with empty markets, locked buildings, and shadows under bridge arches.

## Holds

Silent bridge arches, empty streets, closed tollhouses, and a few homeless wanderers.

## Offers

A quiet, observational path to the sleepless traveller walking through the night.

## Withheld

Warmth, food, and friendly conversation, which the cold nocturnal streets refuse to give.
