# Persona: Dullborough Friend

## Taxonomy

The childhood friends of provincial England: a childhood friend persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Uncommercial Traveller](play_csf_008_the_uncommercial_traveller.md)

## Projection

The Dullborough Friend, an old acquaintance in Dullborough who remembers the Traveller from their schoolboy days.

## Action

Meet the Traveller in the street, talk of childhood schoolboys, walk to the old churchyard, and point out old houses.

## Shadow

His quiet, nostalgic conversations remind the Traveller of the passage of time and the complete decay of their early childhood memories.

## Tell

He smiles warmly, asking: "Do you remember the old playing field, my boy?"
