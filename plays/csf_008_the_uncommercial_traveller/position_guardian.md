# Position: Guardian

## Taxonomy

The workhouse administrators of London: a workhouse guardian role.

## Owner

- Project: [The Uncommercial Traveller](play_csf_008_the_uncommercial_traveller.md)

## Has

A workhouse ledger, a ring of keys, an iron gate, and a mandate to manage the local parish poor.

## Orders

Maintain the workhouse gates, register incoming paupers, distribute daily bread rations, and enforce strict institutional discipline.

## Loses

Her patience, when the inmates complain of bad food or when inspectors discover dirty conditions.

## Drives

The strict preservation of order and low cost in the workhouse administration.
