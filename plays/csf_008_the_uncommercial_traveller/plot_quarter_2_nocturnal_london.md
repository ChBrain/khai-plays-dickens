# Plot: Nocturnal London

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [The Uncommercial Traveller](play_csf_008_the_uncommercial_traveller.md)

## Cue

A silent, dark bridge arch over the Thames at midnight.

## Action

[Traveller](persona_traveller.md) in his [Traveller Position](position_traveller.md) position executes the [Nocturnal Wandering](process_nocturnal_wandering.md) process through [place_nocturnal_london.md](place_nocturnal_london.md), leaning on his [Walking Stick](piece_walking_stick.md) to watch the silent city, and witnessing the misery of [Homeless Wanderer](persona_homeless_wanderer.md) sheltering under the dark arches.

## Stage

The Traveller walks through empty markets and locked tollhouses, while Homeless Wanderers shiver in the cold fog, and the river bells toll the midnight hour.

## Tension

The Traveller's insomnia drives him to witness the silent, neglected poverty of the night city.
