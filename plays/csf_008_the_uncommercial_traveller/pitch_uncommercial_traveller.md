# Pitch: Uncommercial Traveller

## Taxonomy

The tuning of tone for the whole play: a production directive.

## Owner

- Owner: [The Uncommercial Traveller](play_csf_008_the_uncommercial_traveller.md)

## Tenor

A solemn, sleepless, and observational atmosphere of midnight London: the tolling of river bells in the fog, the cold damp iron gates of workhouses, the scratching of dry straw in pauper beds, and the dusty benches of night stagecoaches.

## Undertow

The slow, steady tapping of a walking stick on dark wet stones, the low muttering of sleepless wanderers under bridge arches, the rustling of parish ledgers, and the distant howling of a wind on the river.

## Nerve

The sympathetic recording of the silent, nocturnal city and the quiet anger at administrative neglect of the poor.

## Echo

A persistent reminder that beneath the busy surface of the commercial empire lies a vast, silent world of human neglect that can only be reached by quiet, personal transit.
