# Position: Traveller

## Taxonomy

The human interest observers of Victorian England: a traveller role.

## Owner

- Project: [The Uncommercial Traveller](play_csf_008_the_uncommercial_traveller.md)

## Has

A walking stick, a leather flask, a notebook, and a mandate to travel for human interest.

## Orders

Travel through cities and provinces, walk the London streets at night, inspect workhouses and schools, and write essays of social observation.

## Loses

His sleep, when his night wanderings expose him to the silent misery of the homeless under the bridge arches.

## Drives

The recording of the true conditions of the poor and the quiet exposure of administrative neglect.
