# Plot: Provincial Reflections

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [The Uncommercial Traveller](play_csf_008_the_uncommercial_traveller.md)

## Cue

An old stone churchyard in the quiet town of Dullborough.

## Action

[Traveller](persona_traveller.md) arrives in [place_dullborough_town.md](place_dullborough_town.md) and meets his old [Dullborough Friend](persona_dullborough_friend.md), who leads him to the old playing fields and churchyard tombs, while they reflect on schoolboy days.

## Stage

The Traveller walks through the changed, narrow streets of Dullborough, while the Dullborough Friend points out childhood houses, and they stand among the silent churchyard tombs.

## Tension

The nostalgic memories of childhood are balanced against the real, cold decay of the old town.
