# Process: Nocturnal Wandering

## Taxonomy

The sleepless walks through London streets: a nocturnal wandering process.

## Owner

- Project: [The Uncommercial Traveller](play_csf_008_the_uncommercial_traveller.md)

## Initiated by

The Traveller's sleeplessness, forcing him to walk the dark London streets at night.

## Direction

The progressive wandering through markets, bridge arches, and tollhouses, showing how the silent city is observed.

## Lever

The Traveller's walking stick and travel flask, meeting the homeless under bridge arches.

## Echo

The Traveller publishes sketches of these night walks, recording the silent misery and beauty of London after dark.
