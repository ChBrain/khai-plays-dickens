# The Uncommercial Traveller: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's shorter work _The Uncommercial Traveller_ (1860). A series of nocturnal walks, institutional inspections, and provincial memories across Victorian England.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _The Uncommercial Traveller_, published from 1860 to 1869, one of Shorter Fiction and Sketches in the house catalog (`csf_008`, [UT]). The text is in the public domain.
