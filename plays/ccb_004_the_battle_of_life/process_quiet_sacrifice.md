# Process: Quiet Sacrifice

## Taxonomy

The moral mechanisms of love: a domestic sacrifice process.

## Owner

- Project: [The Battle of Life](play_ccb_004_the_battle_of_life.md)

## Initiated by

Marion's discovery that her sister Grace deeply loves Alfred Heathfield, whom Marion is also in love with.

## Direction

The progressive exile of Marion, who disappears and lives in secret for six years to allow Grace and Alfred to marry and find happiness.

## Lever

Marion's secret residence with Aunt Martha and her eventually planned return when the marriage is secure.

## Echo

Grace and Alfred are married, and Marion's return resolves the misunderstanding with tears of joy.
