# Persona: Dr. Jeddler

## Taxonomy

The country doctors of England: a philosopher persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Battle of Life](play_ccb_004_the_battle_of_life.md)

## Projection

A kind, hearty man in his [Philosopher](position_philosopher.md) position, who views life as a ridiculous joke or farce, built on an ancient battlefield.

## Action

Lecture his daughters on the absurdity of human struggle, joke about serious things, and later fall into deep sorrow when Marion vanishes.

## Shadow

His cynical philosophy is a shield against the real tragedy of life, which breaks when he is forced to confront the actual loss of his daughter.

## Tell

He laughs heartily and taps his walking-stick on the ground when talking of life's absurdities.
