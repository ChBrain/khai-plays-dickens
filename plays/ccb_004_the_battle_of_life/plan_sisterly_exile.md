# Plan: Sisterly Exile Plan

## Taxonomy

The execution of self-sacrifice: a plan of sisterly exile.

## Owner

- Owner: [Marion](persona_marion.md)

## Direction

To disappear from the battlefield house on the night of Alfred's return, leaving a letter for Grace, and to live in secret with Aunt Martha so that Grace and Alfred can marry.

## Orders

Marion is ordered to write the letter to Grace, slip away during the dance, travel in secret to Aunt Martha's cottage, and remain hidden for six years.

## Implementation

Aunt Martha's cottage, utilizing the silence of the countryside to maintain her exile.

## Targets

- [ ] Marion disappears during the dance without raising immediate alarm
- [ ] Grace receives the letter explaining her flight
- [ ] Marion remains in secret exile until Grace and Alfred's marriage is secure
