# Persona: Marion

## Taxonomy

The daughters of philosophers: a self-sacrificing persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Battle of Life](play_ccb_004_the_battle_of_life.md)

## Projection

A beautiful, bright young woman, full of deep affection, who loves her sister Grace and her suitor Alfred.

## Action

Sacrifice her own love for Alfred, disappear on the night of his return, and live in secret exile to allow Grace to marry him.

## Shadow

Her flight is misinterpreted as a scandalous elopement with Warden, exposing her family to shame and Alfred to grief.

## Tell

She clings to her sister and looks at Alfred with silent, tearful eyes before he departs.
