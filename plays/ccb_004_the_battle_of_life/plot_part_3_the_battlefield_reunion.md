# Plot: The Battlefield Reunion

## Taxonomy

The fytte of three parts: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [The Battle of Life](play_ccb_004_the_battle_of_life.md)

## Cue

A quiet summer evening six years later, moving from [Clemency's Inn](place_clemency_inn.md) to the orchard.

## Action

[Marion](persona_marion.md) returns from her secret residence at [Aunt's Cottage](place_aunt_cottage.md), resolving the [Quiet Sacrifice](process_quiet_sacrifice.md) process and explaining her exile to [Grace](persona_grace.md), [Alfred](persona_alfred.md), and [Dr. Jeddler](persona_dr_jeddler.md) when [Michael Warden](persona_michael_warden.md) returns.

## Stage

[Clemency](persona_clemency.md) welcomes Warden at the inn, before they all gather in the battlefield orchard where Marion is reunited with her sister.

## Tension

The family must learn the truth of Marion's sacrifice and forgive the years of absence, resolving the scandal and bringing peace to the old battlefield.
