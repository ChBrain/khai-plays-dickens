# Position: Philosopher

## Taxonomy

The thinkers of the countryside: a philosophical role.

## Owner

- Project: [The Battle of Life](play_ccb_004_the_battle_of_life.md)

## Has

A library of classical books, a garden built on a battlefield, and the mandate to lecture his daughters on the farce of human existence.

## Orders

Treat all human struggles as a grand joke, ignore serious emotions as absurdities, and maintain a cheerful indifference to tragedy.

## Loses

The capacity to see the genuine depth of his daughters' sacrifices, blinded by his own intellectual cynicism.

## Drives

The constant trivialization of human emotion, treating the battle of life as a game of puppets.
