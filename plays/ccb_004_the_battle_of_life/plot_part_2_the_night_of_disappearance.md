# Plot: The Night of Disappearance

## Taxonomy

The fytte of three parts: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [The Battle of Life](play_ccb_004_the_battle_of_life.md)

## Cue

A cold winter night three years later, during a dance at [Battlefield House](place_battlefield_house.md).

## Action

[Marion](persona_marion.md) executes the [Sisterly Exile Plan](plan_sisterly_exile.md), disappearing into the night to allow [Grace](persona_grace.md) and [Alfred](persona_alfred.md) to marry, while [Michael Warden](persona_michael_warden.md) in his [Warden Suitor](position_warden_suitor.md) position flees the country.

## Stage

[Clemency](persona_clemency.md) finds [Marion's Letter](piece_letter.md) on the table after Marion has slipped away, while Grace and Alfred stand in their grief before [Dr. Jeddler](persona_dr_jeddler.md).

## Tension

Marion's flight is believed to be an elopement with Warden, leaving Alfred and Grace in deep sorrow, while the quiet sacrifice of her exile remains hidden.
