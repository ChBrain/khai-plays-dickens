# Persona: Clemency

## Taxonomy

The servants of the household: a faithful domestic persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Battle of Life](play_ccb_004_the_battle_of_life.md)

## Projection

A simple, faithful, good-natured servant with a red face and clumsy hands, who carries her pocket [Nutmeg-Grater](piece_nutmeg_grater.md) as her moral guide.

## Action

Do all the heavy work, keep the sisters' secrets, marry Britain, and run the Nutmeg-Grater Inn.

## Shadow

Her simple nature makes her easily confused by the doctor's cynical jokes, but her moral rules remain unshakeable.

## Tell

She rubs her elbows and checks her grater when trying to solve a moral problem.
