# Persona: George Mother

## Taxonomy

The neglected mothers of Preston cellars: an abusive mother persona.

## Owner

- Project: [George Silverman's Explanation](play_csf_006_george_silvermans_explanation.md)

## Projection

George's mother, a poor, abusive, neglected woman who lives in a damp Preston cellar, suffering from urban fever.

Her abusive, angry treatment of George is a product of her extreme poverty and despair, leaving him with permanent emotional scars.
