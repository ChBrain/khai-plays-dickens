# Plot: Selfless Sacrifice

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

## Cue

A chapel vestry room under cold, gloomy rain, starting inside [place_chapel_vestry.md](place_chapel_vestry.md).

## Action

[George Silverman](persona_george.md) executes the [Secret Marriage Plan](plan_secret_marriage.md) to secure the [Marriage License](piece_marriage_license.md) and marry [Adelina](persona_adelina.md) to [Mr Wharton](persona_wharton.md), triggering the [Misunderstood Charity](process_misunderstood_charity.md) process where [Brother Hawkyard](persona_hawkyard.md) accuses him of self-interest and George accepts the public disgrace.

## Stage

George stands in the chapel vestry holding the marriage license, while Brother Hawkyard loudly accuses him and George bows his head in silent martyrdom.

## Tension

George's selfless arrange of Adelina's marriage is branded as bribery, accepting the public blame in silent sacrifice.
