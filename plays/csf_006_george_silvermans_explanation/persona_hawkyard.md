# Persona: Brother Hawkyard

## Taxonomy

The hypocritical preachers of old England: a self-promoting guardian persona.

## Owner

- Project: [George Silverman's Explanation](play_csf_006_george_silvermans_explanation.md)

## Projection

Brother Hawkyard, a loud, boasting, self-righteous nonconformist preacher in Preston, who acts as George's guardian.

## Action

Adopt George, demand expense accounts, boast of his charity to chapel crowds, and accuse George of bribery.

## Shadow

His boasting, self-righteous preaching and audits of the [Expense Ledger](piece_expense_ledger.md) cover his financial greed and hypocritical love of public praise.

## Tell

He bangs his ledger, shouting: "I have spent fifty pounds on this orphan, chapel friends, out of pure Christian love!"
