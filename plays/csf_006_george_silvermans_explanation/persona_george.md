# Persona: George

## Taxonomy

The misunderstood clergymen of England: a quiet martyr persona.

## Owner

- Project: [George Silverman's Explanation](play_csf_006_george_silvermans_explanation.md)

## Projection

George Silverman, a quiet, melancholy, highly educated clergyman in his [Clergyman](position_clergyman.md) position, who grew up as a [Charity Ward](position_charity_ward.md), whose study contains Cambridge books.

## Action

Recover in the country, study at Cambridge, tutor Adelina, execute the [Secret Marriage Plan](plan_secret_marriage.md), and write his explanation.

## Shadow

His childhood neglect in the Preston cellar left him with a deep, permanent feeling of worthlessness, accepting public disgrace in silent martyrdom.

## Tell

He sighs, writing in his notebook: "I am George Silverman, and this is my explanation."
