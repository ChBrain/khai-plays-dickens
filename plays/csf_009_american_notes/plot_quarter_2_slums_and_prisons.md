# Plot: Slums and Prisons

## Taxonomy

The fytte of urban audits: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [American Notes](play_csf_009_american_notes.md)

## Cue

The noisy streets of New York, where pigs wander through the filth.

## Action

[Charles Dickens](persona_dickens.md) explores the tenements of [Five Points](place_five_points.md) and conducts an [Institutional Audit](process_institutional_audit.md) in the radial corridors of [Eastern Penitentiary](place_eastern_penitentiary.md) while examining the prison cells.

## Stage

[Charles Dickens](persona_dickens.md) observes the turnkey using the [Solitary Key](piece_solitary_key.md) to lock up prisoners, and writes about the education of [Laura Bridgman](persona_laura_bridgman.md).

## Tension

The shock of urban squalor and the extreme mental depression of prisoners under solitary confinement raise serious doubts in the mind of the visitor regarding American social progress.
