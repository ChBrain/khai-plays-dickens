# Plot: Slave Soil and Canada

## Taxonomy

The fytte of southern shadows: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [American Notes](play_csf_009_american_notes.md)

## Cue

The crossing of the Potomac River, entering the territory of Virginia.

## Action

[Charles Dickens](persona_dickens.md) witnesses the harsh realities of slave soil before traveling west and eventually north into Canada, fulfilling the final stages of the [Steamship Crossing Plan](plan_atlantic_steamship_crossing.md).

## Stage

[Charles Dickens](persona_dickens.md) acts in the [Critic](position_critic.md) position to record his protest against slavery, while Canadian educators acting in the [Reformer](position_reformer.md) position show him their local schools.

## Tension

The moral horror of slavery is balanced by the sublime beauty of Niagara Falls and the calm order of Canadian institutions before his departure for England.
