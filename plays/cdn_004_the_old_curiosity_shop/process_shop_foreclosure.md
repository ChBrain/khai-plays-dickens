# Process: Shop Foreclosure

## Taxonomy

The moral mechanisms of debt: a shop foreclosure.

## Owner

- Project: [The Old Curiosity Shop](play_cdn_004_the_old_curiosity_shop.md)

## Initiated by

Grandfather's gambling losses, leading him to borrow heavily from Daniel Quilp, who discovers the default and forecloses on the shop.

## Direction

The progressive seizure of the shop inventory and the eviction of the owners, showing how usury debts dismantle a home and initiate a flight.

## Lever

Quilp's legal writ and his physical occupation of the rooms, meeting Nell's resolve to lead her grandfather away.

## Echo

The shop is stripped, the owners flee into the countryside, and Quilp launches a pursuit to recover his debts.
