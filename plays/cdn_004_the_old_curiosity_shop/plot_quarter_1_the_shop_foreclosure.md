## Action

[Grandfather](persona_grandfather.md) in his [Shopkeeper](position_shopkeeper.md) position loses his fortune on cards, leading [Quilp](persona_quilp.md) in his [Moneylender](position_moneylender.md) position to foreclose on the shop, forcing [Nell](persona_nell.md) to initiate the [Country Flight Plan](plan_country_flight.md) under the key of the [Rural Pilgrimage Pitch](pitch_rural_pilgrimage.md).

## Stage

Grandfather sits at his desk holding the [Deck of Cards](piece_deck_cards.md), while Quilp occupies the shop rooms, sleeping on the table and smoking.

## Tension

Nell must lead her grandfather out of the shop in the midnight hours to escape the dwarf's harassment, initiating their long wandering.
