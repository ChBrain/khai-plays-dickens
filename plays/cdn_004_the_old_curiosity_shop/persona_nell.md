# Persona: Nell

## Taxonomy

The pure children of England: a child protagonist persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Old Curiosity Shop](play_cdn_004_the_old_curiosity_shop.md)

## Projection

A young girl of sweet, innocent face, who lives in a dusty shop surrounded by grotesque armor, but whose spirit remains clean and untarnished.

## Action

Lead her grandfather out of the shop, walk the country roads, guide Mrs. Jarley's waxworks, and care for the ancient village church.

## Shadow

Her fragile physical strength is audited by the long, cold wandering, leading to her peaceful death before her friends can rescue her.

## Tell

She speaks with a gentle, comforting voice and holds her grandfather's hand as they walk.
