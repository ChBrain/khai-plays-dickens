# Position: Moneylender

## Taxonomy

The usurers of the wharf: a moneylender role.

## Owner

- Project: [The Old Curiosity Shop](play_cdn_004_the_old_curiosity_shop.md)

## Has

A dark office by the Thames wharf, safes of bills, a network of spies, and a mandate to collect debts and foreclose on shops.

## Orders

Lend money to desperate gamblers, foreclose on defaulting shops, search for runaway debtors, and spy on the debtors' associates.

## Loses

The standing of a respectable citizen, the capacity for domestic peace, and the safety of a quiet, dry home.

## Drives

The aggressive pursuit and harassment of debtors, converting usury debts into complete control over their lives.
