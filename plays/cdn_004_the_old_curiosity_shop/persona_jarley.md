# Persona: Mrs. Jarley

## Taxonomy

The exhibition managers of England: a showwoman persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Old Curiosity Shop](play_cdn_004_the_old_curiosity_shop.md)

## Projection

Mrs. Jarley, a comfortable, stout woman in a large bonnet, who travels in a red caravan and manages the waxwork exhibition.

## Action

Employ Nell to guide visitors, feed the runaways, exhibit historical figures at fairs, and travel the countryside.

## Shadow

Her business is built on showmanship and wax novelties, but she possesses a warm, motherly heart that offers genuine protection to Nell.

## Tell

She drinks tea in her caravan and declares: "Jarley's is the only genuine waxwork exhibition."
