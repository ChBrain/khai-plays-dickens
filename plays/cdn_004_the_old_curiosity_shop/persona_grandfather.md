# Persona: Grandfather

## Taxonomy

The gamblers of London: a self-destructive grandfather persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Old Curiosity Shop](play_cdn_004_the_old_curiosity_shop.md)

## Projection

An old man with long grey hair and a wild, anxious face, who in his [Shopkeeper](position_shopkeeper.md) position is obsessed with securing a fortune.

## Action

Keep the curiosity shop, gamble at card games late at night, borrow from Quilp, and wander with Nell in the countryside.

## Shadow

His obsession with gambling is driven by his love for Nell, but actually leads to his ruin and Nell's physical destruction.

## Tell

He mutters about card combinations and clutches Nell's arm in fear when the usurer's name is mentioned.
