# Persona: Dick Swiveller

## Taxonomy

The eccentric clerks of London: a theatrical clerk persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Old Curiosity Shop](play_cdn_004_the_old_curiosity_shop.md)

## Projection

Richard Swiveller, a light-hearted, theatrical young man in a dirty collar and a smart coat, who quotes poetry constantly.

## Action

Work as clerk to Sampson Brass under Quilp's orders, play cribbage with the Marchioness, and later assist in exposing Brass's plots.

## Shadow

He is initially used by Quilp to spy on the family, but his good heart and theatrical eccentricity lead him to rebel and save the innocent.

## Tell

He quotes Tom Moore's poetry and drinks beer from a pewter mug with a theatrical flourish.
