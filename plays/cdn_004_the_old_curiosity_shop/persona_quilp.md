# Persona: Quilp

## Taxonomy

The moneylenders of the wharf: a grotesque dwarf persona.

Daniel Quilp, a grotesque, broad-shouldered dwarf in his [Moneylender](position_moneylender.md) position, with a yellow, fanged face and a constant, malicious grin.

Foreclose on the curiosity shop, drink boiling spirits, hunt the runaways through the country, and drown in the foggy Thames.
