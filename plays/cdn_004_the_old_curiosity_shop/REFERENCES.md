# The Old Curiosity Shop: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's novel _The Old Curiosity Shop_ (1840). Pilgrimage of Nell and Grandfather, staging curiosity trade, usurious foreclosure, and country flight.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _The Old Curiosity Shop_, published in weekly parts from 1840 to 1841, one of The Novels in the house catalog (`cdn_004`, [OCS]). The text is in the public domain.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **The catalog is the index**: this warrant answers for one staged work; the full Dickens catalogue and its `cdn_004` numbering live in the house register, not here.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
