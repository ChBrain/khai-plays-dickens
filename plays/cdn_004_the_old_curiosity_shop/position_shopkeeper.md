# Position: Shopkeeper

## Taxonomy

The merchants of curiosity: a shopkeeper role.

## Owner

- Project: [The Old Curiosity Shop](play_cdn_004_the_old_curiosity_shop.md)

## Has

An old curiosity shop, shelves of antique armor and tapestries, account books of loans, and a mandate to manage the curiosity inventory.

## Orders

Sell antique curiosity goods, borrow money from usurers to fund card gambling, keep secrets from family members, and lose the shop to creditors.

## Loses

The ownership of the shop, the stability of a London home, and the safety of a comfortable shelter for his child.

## Drives

The desperate attempt to secure a fortune for his grandchild, translating the shopkeeper role into secret gambling debts.
