# Pitch: The Mudfog Papers

## Taxonomy

The tuning of tone for the whole play: a production directive.

## Owner

- Owner: [The Mudfog Papers](play_csf_007_the_mudfog_papers.md)

## Tenor

A damp, maritime, and pompously ridiculous provincial town where the scent of coal and river mud mixes with the dry paper rustle of scientific pamphlets and the heavy brass clank of useless mayoral regalia.

## Undertow

The monotonous ticking of grandfather clocks in the municipal offices, the splash of river water against the docks, the pompous clearing of throats, and the chaotic hum of a crowd waiting for a public display to collapse.

## Nerve

The desperate social ambition of a newly rich coal-dealer turned mayor and the intellectual vanity of professors who present pocket-picking machines as public benefits.

## Echo

A persistent reminder that municipal vanity and academic obsession are but different forms of the same pompous folly, and that the simple business of the town will outlast the grandest scientific theories.
