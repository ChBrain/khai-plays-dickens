# Persona: Nicholas Tulrumble

## Taxonomy

The coal-dealers of Mudfog: a newly rich mayor persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Mudfog Papers](play_csf_007_the_mudfog_papers.md)

## Projection

Mr. Nicholas Tulrumble, a wealthy, self-important retired coal-dealer in his [Mayor](position_mayor.md) position, whose round, red face and pompous walk reveal a man desperate to be treated as a lord.

## Action

Erect a grandiose pageant, parade through the streets in heavy brass armor, and attempt to write his name into the town history.

## Shadow

He cannot see that his sudden rise from coal-dealing to the mayoralty makes him a subject of local mockery, rather than respect, and that his heavy armor is a physical trap.

## Tell

He constantly clears his throat with a loud, trumpeting sound, adjusting his mayoral chain to draw eyes.
