# Plot: The Association Departs

## Taxonomy

The fytte of final dispersion: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [The Mudfog Papers](play_csf_007_the_mudfog_papers.md)

## Cue

A stormy evening outside [Association Hall](place_association_hall.md), where the wind is howling and the professors prepare for their journey back to London.

## Action

The small exhibition steam boiler explodes, initiating the [Scientific Absurdity](process_scientific_absurdity.md) dispersion, while [Nicholas Tulrumble](persona_tulrumble.md) abandons his pageant ideas and the professors flee the hall.

## Stage

[Nicholas Tulrumble](persona_tulrumble.md) stands on the muddy street with his [Mayoral Mace](piece_mayoral_mace.md), while [Professor Snore](persona_snore.md) and the other academics run for the carriages amidst the steam and smoke.

## Tension

[Professor Snore](persona_snore.md) tries to collect his remaining scientific papers, while the escaping steam and the driving rain force him to abandon his vanity and escape the town.
