# Place: Mudfog Town

## Taxonomy

The damp river towns of Kent: a provincial river town.

## Owner

- Project: [The Mudfog Papers](play_csf_007_the_mudfog_papers.md)

## Shown

A low-lying provincial town situated by a muddy river, with wet cobblestone streets, brick houses, and a constant, damp fog hanging over the docks.

## Holds

A town hall, municipal offices, Nicholas Tulrumble's mansion, a coal-yard, and a public square.

## Offers

A maritime refuge of trade and local politics, smelling of tar, rope, and river mud.

## Withheld

Dry, high-elevation weather, which the low-lying river valley refuses to allow.
