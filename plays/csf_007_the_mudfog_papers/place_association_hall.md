# Place: Association Hall

## Taxonomy

The scientific meeting rooms of England: an exhibition hall.

## Owner

- Project: [The Mudfog Papers](play_csf_007_the_mudfog_papers.md)

## Shown

A large, drafty hall temporarily converted for the Mudfog Association, with wooden benches, a speaker's lectern, and display tables for scientific models.

## Holds

A speaker's table, display cases, specimen jars, blackboards of formulas, and a small exhibition steam boiler.

## Offers

A crowded theater for intellectual performance, where professors exhibit their absurd theories.

## Withheld

Practical, common-sense arguments, which the academic rules of the association refuse to admit.
