# Plot: Scientific Exhibitions

## Taxonomy

The fytte of mechanical wonders: a narrative plot.

## Owner

## Cue

The main exhibition room in [Association Hall](place_association_hall.md), filled with strange models, scientific instruments, and local spectators.

## Action

[Professor Snore](persona_snore.md) demonstrates the utility of the [Pocket-Picking Machine](piece_pocket_picking_machine.md) to show how science can prevent crime, while the machine unexpectedly steals his own watch during the display.

## Stage

[Professor Snore](persona_snore.md) stands beside the model on the exhibition table, while [Nicholas Tulrumble](persona_tulrumble.md) inspects the cogs with municipal interest.

## Tension

[Professor Snore](persona_snore.md) claims the machine offers perfect security, while the mechanical pincers snap shut on his waistcoat pocket, extracting his gold watch in front of the laughing crowd.
