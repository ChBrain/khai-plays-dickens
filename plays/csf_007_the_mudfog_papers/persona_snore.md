# Persona: Professor Snore

## Taxonomy

The scientists of the Mudfog Association: a pompous academic persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Mudfog Papers](play_csf_007_the_mudfog_papers.md)

## Projection

Professor Snore, a thin, spectacled philosopher in his [Academic](position_academic.md) position, whose absent-minded gaze and slow, monotone voice are intended to convey deep scientific thought.

## Action

Deliver tedious opening addresses, inspect local institutions, and demonstrate absurd inventions like the pocket-picking engine.

## Shadow

His absolute faith in abstract theory makes him blind to the real-world uselessness and danger of the inventions he endorses.

## Tell

He periodically pushes his spectacles up his nose and peer closely at the most common, insignificant objects.
