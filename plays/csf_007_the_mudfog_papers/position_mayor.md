# Position: Mayor

## Taxonomy

The municipal officers of Mudfog: a provincial mayor role.

## Owner

- Project: [The Mudfog Papers](play_csf_007_the_mudfog_papers.md)

## Has

A mayoral mace, a gold-laced gown, a suit of heavy brass armor, and a mandate to preserve municipal order.

## Orders

Declare public holidays, preside over the town council meetings, organize civic processions, and suppress local disturbances.

## Loses

His public dignity when his heavy brass armor gets stuck, his reputation when the pageant collapses, and his mayoral office when his term expires.

## Drives

The search for civic distinction and local immortality, trying to force his name onto the town records through sheer display.
