# Plan: Grandiose Pageant

## Taxonomy

The flight from provincial obscurity: a plan of municipal pageant.

## Owner

- Owner: [Nicholas Tulrumble](persona_tulrumble.md)

## Direction

To establish Tulrumble's name in Mudfog history by staging a grand, ancient-style public pageant through the streets, utilizing hired actors and historical armor.

## Orders

Tulrumble is ordered to purchase a suits of brass armor, hire local workers to parade as ancient guards, carry the mayoral mace, and march to the town hall.

## Implementation

The streets of Mudfog and the public square, utilizing the town council.

## Targets

- [ ] Tulrumble purchases a suit of heavy brass armor for the show
- [ ] Hired guards are dressed in historic uniforms to escort the mayor
- [ ] The procession marches to the town hall to show off his new status
