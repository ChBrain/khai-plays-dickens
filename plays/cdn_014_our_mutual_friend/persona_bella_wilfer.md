# Persona: Bella Wilfer

## Taxonomy

The money-minded heroines of London: a changing heroine persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Our Mutual Friend](play_cdn_014_our_mutual_friend.md)

## Projection

Bella Wilfer, a beautiful, high-spirited young woman, whose charming demeanor hides a frank, declared ambition to marry for money.

## Action

Be betrothed to John Harmon, live with the Boffins, reject Rokesmith's poor suit, witness Boffin's miserly change, marry Rokesmith, and become a mother.

## Shadow

Her mercenary declarations are a defense against poverty, but her real affection is awakened by Rokesmith's devotion and Boffin's miserly change.

## Tell

She tosses her curls, declaring: "I am a mercenary girl, and I must marry for money!"
