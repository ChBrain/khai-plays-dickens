# Position: Dressmaker

## Taxonomy

The dolls' dressmakers of London: a dolls' dressmaker role.

## Owner

- Project: [Our Mutual Friend](play_cdn_014_our_mutual_friend.md)

## Has

Fabric shears, needles, thread, scrap fabric, wooden dolls, and a mandate to sew outfits.

## Orders

Cut fabric to size, dress wooden dolls for rich clients, talk of 'fancy' and 'children' in the skies, and offer domestic refuge to girls in distress.

## Loses

The support of a reliable parent (her drunken father), but never her sharp tongue or her imaginative fancy.

## Drives

The support of her household through her independent, creative work, using fancy to escape her crippled reality.
