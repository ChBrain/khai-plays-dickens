# Persona: Jenny Wren

## Taxonomy

The dolls' dressmakers of London: a dolls' dressmaker persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Our Mutual Friend](play_cdn_014_our_mutual_friend.md)

## Projection

Jenny Wren (Fanny Cleaver), a sharp, crippled young girl in her [Dressmaker](position_dressmaker.md) position, with beautiful golden hair and a crutch.

## Action

Sew doll clothes, manage her drunken father (her 'bad child'), shelter Lizzie Hexam, assist Eugene Wrayburn, and talk of the sky.

## Shadow

Her sharp, cynical tongue is a defense against the pain of her crippled legs and her neglectful father, but she creates fancy's beauty.

## Tell

She taps her crutch on the floor, calling: "My bad child has come home drunk again!"
