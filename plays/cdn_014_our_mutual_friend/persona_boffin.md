# Persona: Boffin

## Taxonomy

The Golden Dustmen of London: a generous heir persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Our Mutual Friend](play_cdn_014_our_mutual_friend.md)

## Projection

Noddy Boffin, a stout, good-natured man with a round, honest face, who inherits the dustman's fortune and is known as the Golden Dustman.

## Action

Inherit the dust-heaps, adopt Bella Wilfer, hire Rokesmith, execute the miser test plan, defeat Silas Wegg, and hand over the estate.

## Shadow

His apparent change into a cruel, greedy miser is a public act to test Bella's heart, though it exposes him to Wegg's blackmail.

## Tell

He beams warmly, adjusts his spectacles, and says: "We are simple people, my dear."
