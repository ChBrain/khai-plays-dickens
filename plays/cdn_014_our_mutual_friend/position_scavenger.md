# Position: Scavenger

## Taxonomy

The river scavengers of the Thames: a river scavenger role.

## Owner

- Project: [Our Mutual Friend](play_cdn_014_our_mutual_friend.md)

## Has

A rowboat, river oars, a pocket flashlight, a hook, and a mandate to search the waters.

## Orders

Row boats along the riverbanks, search the waters for bodies, fish out drowned individuals, and search their pockets for coins.

## Loses

The respect of respectable society, and his life when his boat capsizes in the dark locks of the river.

## Drives

The hard, daily survival on the scavenger economy of the river, turning death into bread for his family.
