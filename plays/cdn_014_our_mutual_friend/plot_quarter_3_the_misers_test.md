[Boffin](persona_boffin.md) executes the [Miser Test Plan](plan_miser_test.md) to test [Bella Wilfer](persona_bella_wilfer.md)'s heart, while Silas Wegg finds the hidden [Harmon Will](piece_harmon_will.md) in the [Dust Heaps](place_dust_heaps.md) through the [Dust Scavenging](process_dust_scavenging.md) process, and [Bradley Headstone](persona_bradley_headstone.md)'s jealousy grows.
