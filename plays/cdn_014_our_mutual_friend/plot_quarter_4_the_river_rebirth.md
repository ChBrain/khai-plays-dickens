A quiet riverbank in the country, shifting to the Boffin mansion in London.

## Action

[Bradley Headstone](persona_bradley_headstone.md) attacks Wrayburn on the [River Thames](place_river_thames.md), but [Lizzie Hexam](persona_lizzie_hexam.md) rescues him, while [Bella Wilfer](persona_bella_wilfer.md) marries [John Harmon](persona_john_harmon.md) in his Rokesmith identity, and [Boffin](persona_boffin.md) exposes Wegg's defeat.

## Stage

Lizzie rows Wrayburn to shore, while Bella and John celebrate their marriage and Wegg is thrown into a scavenger cart.

The threats are resolved: the greed of Wegg is exposed, Headstone is dead, and John and Bella inherit the fortune in mutual love.
