# Our Mutual Friend: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's novel _Our Mutual Friend_ (1864). A drama of wealth and rebirth, staging dust-heaps, a river scavenger economy, and Jenny Wren's dolls' workshop.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _Our Mutual Friend_, published in monthly parts from 1864 to 1865, one of The Novels in the house catalog (`cdn_014`, [OMF]). The text is in the public domain.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **The catalog is the index**: this warrant answers for one staged work; the full Dickens catalogue and its `cdn_014` numbering live in the house register, not here.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
