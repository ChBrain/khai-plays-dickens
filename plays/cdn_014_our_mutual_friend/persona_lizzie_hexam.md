# Persona: Lizzie Hexam

## Taxonomy

The river scavengers' daughters: a pure-hearted survivor persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Our Mutual Friend](play_cdn_014_our_mutual_friend.md)

## Projection

Lizzie Hexam, a beautiful, dark-haired young woman in her [Scavenger](position_scavenger.md) position, whose face reflects a deep, quiet strength and a hatred of the scavenger river life.

## Action

Row her father's scavenger boat, flee to London, find refuge with Jenny Wren, be pursued by Headstone, rescue Eugene, and marry him.

## Shadow

Her devotion to her brother Charley leads her to neglect herself, but her courage rescues Eugene from death on the river.

## Tell

She looks at the fire in the grate, seeing pictures of her brother's future in the glowing coals.
