# Position: Valet

## Taxonomy

The servants of the gentry: a valet role.

## Owner

- Project: [The Pickwick Papers](play_cdn_001_the_pickwick_papers.md)

## Has

A clothes brush, boot blacking, a livery waistcoat, and a collection of sharp, street-wise witticisms.

## Orders

Brush the master's clothes, shine the boots, manage the luggage at country inns, and protect the master from con-men and predatory lawyers.

## Loses

The right to live without physical labor, the freedom to choose his own travels, and the independence of a free London street worker.

## Drives

The loyal assistance and protection of a naive master, translating street-wise observation into protective service.
