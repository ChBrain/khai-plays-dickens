# Pitch: Benevolent Tour

## Taxonomy

The tuning of tone for the whole play: a production directive.

## Owner

- Owner: [The Pickwick Papers](play_cdn_001_the_pickwick_papers.md)

## Tenor

A Victorian traveler's register, full of comedy, country inns, coaching-horns, and simple, naive benevolence, with a gentle, humored observation of English life.

## Undertow

The sharp, metallic clatter of the courtroom and the heavy, crowded doors of the Fleet Prison, waiting to audit the travelers' innocence.

## Nerve

The reader's affection for Pickwick's absolute innocence, set against the calculating, predatory maneuvers of Dodson and Fogg.

## Echo

A persistent reassurance that simple integrity and loyal companionship are the true safeguards against legal corruption.
