# Plot: The Dissolution

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [The Pickwick Papers](play_cdn_001_the_pickwick_papers.md)

## Cue

A cold morning inside [Fleet Prison](place_fleet_prison.md), shifting to Pickwick's house in Dulwich.

## Action

[Mrs. Bardell](persona_mrs_bardell.md) is imprisoned for costs, leading [Pickwick](persona_pickwick.md) to resolve the [Passive Resistance Plan](plan_passive_resistance.md) by paying [Dodson](persona_dodson.md) under protest, releasing them both, and dissolving the club.

## Stage

[Sam Weller](persona_sam_weller.md) attends his master in the Fleet, before they return to the countryside and Pickwick retires to his house.

## Tension

Pickwick must compromise his principle to save Mrs. Bardell and help his friends marry, resolving the [Legal Audit](process_legal_audit.md) and returning to a quiet, benevolent life.
