# Persona: Sam Weller

## Taxonomy

The servants of the gentry: a street-wise valet persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Pickwick Papers](play_cdn_001_the_pickwick_papers.md)

## Projection

A sharp, cheerful, street-wise young man in his [Valet](position_valet.md) position, with a cockney accent and a constant flow of humorous comparisons.

## Action

Shine boots, brush clothes, serve Pickwick with absolute loyalty, and get himself arrested for debt to remain with his master in prison.

## Shadow

His street-wise nature is the necessary balance to Pickwick's naive innocence, protecting his master from legal and social traps.

## Tell

He winks cheerfully and delivers a sharp comparison starting with: "As the man said..."
