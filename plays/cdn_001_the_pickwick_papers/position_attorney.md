# Position: Attorney

## Taxonomy

The offices of the law: a predatory attorney role.

## Owner

- Project: [The Pickwick Papers](play_cdn_001_the_pickwick_papers.md)

## Has

A legal office, a desk of clerks, parchment writs, and a mandate to seek out breach-of-promise suits and collect damages.

## Orders

Induce clients to sue for damages, serve writs on gentlemen, charge heavy legal fees, and arrest non-paying clients for debt.

## Loses

The reputation of honesty, the capacity for simple compassion, and the standing of a fair officer of justice.

## Drives

The extraction of fees from comfortable gentlemen, turning misunderstanding into lucrative litigation.
