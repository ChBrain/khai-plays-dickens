# Persona: Tony Weller

## Taxonomy

The coachmen of England: a working-class coachman persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Pickwick Papers](play_cdn_001_the_pickwick_papers.md)

## Projection

A large, stout, red-faced coachman, who wears a multi-caped coat and has a deep, hoarse voice.

## Action

Drive the stage-coach, warn Sam against widows, and assist Sam in getting arrested so he can serve Pickwick in prison.

## Shadow

His distrust of widows and simple coachman's wisdom represent the down-to-earth, non-legal world outside the courtroom.

## Tell

He sighs deeply, drinks his gin-and-water, and shakes his head over the dangers of widows.
