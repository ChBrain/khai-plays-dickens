# Piece: Telescope

## Taxonomy

The instruments of science: a notebook and telescope.

## Owner

- Project: [The Pickwick Papers](play_cdn_001_the_pickwick_papers.md)

## Place

[Dingley Dell](place_dingley_dell.md)

## Load Bearing

Pickwick's naive observation. Without this scientific notebook and telescope representing his naive attempt to observe and record human nature, his traveling committee role collapses.

## Apparent

A brass pocket telescope and a thick leather notebook, filled with observations on tittlebats and local customs.

## Yearbook

- For years, Pickwick has kept these notes in his study.
- Today, he uses the telescope to observe the country customs at Dingley Dell.
- The notebook is filled with Alfred Jingle's false descriptions.
