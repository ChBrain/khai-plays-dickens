# Process: Legal Audit

## Taxonomy

The moral mechanisms of predation: a legal audit.

## Owner

- Project: [The Pickwick Papers](play_cdn_001_the_pickwick_papers.md)

## Initiated by

Mrs. Bardell's misunderstanding of Pickwick's conversation about hiring Sam Weller, which she mistakes for a proposal of marriage.

## Direction

The progressive prosecution of the breach-of-promise suit by Dodson and Fogg, showing how legal machinery manufactures a debt and audits naive innocence.

## Lever

The trial verdict and the damages assessment, meeting Pickwick's absolute refusal to pay the predatory lawyers.

## Echo

Pickwick is imprisoned in the Fleet, and the legal trap is eventually resolved by settlement under protest.
