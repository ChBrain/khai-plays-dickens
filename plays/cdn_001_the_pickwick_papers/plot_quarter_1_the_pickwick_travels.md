# Plot: The Pickwick Travels

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [The Pickwick Papers](play_cdn_001_the_pickwick_papers.md)

## Cue

A bright spring morning, where the coach-horn blows and the committee sets out from London.

## Action

[Pickwick](persona_pickwick.md) leads the traveling committee to observe the countryside, while [Jingle](persona_jingle.md) dupes them with slick talk and elopes with Rachel Wardle under the key of the [Benevolent Tour Pitch](pitch_benevolent_tour.md).

## Stage

Pickwick sits at Manor Farm in [Dingley Dell](place_dingley_dell.md), observing customs with his [Telescope](piece_telescope.md), while Jingle schemes to escape with the lady's money.

## Tension

Pickwick's naive trust makes him believe Jingle is an honorable traveler, failing to see the con-man's tricks until the elopement is complete.
