# Persona: Jingle

## Taxonomy

The con-men of the road: a slick-talking actor persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Pickwick Papers](play_cdn_001_the_pickwick_papers.md)

## Projection

Alfred Jingle, a slick-talking, penniless actor in a shabby green coat, who speaks in rapid, broken phrases.

## Action

Dope the Pickwickians, elope with Rachel Wardle for money, and later fall into ruin and end up in the Fleet Prison.

## Shadow

His rapid, broken speech is a tool of deception, hiding his lack of resources and cynical exploitation of naive gentlemen.

## Tell

He speaks in rapid, telegraphic bursts: "Very good, excellent, capital, very."
