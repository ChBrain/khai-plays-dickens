# Place: Courtroom

## Taxonomy

The courts of London: a legal hall.

## Owner

- Project: [The Pickwick Papers](play_cdn_001_the_pickwick_papers.md)

## Shown

A packed, solemn courtroom in the Guildhall, where the judge sits on high, and the bar is crowded with barristers and witnesses.

## Holds

The judge's bench, the jury box, the witness stand, and the tables for lawyers Dodson and Fogg.

## Offers

A stage for legal arguments and the formal trial of breach-of-promise, where simple truth is audited by legal tactics.

## Withheld

Common sense and simple justice, which the legal machinery and technicalities ignore.
