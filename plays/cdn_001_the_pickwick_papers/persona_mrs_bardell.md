# Persona: Mrs. Bardell

## Taxonomy

The landladies of London: a domestic lodging-house persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Pickwick Papers](play_cdn_001_the_pickwick_papers.md)

## Projection

Mrs. Martha Bardell, a comfortable, bustling widow who runs a lodging-house in Goswell Street.

## Action

Tend Pickwick's rooms, misunderstand his conversation as a marriage proposal, sue him for breach-of-promise, and end up in the Fleet herself.

## Shadow

She is easily manipulated by the predatory attorneys Dodson and Fogg, who use her misunderstanding to extract damages from Pickwick.

## Tell

She faints into Pickwick's arms when she believes he has proposed.
