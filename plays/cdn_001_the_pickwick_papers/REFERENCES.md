# The Pickwick Papers: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's novel _The Posthumous Papers of the Pickwick Club_ (1836). The travels of the Pickwickians, staging naive benevolence audited by a breach-of-promise suit and the Fleet Prison.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _The Posthumous Papers of the Pickwick Club_, published in monthly parts from 1836 to 1837, one of The Novels in the house catalog (`cdn_001`, [PP]). The text is in the public domain.
