# Piece: Legal Writ

## Taxonomy

The papers of the law: a writ of breach-of-promise.

## Owner

- Project: [The Pickwick Papers](play_cdn_001_the_pickwick_papers.md)

## Place

[Courtroom](place_courtroom.md)

## Load Bearing

The breach-of-promise suit. Without this writ issued by Dodson and Fogg, the litigation of _Bardell v. Pickwick_ and Pickwick's imprisonment collapse.

## Apparent

A folded sheet of parchment, stamped with the court seal, containing the charge of breach-of-promise of marriage.

## Yearbook

- Last week, Dodson and Fogg prepared the writ in their office.
- Today, the writ is served on Samuel Pickwick, initiating the lawsuit.
- The writ is presented during the trial in the courtroom.
