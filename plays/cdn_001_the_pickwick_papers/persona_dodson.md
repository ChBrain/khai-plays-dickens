# Persona: Dodson

## Taxonomy

The attorneys of London: a predatory legal persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Pickwick Papers](play_cdn_001_the_pickwick_papers.md)

## Projection

A sharp, calculating attorney in his [Attorney](position_attorney.md) position, who with his partner Fogg runs a lucrative practice based on sharp practice and fees.

## Action

File the breach-of-promise suit, secure a verdict through sharp practice, and imprison both Pickwick and Mrs. Bardell for costs.

## Shadow

He is the mechanical engine of legal predation in the play, treating all human relations as opportunities for charging fees.

## Tell

He rubs his hands together and smiles coldly when discussing legal costs.
