# Place: Fleet Prison

## Taxonomy

The prisons of London: a debtor's prison.

## Owner

- Project: [The Pickwick Papers](play_cdn_001_the_pickwick_papers.md)

## Shown

A crowded, dirty, cold prison, where the wards are filled with desperate debtors and the yard is surrounded by high brick walls.

## Holds

Redlaw-like cell rooms, a crowded yard, poor lodgers, and the warden's gates.

## Offers

A cold shelter for those who refuse or cannot pay their debts, where poverty and legal predation are locked up together.

## Withheld

Comfort, cleanliness, and the freedom to walk the open roads of the countryside.
