# Plot: The Fleet Prison

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [The Pickwick Papers](play_cdn_001_the_pickwick_papers.md)

## Cue

A packed courtroom in the Guildhall, shifting to the gates of [Fleet Prison](place_fleet_prison.md).

## Action

The trial of _Bardell v. Pickwick_ takes place in the [Courtroom](place_courtroom.md) under the [Legal Writ](piece_legal_writ.md), leading [Pickwick](persona_pickwick.md) to execute the [Passive Resistance Plan](plan_passive_resistance.md) when [Dodson](persona_dodson.md) wins the verdict.

## Stage

Pickwick stands in the dock before the jury, while [Sam Weller](persona_sam_weller.md) and [Tony Weller](persona_tony_weller.md) watch. Later, Pickwick is locked in a dark cell in the Fleet.

## Tension

Pickwick refuses to pay the damages to the predatory attorneys on principle, choosing to endure the horrors of the debtor's prison instead of yielding to legal predation.
