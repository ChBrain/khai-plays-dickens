# Persona: Pickwick

## Taxonomy

The traveling committees of London: a benevolent president persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Pickwick Papers](play_cdn_001_the_pickwick_papers.md)

## Projection

A retired gentleman in tights and gaiters, with a bald head and circular spectacles, whose face beams with naive benevolence.

## Action

Lead the Pickwick Club committee travels, observe human nature, refuse to pay Dodson and Fogg, and choose to enter the Fleet Prison.

## Shadow

His absolute innocence and lack of suspicion make him a target for con-men like Jingle and predatory lawyers like Dodson.

## Tell

He beams through his spectacles and adjusts his gaiters when pleased.
