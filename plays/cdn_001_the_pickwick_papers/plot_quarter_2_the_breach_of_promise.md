# Plot: The Breach of Promise

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [The Pickwick Papers](play_cdn_001_the_pickwick_papers.md)

## Cue

A London morning at the White Hart Inn, moving to Goswell Street and the attorney's office.

## Action

[Pickwick](persona_pickwick.md) hires [Sam Weller](persona_sam_weller.md) in his [Valet](position_valet.md) position, while [Dodson](persona_dodson.md) in his [Attorney](position_attorney.md) position initiates the [Legal Audit](process_legal_audit.md) process for [Mrs. Bardell](persona_mrs_bardell.md).

## Stage

Sam polishes [Weller's Boots](piece_boots.md) in the yard, while Dodson prepares the [Legal Writ](piece_legal_writ.md) charging Pickwick with breach-of-promise of marriage.

## Tension

Mrs. Bardell faints into Pickwick's arms, creating the misunderstanding that the predatory lawyers exploit to entrap him in the legal machinery.
