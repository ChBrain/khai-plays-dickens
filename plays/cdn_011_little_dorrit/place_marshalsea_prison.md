## Taxonomy

The prisons of Southwark: a debtors' prison.

## Owner

## Shown

A high-walled, crowded debtors' prison in Southwark, with narrow yards, spiked walls, and small dark rooms.

William Dorrit's room, the turnkey's keys, and families ruined by debt.

A cramped, spiked confinement for debtors, which becomes a strange, protective sanctuary.
