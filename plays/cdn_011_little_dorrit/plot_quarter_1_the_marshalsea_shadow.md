# Plot: The Marshalsea Shadow

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Little Dorrit](play_cdn_011_little_dorrit.md)

## Cue

A grey, damp London evening, starting inside [Marshalsea Prison](place_marshalsea_prison.md).

## Action

[Arthur](persona_arthur.md) returns to London and meets [Amy](persona_amy.md) in the prison where her father [Father Dorrit](persona_father_dorrit.md) lives in his [Debtor](position_debtor.md) position, while Arthur is delayed at the [Circumlocution Office](place_circumlocution_office.md) by [Tite Barnacle](persona_tite_barnacle.md) in his [Bureaucrat](position_bureaucrat.md) position.

## Stage

Amy sits sewing by the prison window under the [Marshalsea Shadow Pitch](pitch_marshalsea_shadow.md), while the turnkey locks the [Marshalsea Gate](piece_marshalsea_gate.md) and Tite Barnacle shuffles papers.

## Tension

Arthur's search for truth is stymied by bureaucratic delay, but his interest in Amy's family initiates the [Debt Clearance Plan](plan_debt_clearance.md).
