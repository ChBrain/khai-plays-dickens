# Persona: Arthur

## Taxonomy

The honest seekers of London: a seeker persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Little Dorrit](play_cdn_011_little_dorrit.md)

## Projection

Arthur Clennam, a grave, thoughtful man in his mid-forties, whose face reflects a quiet, earnest search for truth and duty.

## Action

Return to London, investigate William Dorrit's debts, run a business with Daniel Doyce, invest with Merdle, fall into debt, and marry Amy.

## Shadow

His grave sense of guilt and duty makes him susceptible to Mrs. Clennam's cold control, but his honesty leads him to accept his own ruin.

## Tell

He speaks in a measured, gentle voice, asking of the family's past debts.
