# Persona: Amy

## Taxonomy

The daughters of the Marshalsea: a self-sacrificing companion persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Little Dorrit](play_cdn_011_little_dorrit.md)

## Projection

Amy Dorrit (Little Dorrit), a tiny, sweet-faced young woman, whose quiet, self-effacing demeanor hides a strong, loving heart.

## Action

Work as a seamstress, support her father in prison, resist high-society expectations, return to nurse Arthur in prison, and marry him.

## Shadow

Her quiet, self-sacrificing nature makes her a servant to her proud family, but her love provides Arthur with his only true sanctuary.

## Tell

She moves quietly, sewing with neat stitches, and looks out from the Marshalsea window.
