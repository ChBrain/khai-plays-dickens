# Plot: Ruin and Redemption

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Little Dorrit](play_cdn_011_little_dorrit.md)

## Cue

A cold morning inside [Marshalsea Prison](place_marshalsea_prison.md), shifting to the sunny street outside.

## Action

[Merdle](persona_merdle.md) commits suicide with the [Merdle Penknife](piece_merdle_penknife.md), bursting the bubble and sending the ruined [Arthur](persona_arthur.md) behind the [Marshalsea Gate](piece_marshalsea_gate.md), where [Amy](persona_amy.md) nurses him, and the exposure of the [Clennam Codicil](piece_clennam_codicil.md) forces [Mrs Clennam](persona_mrs_clennam.md)'s collapse.

## Stage

Arthur lies sick in his cell while Amy quietens him, and Mrs. Clennam's old house collapses in a heap of dust.

## Tension

The speculation bubble is resolved in total ruin, but Arthur and Amy find redemption and marriage outside the Marshalsea walls.
