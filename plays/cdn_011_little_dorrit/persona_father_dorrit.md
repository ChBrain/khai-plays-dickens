# Persona: Father Dorrit

## Taxonomy

The proud debtors of the Marshalsea: a proud debtor persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Little Dorrit](play_cdn_011_little_dorrit.md)

## Projection

William Dorrit, a grey-haired, dignified gentleman in his [Debtor](position_debtor.md) position, who accepts tribute as the Father of the Marshalsea.

## Action

Lodge in the Marshalsea, accept money from visitors, inherit a great fortune, travel in Europe, demand high-society conformity, and die in Rome.

## Shadow

His dignified pride is a defense against the shame of debt, but it makes him ungrateful to Amy and vulnerable to Merdle's speculation.

## Tell

He bows with grand dignity, accepts a small coin, and strokes his white hair.
