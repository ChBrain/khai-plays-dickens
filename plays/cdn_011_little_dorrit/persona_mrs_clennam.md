# Persona: Mrs Clennam

## Taxonomy

The stern, invalid mothers of London: a stern parent persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Little Dorrit](play_cdn_011_little_dorrit.md)

## Projection

Mrs. Clennam, a cold, hard-featured lady, confined to her wheelchair in a dark, decaying London house, representing stern, self-righteous guilt.

## Action

Manage the family firm, refuse Arthur's questions, hide the secret codicil, face Blandois's blackmail, and collapse in the street.

## Shadow

Her stern, religious self-righteousness is a mask to hide her theft of Arthur's mother's inheritance, resulting in the collapse of her house.

## Tell

She sits rigidly in her wheelchair, muttering of her duty and her guilt, and stares at the dark walls.
