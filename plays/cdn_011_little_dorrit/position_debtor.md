# Position: Debtor

## Taxonomy

The debtors of the Marshalsea: a debtor role.

## Owner

- Project: [Little Dorrit](play_cdn_011_little_dorrit.md)

## Has

A prison room, key access, a list of debts, and a mandate to live within the walls.

## Orders

Sit in prison cells, receive visitors, demand tribute from newer prisoners, and hide past poverty when released.

## Loses

The sense of real independence, and the capacity to live without the protective shadow of the prison walls.

## Drives

The preservation of personal pride and status within the narrow society of the debtors' prison.
