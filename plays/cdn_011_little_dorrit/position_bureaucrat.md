# Position: Bureaucrat

## Taxonomy

The government clerks of London: a bureaucrat role.

## Owner

- Project: [Little Dorrit](play_cdn_011_little_dorrit.md)

## Has

An office desk, red tape, stacks of petitions, and a mandate to manage public affairs.

## Orders

Shuffle papers from tray to tray, advise litigants and inventors 'how not to do it', and ignore all public inquiries.

## Loses

The capacity to resolve any public business or help any citizen who seeks relief.

## Drives

The preservation of department gridlock and the defense of aristocratic family appointments against all reform.
