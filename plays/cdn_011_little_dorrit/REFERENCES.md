# Little Dorrit: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's novel _Little Dorrit_ (1855). A drama of imprisonment and delay, staging the debtors' prison, Circumlocution Office, and speculation.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _Little Dorrit_, published in monthly parts from 1855 to 1857, one of The Novels in the house catalog (`cdn_011`, [LD]). The text is in the public domain.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **The catalog is the index**: this warrant answers for one staged work; the full Dickens catalogue and its `cdn_011` numbering live in the house register, not here.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
