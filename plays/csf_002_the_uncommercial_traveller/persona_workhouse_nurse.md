# Persona: Workhouse Nurse

## Taxonomy

The workhouse administrators of London: a nurse persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Uncommercial Traveller](play_csf_002_the_uncommercial_traveller.md)

## Projection

The workhouse nurse (or matron), a tired, firm middle-aged woman in her [Guardian Position](position_guardian.md) position, who manages the female wards of the Wapping Workhouse.

## Action

Register paupers, check bed cleanliness, read the workhouse ledger, distribute daily bread, and maintain order in the wards.

## Shadow

Her strict, firm manner is a response to the constant pressure of limited parish funds, enforcing rules to keep the institution running.

## Tell

She opens the heavy leather ledger, writing rapidly as she asks paupers their names.
