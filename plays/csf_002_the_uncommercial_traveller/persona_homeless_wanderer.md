# Persona: Homeless Wanderer

## Taxonomy

The nocturnal wanderers of London: a wanderer persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Uncommercial Traveller](play_csf_002_the_uncommercial_traveller.md)

## Projection

The Homeless Wanderer, a quiet, poorly dressed man who sleeps under bridge arches and walks the streets at night.

## Action

Walk the empty streets, shelter under bridge arches, avoid watchmen, and seek daily bread or charity.

## Shadow

His silent, passive presence represents the extreme poverty of the city, surviving the cold night streets with patient quietness.

## Tell

He pulls his thin coat tighter around himself, staring silently at the dark river.
