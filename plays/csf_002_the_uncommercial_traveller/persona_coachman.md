# Persona: Coachman

## Taxonomy

The coach drivers of provincial transit: a coachman persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Uncommercial Traveller](play_csf_002_the_uncommercial_traveller.md)

## Projection

The Coachman, a red-faced, stout coach driver who drives stagecoaches and mail-coaches through the night.

## Action

Drive night stagecoaches, manage horses, converse with the Traveller, and stop at coaching inns for warm drinks.

## Shadow

His tough, weather-beaten nature is a response to long night journeys in cold weather, surviving the road with quiet discipline.

## Tell

He whips his horses, calling out to them, and blows his horn in the cold night air.
