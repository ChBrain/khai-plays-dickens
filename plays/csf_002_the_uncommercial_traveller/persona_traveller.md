# Persona: Traveller

## Taxonomy

The human interest observers of Victorian England: a traveller persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Uncommercial Traveller](play_csf_002_the_uncommercial_traveller.md)

## Projection

The Uncommercial Traveller, a quiet, observant middle-aged gentleman in his [Traveller Position](position_traveller.md) position, who travels for the "house of Human Interest Brothers."

## Action

Walk the London streets at night, visit provincial towns, inspect Wapping Workhouse, converse with coachmen, and record observations in his essays.

## Shadow

His sleepless nights drive him to wander, turning his personal insomnia into a sympathetic study of the city's poorest residents.

## Tell

He taps his stout walking stick on the wet stones, taking a quiet drink from his leather flask.
