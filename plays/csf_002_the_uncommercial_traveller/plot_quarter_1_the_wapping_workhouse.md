# Plot: The Wapping Workhouse

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [The Uncommercial Traveller](play_csf_002_the_uncommercial_traveller.md)

## Cue

A cold, grey stone courtyard of Wapping Workhouse, starting inside [place_wapping_workhouse.md](place_wapping_workhouse.md).

## Action

[Traveller](persona_traveller.md) in his [Traveller Position](position_traveller.md) position executes the [Workhouse Inspection Plan](plan_workhouse_inspection.md) in [place_wapping_workhouse.md](place_wapping_workhouse.md), while [Workhouse Nurse](persona_workhouse_nurse.md) in her [Guardian Position](position_guardian.md) position manages the female pauper wards and registers names in the [Workhouse Ledger](piece_workhouse_ledger.md) under the [Uncommercial Traveller Pitch](pitch_uncommercial_traveller.md), with [Parish Officer](persona_parish_officer.md) defending the cost-efficiency of the institution.

## Stage

The Traveller inspects the clean but bleak pauper beds, while the Workhouse Nurse registers incoming poor women, and the Parish Officer defends the workhouse policies.

## Tension

The Traveller seeks to expose the bleak realities of pauper life while the Workhouse Nurse enforces institutional rules.
