# Piece: Workhouse Ledger

## Taxonomy

The symbols of institutional registry: a heavy leather ledger.

## Owner

- Project: [The Uncommercial Traveller](play_csf_002_the_uncommercial_traveller.md)

## Place

[Wapping Workhouse](place_wapping_workhouse.md)

## Load Bearing

The records of Wapping paupers. Without this heavy leather workhouse ledger representing the official registration of inmates, the workhouse inspection plan collapses.

## Apparent

A large, heavy, leather-bound ledger with thick paper, containing names of local paupers.

## Yearbook

- For years, the workhouse nurse wrote names in this registry.
- Today, she opens the ledger to register a new group of poor inmates.
- The ledger remains a record of local parish poverty and charity.
