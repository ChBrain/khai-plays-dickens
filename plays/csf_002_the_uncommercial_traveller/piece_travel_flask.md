# Piece: Travel Flask

## Taxonomy

The symbols of night journeys: a leather-covered flask.

## Owner

- Project: [The Uncommercial Traveller](play_csf_002_the_uncommercial_traveller.md)

## Place

[Dullborough Town](place_dullborough_town.md)

## Load Bearing

The traveller's night comfort. Without this leather-covered glass flask representing his secret supply of warmth on cold nights, his night transit comfort collapses.

## Apparent

A small, pocket-sized glass flask covered in dark leather, with a metal cup on the base.

## Yearbook

- For years, the Traveller filled this flask with brandy before journeys.
- Today, he takes a drink from the flask in the cold night stagecoach.
- The flask is refilled at coaching inns along the road.
