# Place: Wapping Workhouse

## Taxonomy

The parish workhouses of London: a local workhouse.

## Owner

- Project: [The Uncommercial Traveller](play_csf_002_the_uncommercial_traveller.md)

## Shown

A cold, grey stone building with heavy iron gates, a small courtyard, and clean but bleak pauper wards.

## Holds

Pauper beds, workhouse ledgers, iron gates, and Mr. Bung's office desks.

## Offers

A bleak, institutional refuge and daily bread to the parish poor.

## Withheld

Comfort, freedom, and family hope, which the strict institutional walls refuse to permit.
