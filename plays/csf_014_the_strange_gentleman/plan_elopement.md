# Plan: Elopement Plan

## Taxonomy

The design of a secret marriage escape: an elopement plan.

## Owner

- Owner: [John Johnson](persona_johnson.md)

## Direction

To escape the opposition of family guardians and secure a secret marriage with Mary Wilson by traveling to Gretna Green.

## Orders

Johnson is ordered to book a post-chaise, bribe the inn staff for corridor keys, coordinate the meeting time with Mary, and avoid detection by the host.

## Implementation

The coaching yard, the chamber keys, and the elopement carriage booked for midnight.

## Targets

- [ ] John Johnson arrives at the St. James's Inn and confirms carriage arrangements
- [ ] Johnson secures the chamber key to facilitate Mary's escape from her room
- [ ] The lovers meet in the dark parlour at midnight to make their escape
- [ ] The elopement carriage departs the inn yard before the host can intervene
