# Persona: Julia Dobbs

## Taxonomy

The determined husband-hunters: a wealthy, husband-seeking lady persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Strange Gentleman](play_csf_014_the_strange_gentleman.md)

## Projection

Julia Dobbs, a wealthy lady traveling in search of a husband, who mistakes the Strange Gentleman's panic for romantic devotion.

## Action

Intercept misdelivered letters, confront the Strange Gentleman, and demand he fulfill his supposed promise of marriage.

## Shadow

Her desperation to marry makes her easily deceived by romantic projections, leading her to press her suit on a man who is only trying to hide.

## Tell

She sighs dramatically and reads out passages from romantic novels while waiting in the parlour.
