# Process: Farce of Errors

## Taxonomy

The process of theatrical comic confusion: a farce process.

## Owner

- Project: [The Strange Gentleman](play_csf_014_the_strange_gentleman.md)

## Initiated by

The host's mix-up of guest names and room keys upon the arrival of the afternoon coach.

## Direction

The frantic opening and closing of doors, misdelivery of letters, and misidentification of romantic partners in the dark corridors.

## Lever

The chamber keys and misdelivered letters that drive the misunderstanding between the guests.

## Echo

The resolution of duels and romantic engagements by the mayor, restoring order to the St. James's Inn.
