# Plot: The Misdelivered Letters

## Taxonomy

The fytte of the mixed mail: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [The Strange Gentleman](play_csf_014_the_strange_gentleman.md)

## Cue

A lively evening in the parlour of the St. James's Inn.

## Action

The mail bag arrives at the [Inn Parlour](place_inn_parlour.md), where the host misdistributes the letters, handing the [Misdelivered Letter](piece_misdelivered_letter.md) to [Julia Dobbs](persona_julia_dobbs.md) and igniting the romantic jealousy of [Charles Tomkins](persona_tomkins.md).

## Stage

[Charles Tomkins](persona_tomkins.md) paces the floor in a fit of rage after reading a misdirected note, while [Julia Dobbs](persona_julia_dobbs.md) confronts the Strange Gentleman with the intercepted love letter.

## Tension

The romantic misunderstandings and threats of duels rise to a fever pitch as the guests accuse each other of deception.
