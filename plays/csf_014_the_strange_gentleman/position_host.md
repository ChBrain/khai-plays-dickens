# Position: Host

## Taxonomy

The managers of hotel confusion: a hotel host role.

## Owner

- Project: [The Strange Gentleman](play_csf_014_the_strange_gentleman.md)

## Has

A ledger of guests, a board of chamber keys, authority over chambermaids, and a mayor's chain.

## Orders

Assign rooms to arriving guests, manage the distribution of chamber keys, resolve disputes between guests, and maintain order in the inn.

## Loses

His grip on the room keys, and his temper when guests repeatedly enter the wrong chambers.

## Drives

The management of a busy, respectable hotel and the resolution of guest conflicts through civic authority.
