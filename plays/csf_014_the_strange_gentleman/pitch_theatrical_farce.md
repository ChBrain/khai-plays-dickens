# Pitch: Theatrical Farce Pitch

## Taxonomy

The tuning of tone for the whole play: a production directive.

## Owner

- Owner: [The Strange Gentleman](play_csf_014_the_strange_gentleman.md)

## Tenor

A bustling, brightly lit coaching inn parlour transitioning to dark, drafty guest corridors with slamming doors.

## Undertow

The constant clatter of horses in the yard, the jingling of brass chamber keys, and the loud, urgent knocking of jealous lovers on guest doors.

## Nerve

The comic friction between the Strange Gentleman's panic and the host's complete inability to keep room keys and letters in order.

## Echo

A lighthearted reminder that the best-laid plans of travelers and lovers are easily mixed up by the simple errors of a busy inn keeper.
