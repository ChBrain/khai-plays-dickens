# Plot: The Arrivals

## Taxonomy

The fytte of the hotel check-in: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [The Strange Gentleman](play_csf_014_the_strange_gentleman.md)

## Cue

A busy afternoon lobby at the St. James's Inn.

## Action

The eccentric guests check into [St. James's Inn](place_st_james_inn.md), where [The Strange Gentleman](persona_strange_gentleman.md) registers under a false name to flee a duel, prompting the [Host](position_host.md) to initiate the [Farce of Errors](process_farce_errors.md) under the direction of the [Theatrical Farce Pitch](pitch_theatrical_farce.md).

## Stage

[The Strange Gentleman](persona_strange_gentleman.md) nervously receives the wrong [Chamber Key](piece_chamber_key.md) from [Owen Overton](persona_overton.md), while [Julia Dobbs](persona_julia_dobbs.md) checks the guest ledger for potential husbands.

## Tension

The misallocation of room keys and names sets the stage for a series of room check errors and romantic pursuits.
