# Position: Eloper

## Taxonomy

The planners of secret elopements: an eloper role.

## Owner

- Project: [The Strange Gentleman](play_csf_014_the_strange_gentleman.md)

## Has

An elopement plan, a pocket watch, a carriage booking, and a supply of bribe money.

## Orders

Coordinate a secret escape, secure room keys, avoid detection by family guardians, and reach the carriage on time.

## Loses

His direction in the dark inn corridors, and his patience when keys are mixed up.

## Drives

The pursuit of romantic happiness and escape from parental control through secret travel.
