# The Strange Gentleman: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's shorter work _The Strange Gentleman_ (1836). A theatrical farce of errors, staging mistaken identity, romantic confusion, and eccentric hotel guests.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _The Strange Gentleman_, published in 1836, one of Shorter Fiction and Sketches in the house catalog (`csf_014`, [TSG]). The text is in the public domain.
