# Persona: Owen Overton

## Taxonomy

The hosts of coaching inns: a helpful, busy innkeeper persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Strange Gentleman](play_csf_014_the_strange_gentleman.md)

## Projection

Owen Overton, the mayor and keeper of the St. James's Inn in his [Host](position_host.md) position, who manages room assignments and resolves guest disputes.

## Action

Assign Room Five to the Strange Gentleman, mix up chamber keys, and step in as mayor to resolve the duels and elopements.

## Shadow

His desire to keep his guests happy and preserve the inn's reputation makes him easily swayed by false identities, compounding the room confusion.

## Tell

He bows politely to arriving guests, jingles his key ring, and points proudly to his mayor's medallion.
