# Persona: John Johnson

## Taxonomy

The romantic elopers of the inn: an eloping lover persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Strange Gentleman](play_csf_014_the_strange_gentleman.md)

## Projection

John Johnson, a guest in his [Eloper](position_eloper.md) position, who has planned a secret escape and marriage with Mary Wilson.

## Action

Coordinate the escape with Mary Wilson, bribe the hotel staff for keys, and mistake other guests for his fiancé in the dark corridors.

## Shadow

His focus on the elopement makes him reckless, ignoring the general room confusion and drawing others into his plans.

## Tell
