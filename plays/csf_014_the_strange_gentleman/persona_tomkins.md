# Persona: Charles Tomkins

## Taxonomy

The jealous lovers of the hotel: a suspicious fiancé persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Strange Gentleman](play_csf_014_the_strange_gentleman.md)

## Projection

Charles Tomkins, a traveler who is extremely suspicious of his fiancé, whose jealousy is fanned by the general mix-up of letters at the inn.

## Action

Search Room Five for his fiancé, demand satisfaction from the Strange Gentleman, and accuse others of theft and deception.

## Shadow

His obsessive jealousy makes him misinterpret every conversation as a plot to steal his fiancé's affections.

## Tell

He paces the floor, mutters to himself, and aggressively knocks on doors while demanding entry.
