# Persona: The Strange Gentleman

## Taxonomy

The eccentric hotel guests: a frantic, duel-fleeing traveler persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Strange Gentleman](play_csf_014_the_strange_gentleman.md)

## Projection

The Strange Gentleman, really named Walker, who registers under a false name to escape a duel, only to find himself at the center of the inn's room and key confusion.

## Action

Register under an alias, hide in Room Five to escape challengers, and seek protection from the mayor.

## Shadow

His cowardice and anxiety make him interpret every casual remark or misdelivered message as a threat of death or an invitation to a duel.

## Tell

He startles at the sound of slamming doors and looks nervously out of the window for challengers.
