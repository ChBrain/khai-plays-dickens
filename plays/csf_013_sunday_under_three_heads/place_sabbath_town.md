# Place: Sabbath Town

## Taxonomy

The restricted towns of England: a sabbath town place.

## Owner

- Project: [Sunday Under Three Heads](play_csf_013_sunday_under_three_heads.md)

## Shown

A gloomy town on a Sunday, with shuttered bakery shops, locked public gardens, and silent streets under a grey sky.

## Holds

Closed shops, locked gates, church bells, and gloomy congregations.

## Offers

A grim exhibition of religious duty, forcing the poor to stay indoors or attend church.

## Withheld

Laughter, recreation, fresh bread, and any open gardens or public houses.
