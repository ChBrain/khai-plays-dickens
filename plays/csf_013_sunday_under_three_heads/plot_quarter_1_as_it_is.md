# Plot: As It Is

## Taxonomy

The fytte of the Sabbath as it stands: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Sunday Under Three Heads](play_csf_013_sunday_under_three_heads.md)

## Cue

A quiet, gloomy Sunday morning in the streets of London.

## Action

[Timothy Sparks](persona_timothy_sparks.md) in his [Reformer](position_reformer.md) position walks through the shuttered streets of [Sabbath Town](place_sabbath_town.md), observing the Sabbatarian churchgoers and the closed shops, under the guidance of the [Sunday Reform Pitch](pitch_sunday_reform.md).

## Stage

[Timothy Sparks](persona_timothy_sparks.md) watches the rich carriage-riders roll past to church while poor families walk through the mud, their children forbidden from playing in the locked public squares.

## Tension

The contrast between the wealth of the Sabbatarian advocates and the gloomy, restricted existence of the poor workers creates a silent social indignation.
