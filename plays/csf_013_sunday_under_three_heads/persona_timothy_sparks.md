# Persona: Timothy Sparks

## Taxonomy

The advocates of social freedom: an indignant, reform-minded narrator persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Sunday Under Three Heads](play_csf_013_sunday_under_three_heads.md)

## Projection

Timothy Sparks, the pseudonymous narrator of the pamphlet in his [Reformer](position_reformer.md) position, who observes and exposes Sabbatarian hypocrisy.

## Action

Observe Sabbath hypocrisy, protest against Sir Andrew Agnew's Sabbatarian bill, and publish his reform plan for Sunday recreation.

## Shadow

His fierce anger and indignation can make him impatient with moderate reformers who seek compromise with Sabbatarians.

## Tell

He writes with a sharp, ironic pen and points out the contrast between the rich man's carriage and the poor man's closed bakery.
