# Place: Legislative Hall

## Taxonomy

The chambers of Westminster: a legislative hall place.

## Owner

- Project: [Sunday Under Three Heads](play_csf_013_sunday_under_three_heads.md)

## Shown

The debating chamber of the House of Commons, with green benches, a speaker's chair, and clerks at a central table.

## Holds

Sabbatarian bills, ledgers of law, Member lists, and petition papers.

## Offers

A stage for legislative debate, where the balance between public liberty and restriction is decided.

## Withheld

Simple agreement, direct consensus, and any escape from the public's voice.
