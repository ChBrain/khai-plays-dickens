# Process: Legislative Restriction

## Taxonomy

The process of drafting and proposing restrictive laws: a legislative process.

## Owner

- Project: [Sunday Under Three Heads](play_csf_013_sunday_under_three_heads.md)

## Initiated by

Sir Andrew Agnew's presentation of the Sabbatarian bill in the House of Commons.

## Direction

The step-by-step attempt to ban food preparation, shut down public transport, lock up public gardens, and enforce gloomy church attendance.

## Lever

The Sabbatarian Bill and the support of religious restrictors in Parliament.

## Echo

The confrontation with public indignation led by Timothy Sparks, resulting in the bill's defeat and the preservation of Sunday recreation.
