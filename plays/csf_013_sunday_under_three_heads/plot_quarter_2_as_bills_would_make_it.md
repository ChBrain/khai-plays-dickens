# Plot: As Bills Would Make It

## Taxonomy

The fytte of legislative restriction: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Sunday Under Three Heads](play_csf_013_sunday_under_three_heads.md)

## Cue

A cold morning inside the shops and streets of London.

## Action

[Sir Andrew Agnew](persona_sir_andrew.md) in his [Restrictor](position_restrictor.md) position presents the [Sabbatarian Bill](piece_sabbatarian_bill.md), initiating the [Legislative Restriction](process_legislative_restriction.md) process that threatens to completely shutter all food shops and travel for the working class.

## Stage

The [Sabbatarian Preacher](persona_preacher.md) stands in the pulpit denouncing Sunday recreation, while [The Working Man](persona_working_man.md) stands outside closed bakeries, unable to buy hot dinners or travel to visit family.

## Tension

The threat of absolute starvation and isolation for the poor under Agnew's bill raises the social stakes to a crisis.
