# Sunday Under Three Heads: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's shorter work _Sunday Under Three Heads_ (1836). A drama of social reform, staging Sabbath restrictions, poor-class recreation, and Sabbatarian legislation.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _Sunday Under Three Heads_, published in 1836, one of Shorter Fiction and Sketches in the house catalog (`csf_013`, [SUTH]). The text is in the public domain.
