# Pitch: Sunday Reform Pitch

## Taxonomy

The tuning of tone for the whole play: a production directive.

## Owner

- Owner: [Sunday Under Three Heads](play_csf_013_sunday_under_three_heads.md)

## Tenor

A contrasting visual canvas: the grey, silent, shuttered streets of a restricted town meeting the bright green, sunlit fields of country cricket.

## Undertow

The slow, heavy tolling of church bells over locked gardens, and the sudden, cheerful crack of a cricket bat hitting a ball in the field.

## Nerve

The direct clash between Sir Andrew Agnew’s cold, moralizing laws and the poor working citizens' natural desire for air, sunlight, and Sunday rest.

## Echo

A persistent warning that forcing religious gloom upon the working classes only breeds resentment and vice, whereas healthy recreation brings true moral health.
