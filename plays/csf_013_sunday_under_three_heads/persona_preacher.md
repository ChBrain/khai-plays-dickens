# Persona: Sabbatarian Preacher

## Taxonomy

The enforcers of chapel doctrine: a stern Sabbatarian preacher persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Sunday Under Three Heads](play_csf_013_sunday_under_three_heads.md)

## Projection

The Sabbatarian Preacher, an advocate of absolute Sunday rest who operates in his [Restrictor](position_restrictor.md) position to denounce Sunday recreation.

## Action

Denounce recreation from the pulpit, support Sir Andrew Agnew's Sabbatarian bill, and demand the closure of public spaces.

## Shadow

His narrow focus on dogma blinds him to the human need for rest and happiness, leading him to advocate for laws that make Sunday a day of gloom.

## Tell

He frowns at any sign of Sunday laughter and holds a heavy Bible while delivering his warnings.
