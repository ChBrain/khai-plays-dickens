# Persona: Sir Andrew Agnew

## Taxonomy

The enforcers of religious law: a wealthy Sabbatarian politician persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Sunday Under Three Heads](play_csf_013_sunday_under_three_heads.md)

## Projection

Sir Andrew Agnew, a wealthy Member of Parliament operating in his [Restrictor](position_restrictor.md) position, who seeks to enforce strict religious observance through legislation.

## Action

Draft the Sabbatarian bill, argue for it in the Legislative Hall, and attempt to shut down all Sunday commerce and recreation.

## Shadow

His wealth shields him from the physical consequences of his own bill, leaving him blind to the suffering of the working class.

## Tell

He holds a copy of the bill and speaks of moral duty while ignoring the practical needs of the laboring population.
