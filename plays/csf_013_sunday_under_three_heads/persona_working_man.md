# Persona: The Working Man

## Taxonomy

The laboring classes of the city: a hard-working citizen persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Sunday Under Three Heads](play_csf_013_sunday_under_three_heads.md)

## Projection

The Working Man, a representative of those who labor six days a week and rely on Sunday for rest, fresh air, and family recreation.

## Action

Labor during the week, seek out green fields for a game of cricket, and protest against Sabbatarian limitations.

## Shadow

His lack of political influence leaves him vulnerable to Sabbatarian laws, forcing him to accept restrictions or face fine and imprisonment.

## Tell

He carries a cricket bat on his shoulder and wears his clean Sunday coat with pride.
