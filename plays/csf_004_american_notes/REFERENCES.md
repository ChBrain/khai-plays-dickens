# American Notes: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's shorter work _American Notes_ (1842). A critical travelogue, auditing American penitentiaries, political habits, and the system of southern slavery.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _American Notes_, published in 1842, one of Shorter Fiction and Sketches in the house catalog (`csf_004`, [AN]). The text is in the public domain.
