# Plan: Steamship Crossing Plan

## Taxonomy

The logistics of ocean and continental travel: a crossing plan.

## Owner

- Owner: [Charles Dickens](persona_dickens.md)

## Direction

To navigate the stormy Atlantic Ocean on a modern steamship and conduct an extensive tour of major cities in the United States and Canada.

## Orders

Dickens is ordered to purchase passage tickets, secure his luggage for winter gales, travel by rail and canal-boats, and inspect schools and prisons.

## Implementation

The steamships, railways, and canal-boats of North America.

## Targets

- [ ] Dickens secures tickets for the steamship Britannia from Liverpool to Boston
- [ ] The travelers survive the winter gales of the Atlantic crossing
- [ ] The tour visits Boston, New York, Philadelphia, Washington, and Richmond
- [ ] The travelers cross into Canada to inspect schools and Niagara Falls
