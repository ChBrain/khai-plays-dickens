# Piece: Solitary Key

## Taxonomy

The instruments of isolation: a heavy iron cell key.

## Owner

- Project: [American Notes](play_csf_004_american_notes.md)

## Place

[Eastern Penitentiary](place_eastern_penitentiary.md)

## Load Bearing

The power to enforce absolute solitary confinement. Without this key, the silent cell blocks cannot keep prisoners isolated from the human voice.

## Apparent

A long, heavy iron key with a dark finish, carried on a large ring by the prison turnkey.

## Yearbook

- Yesterday, the key was forged and tested on the thick cell doors.
- Today, the key turns in the lock to shut out the light and sound for a new prisoner.
- Tomorrow, the key hangs in the warden office as a relic of old discipline methods.
