# Position: Critic

## Taxonomy

The observers of foreign customs: a social critic role.

## Owner

- Project: [American Notes](play_csf_004_american_notes.md)

## Has

A travel notebook, letters of introduction, transport tickets, and a sharp pen.

## Orders

Observe public behavior and manners, inspect prisons and asylums, and record critical reflections on foreign society.

## Loses

His patience at tobacco spitting, his initial high hopes for the republic, and his temper over copy piracy.

## Drives

The desire to tell the truth about American institutions, exposing both their achievements and their flaws.
