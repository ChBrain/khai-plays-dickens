# Pitch: American Notes

## Taxonomy

The tuning of tone for the whole play: a production directive.

## Owner

## Tenor

A vast, restless peninsular landscape where bustling, whitewashed cities contrast with muddy streets, wooden tenements, and the heavy damp smell of canal waters and tobacco juice.

## Undertow

The steady, churning splash of steamboat paddles on the rivers, the scraping of canal-boat ropes, the constant metallic clicking of printing presses, and the silent footsteps of prisoners in solitary corridors.

## Nerve

The disillusionment of a northern traveler who expected a democratic paradise but found institutional prison isolation, political spitting habits, and the dark trade of human slavery.

## Echo

A persistent warning that a nation boasting of absolute liberty can easily build the most solitary cells and tolerate the deepest forms of human bondage.
