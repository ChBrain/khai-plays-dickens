# Persona: Alicia

## Taxonomy

The sensible princesses of fairy tales: a helpful daughter persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Holiday Romance](play_csf_009_holiday_romance.md)

## Projection

Princess Alicia, a sensible, industrious, kind eldest daughter of King Watkins, who manages the royal family's kitchen.

## Action

Cook for nineteen siblings, manage the kitchen, save the magic fishbone, refuse to wish until doing her best, and marry Prince Certainty.

## Shadow

Her sensible, hard-working domestic care is a contrast to her father's helpless despair, saving the fairy magic for the absolute worst moment.

## Tell

She rubs her pocket, saying: "We must do our best first, papa, and then the magic will help us."
