# Persona: Schoolmaster

## Taxonomy

The cruel teachers of old England: a schoolmaster persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Holiday Romance](play_csf_009_holiday_romance.md)

## Projection

The Latin schoolmaster, a severe, pedantic, cruel teacher who beats children and forces them to learn Latin grammar.

## Action

Correct grammar, scold children, assign Latin lessons, be captured by Boldheart, and be forced to eat grammar books.

## Shadow

His pedantic, severe authority is completely subverted on the pirate ship, where he becomes a helpless, crying captive forced to obey the boys.

## Tell

He waves a Latin grammar book, shouting: "Translate this sentence, Redforth, or face the rod!"
