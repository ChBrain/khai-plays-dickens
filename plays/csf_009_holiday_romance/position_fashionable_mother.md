# Position: Fashionable Mother

## Taxonomy

The toy-town housekeepers: a fashionable mother role.

## Owner

- Project: [Holiday Romance](play_csf_009_holiday_romance.md)

## Has

A baby doll, keys, a toy tea set, and a mandate to manage the household.

## Orders

Manage the nursery, hire child nurses, send grown-up children to bed early, and host fashionable tea parties.

## Loses

Her dependency, when she manages the toy-town society and treats adults as naughty children.

## Drives

The creation of a playful society where children hold the keys and adults are kept in order.
