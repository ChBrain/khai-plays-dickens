# Pitch: Holiday Romance

## Taxonomy

The tuning of tone for the whole play: a production directive.

## Owner

- Owner: [Holiday Romance](play_csf_009_holiday_romance.md)

## Tenor

A playful, energetic, and imaginative children's schoolroom atmosphere: the sharp clattering of wooden toy swords, the sweet smell of gingerbread and wedding cake, the salt breeze of a pirate ship deck, and the high-pitched scoldings of children playing grown-ups.

## Undertow

The quiet whispers of schoolroom conspiracies, the ticking of school clocks, the rhythmic rustling of paper romances, the distant splashing of sea waves, and the tiny squeaking of baby dolls.

## Nerve

The subversion of dry, boring adult rules by the bright, romantic imagination of children.

## Echo

A persistent reminder that children see the world with a clean simplicity that puts the complicated, money-obsessed anxieties of grown-ups to shame.
