# Holiday Romance: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's shorter work _Holiday Romance_ (1868). A children's romance subverting grown-up rules through mock trials, magic fishbones, and pirate adventures.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _Holiday Romance_, published in 1868, one of Shorter Fiction and Sketches in the house catalog (`csf_009`, [HR]). The text is in the public domain.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **The catalog is the index**: this warrant answers for one staged work; the full Dickens catalogue and its `csf_009` numbering live in the house register, not here.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
