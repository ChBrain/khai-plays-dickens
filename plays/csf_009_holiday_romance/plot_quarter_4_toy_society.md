# Plot: Toy Society

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Holiday Romance](play_csf_009_holiday_romance.md)

## Cue

A parlor room decorated for a children's tea party.

## Action

[Mrs Orange](persona_mrs_orange.md) in her [Fashionable Mother](position_fashionable_mother.md) position manages her toy household, cradling her [Baby Doll](piece_baby_doll.md), hiring grown-ups as servants, sending them to bed early, and hosting tea party gossip.

## Stage

Mrs. Orange sits at a toy tea table pouring tea and cradling the baby doll, while she scolds her grown-up servant for noisy behavior.

## Tension

The children manage the keys and household authority, while adults are treated as naughty children.
