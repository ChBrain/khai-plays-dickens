# Plot: Magic Fishbone

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Holiday Romance](play_csf_009_holiday_romance.md)

## Cue

A busy kitchen with nineteen hungry children, starting inside [place_fairy_kitchen.md](place_fairy_kitchen.md).

## Action

[Alicia](persona_alicia.md) works hard in [place_fairy_kitchen.md](place_fairy_kitchen.md) to cook and support her family, while her father [King Watkins](persona_king_watkins.md) complains of poverty and [Grandmarina](persona_grandmarina.md) lectures him, until Alicia rubs the [Magic Fishbone](piece_magic_fishbone.md) to wish for money when Watkins is in absolute despair.

## Stage

Alicia cooks at the stove, while King Watkins sighs at the table and Fairy Grandmarina appears in peacock feathers to deliver her lecture.

## Tension

Alicia must work hard first before she is allowed to use fairy magic to resolve their poverty.
