# Persona: John Jarndyce

## Taxonomy

The benevolent guardians of Hertfordshire: a guardian persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Bleak House](play_cdn_009_bleak_house.md)

## Projection

John Jarndyce, a handsome, grey-haired gentleman of warm, generous heart, who avoids the discussions of Chancery by retreating to his growlery.

## Action

Adopt Esther, Richard, and Ada, protect them from the legal suit, maintain Bleak House, and complain of the East Wind when troubled.

## Shadow

His benevolence is a sanctuary, but his refusal to confront Chancery directly leaves his wards exposed to its slow, grinding decay.

## Tell

He looks out the window, shivers, and mutters: "The wind is in the East."
