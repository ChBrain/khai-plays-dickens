# Persona: Richard

## Taxonomy

The obsessed litigants of London: a doomed litigant persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Bleak House](play_cdn_009_bleak_house.md)

## Projection

Richard Carstone, a handsome, bright young man in his [Litigant](position_litigant.md) position, whose hopefulness turns to feverish obsession under Chancery's influence.

## Action

Abandon medicine and law, marry Ada Clare, spend all his money on Jarndyce and Jarndyce, and die of despair.

## Shadow

His expectation of a legal fortune turns into a sickness that destroys his health, his careers, and his relationship with John Jarndyce.

## Tell

He looks feverishly at legal papers, declaring: "The suit must end soon, Esther!"
