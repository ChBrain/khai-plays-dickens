# Persona: Esther

## Taxonomy

The housekeeping narrators of England: a narrator persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Bleak House](play_cdn_009_bleak_house.md)

## Projection

Esther Summerson, a young lady of sweet, modest countenance, who carries her keys and chronicles the events of Bleak House with self-effacing care.

## Action

Jingle Bleak House keys, nurse Jo through smallpox, discover her mother Lady Dedlock, search for her in the winter storm, and marry Allan Woodcourt.

## Shadow

Her self-deprecating modesty masks her deep resilience, while her facial scars from smallpox represent her physical sacrifice for the slum's victims.

## Tell

She writes her recollections in a quiet journal and is greeted by the jingling of her keys.
