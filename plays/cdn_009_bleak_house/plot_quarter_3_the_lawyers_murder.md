# Plot: The Lawyer's Murder

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Bleak House](play_cdn_009_bleak_house.md)

## Cue

A dark London night, shattered by a gunshot in Lincoln's Inn.

## Action

[Tulkinghorn](persona_tulkinghorn.md) is murdered, prompting [Bucket](persona_bucket.md) in his [Detective](position_detective.md) position to investigate, which forces the terrified [Lady Dedlock](persona_lady_dedlock.md) to initiate the [Dedlock Hunt Plan](plan_dedlock_hunt.md) and flee into the winter storm.

## Stage

Bucket points his forefinger at the painted Allegory on the ceiling, while Lady Dedlock slips out of her house disguised in poor clothes.

## Tension

The murder is solved by Bucket's arrest of Hortense, but the chase through the winter snow to find Lady Dedlock rises to a crisis.
