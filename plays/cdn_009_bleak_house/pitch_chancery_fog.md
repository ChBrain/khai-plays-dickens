# Pitch: Chancery Fog

## Taxonomy

The tuning of tone for the whole play: a production directive.

## Owner

- Owner: [Bleak House](play_cdn_009_bleak_house.md)

## Tenor

A heavy London fog, wet and mud-caked, hanging over Westminster Hall, contrasting the damp smell of rotting legal briefs and Krook's dusty shop with the bright, cozy hearths and jingling keys of Bleak House.

## Undertow

The slow, grinding machinery of the Court of Chancery, dragging families down into ruin, and the rustling paper of millions of useless legal pages.

## Nerve

The tension of Mr. Tulkinghorn's silent blackmail of Lady Dedlock and the desperation of the chase through the winter snow.

## Echo

A persistent warning that institutions left to decay will rot the society that supports them, while simple domestic affection remains the only true sanctuary.
