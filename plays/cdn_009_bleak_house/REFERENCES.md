# Bleak House: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's novel _Bleak House_ (1852). A drama of legal delay, staging Chancery paralysis, litigant obsession, and detective investigation.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _Bleak House_, published in monthly parts from 1852 to 1853, one of The Novels in the house catalog (`cdn_009`, [BH]). The text is in the public domain.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
