# Position: Detective

## Taxonomy

The police detectives of London: a detective role.

## Owner

- Project: [Bleak House](play_cdn_009_bleak_house.md)

## Has

An official warrant, a notebook, sharp eyes, and a mandate to investigate crimes and find missing persons.

## Orders

Point a silent, detecting finger, shadow suspects through the dark streets, solve murders, and rescue runaways in winter storms.

## Loses

The suspect when they flee into the winter night, and the chance to save them before they reach the cemetery gate.

## Drives

The uncovering of hidden connections, linking the high houses of the aristocracy with the low slums of the city.
