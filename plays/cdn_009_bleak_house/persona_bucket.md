# Persona: Bucket

## Taxonomy

The police detectives of London: a sharp detective persona.

## Owner

## Projection

Inspector Bucket, a sharp, middle-aged officer in his [Detective](position_detective.md) position, with a quiet, watchful eye and a habit of pointing with his forefinger.

## Action

Investigate Tulkinghorn's murder, arrest Mademoiselle Hortense, follow Lady Dedlock's flight through the snow, and find her at the gate.

His sharp, friendly manner is a tool of detection, letting him extract secrets from the poor and the rich alike.
