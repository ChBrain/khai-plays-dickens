# Persona: Lady Dedlock

## Taxonomy

The proud mothers of England: a proud mother persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Bleak House](play_cdn_009_bleak_house.md)

## Projection

Lady Dedlock of Chesney Wold, a cold, elegant lady of high fashion, whose proud face hides a secret past and a deep maternal grief.

## Action

Recognize Hawdon's handwriting, swoon in public, confess her motherhood to Esther, flee Mr. Tulkinghorn's blackmail, and die at Hawdon's grave.

## Shadow

Her cold, bored demeanor is a mask to protect her husband's honor, but her secret past makes her a target for Tulkinghorn's legal predation.

## Tell

She stares coldly at a portrait and whispers of her exhaustion with fashion.
