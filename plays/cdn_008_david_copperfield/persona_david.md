# Persona: David

## Taxonomy

The autobiographical heroes of England: a protagonist persona.

## Owner

- Project: [David Copperfield](play_cdn_008_david_copperfield.md)

## Projection

A young man of earnest, thoughtful face, whose memory holds the personal history of his life, from the Blunderstone rookery to his London struggles.

## Action

Clean wine bottles in the warehouse, walk the Dover road, study at Canterbury, write his memory-history, and marry Agnes Wickfield.

## Shadow

His naive, trustful nature makes him blind to Steerforth's selfishness and Dora's child-like fragility, before his memory teaches him wisdom.

## Tell

She speaks with an earnest, reflective voice, writing his personal experiences in a large notebook.
