# Persona: Micawber

## Taxonomy

The eloquent debtors of London: an optimistic companion persona.

## Owner

- Project: [David Copperfield](play_cdn_008_david_copperfield.md)

## Projection

Wilkins Micawber, a bald, stout man in a very high collar, carrying a double eyeglass and a cane, representing eloquent, debt-ridden optimism.

## Action

Lodge David in London, fall into debtors' prison, work as clerk to Uriah Heep, expose Heep's frauds, and emigrate to Australia.

## Shadow

His constant debt and optimism make him a passive victim of financial crises, but his honesty leads him to risk everything to expose Heep.

## Tell

He flourishes his cane, peers through his eyeglass, and declares: "Annual income twenty pounds, annual expenditure nineteen nineteen and six, result happiness."
