# Persona: Murdstone

## Taxonomy

The cruel stepfathers of England: a step-parent tyrant persona.

## Owner

- Project: [David Copperfield](play_cdn_008_david_copperfield.md)

## Projection

Edward Murdstone, a tall, dark man in his [Stepfather](position_stepfather.md) position, with a black beard, a square jaw, and a cold, silent demeanor.

## Action

Marry David's mother, beat young David for his lessons, send him to Salem House, and evict him to the London warehouse.

## Shadow

He represents the raw, cold force of "firmness" and domestic tyranny, treating child-rearing and family management as an audit of obedience.

## Tell

He speaks in a deep, measured voice, muttering about the necessity of firmness, and strokes his black whiskers.
