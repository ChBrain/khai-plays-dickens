# Place: Murdstone Warehouse

## Taxonomy

The dark warehouses of London: a bottling warehouse.

## Owner

- Project: [David Copperfield](play_cdn_008_david_copperfield.md)

## Shown

A dark, dirty, rat-infested bottling warehouse by the Thames, smelling of old corks, sour wine, and mud.

## Holds

Piles of empty wine bottles, wash-tubs, packing cases, and David's small workspace.

## Offers

A grueling, exhausting livelihood of manual labor for young David, keeping him in poverty.

## Withheld

Education, safety, and family comfort, which the dark walls refuse to offer the young boy.
