# Plot: The Warehouse Drudge

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [David Copperfield](play_cdn_008_david_copperfield.md)

## Cue

A dark London winter night, starting inside [Murdstone Warehouse](place_murdstone_warehouse.md).

## Action

[Murdstone](persona_murdstone.md) in his [Stepfather](position_stepfather.md) position sends young [David](persona_david.md) to work in the warehouse, lodging him with the debt-ridden [Micawber](persona_micawber.md) under the key of the [Personal History Pitch](pitch_personal_history.md).

## Stage

David sits in the warehouse washing bottles and holding the [Copperfield Caul](piece_copperfield_caul.md) in his pocket, while the Micawber family is evicted for debt.

## Tension

David's physical and emotional neglect in the warehouse prompts him to initiate the [Dover Flight Plan](plan_dover_flight.md) to run away.
