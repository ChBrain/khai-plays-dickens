# Persona: Uriah Heep

## Taxonomy

The hypocritical clerks of Canterbury: a treacherous clerk persona.

## Owner

- Project: [David Copperfield](play_cdn_008_david_copperfield.md)

## Projection

Uriah Heep, a young man in his [Clerk](position_clerk.md) position, with a cadaverous face, red hair cropped close, and damp, cold hands he constantly rubs.

## Action

Work for Mr. Wickfield, alter the estate ledgers, blackmail his master, demand Agnes's hand, and end his days in prison.

## Shadow

His constant talk of being "umble" is a tool of social climbing and malice, hiding a cold ambition that seeks to ruin the family.

## Tell

He rubs his hands, wriggles his body snake-like, and repeats: "We are very umble, Master Copperfield."
