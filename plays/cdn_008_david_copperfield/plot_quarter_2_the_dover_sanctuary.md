# Plot: The Dover Sanctuary

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [David Copperfield](play_cdn_008_david_copperfield.md)

## Cue

A bright summer morning on the Dover cliffs, starting in [Dover Cottage](place_dover_cottage.md).

## Action

[David](persona_david.md) executes the [Dover Flight Plan](plan_dover_flight.md), reaching Dover where [Aunt Betsey](persona_aunt_betsey.md) adopts him and sends him to study in Canterbury, lodging him at [Wickfield House](place_wickfield_house.md) with [Agnes](persona_agnes.md).

## Stage

Mr. Dick flies the giant [Dick Kite](piece_dick_kite.md) on the cliffs, while Aunt Betsey chases donkeys from her lawn and Agnes welcomes David to Canterbury.

## Tension

The flight is resolved by Aunt Betsey's protection, but the quiet Canterbury shelter is threatened by the arrival of the new clerk Heep.
