# David Copperfield: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's novel _David Copperfield_ (1849). A personal history of growth and memory, staging step-parent tyranny, fawning blackmail, and bohemian optimism.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _David Copperfield_, published in monthly parts from 1849 to 1850, one of The Novels in the house catalog (`cdn_008`, [DC]). The text is in the public domain.
