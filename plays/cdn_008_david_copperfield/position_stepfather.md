# Position: Stepfather

## Taxonomy

The step-parent tyrants of England: a step-parent tyrant role.

## Owner

- Project: [David Copperfield](play_cdn_008_david_copperfield.md)

## Has

A cane, a black bottling warehouse, authority over domestic properties, and a mandate to manage and punish children.

## Orders

Beat pupils for failing lessons, audit and control the mother's property, send disobedient boys to work in London warehouses, and demand cold obedience.

## Loses

The right to direct the boy's life when he runs away, and the respect of society when his cruelty is exposed.

## Drives

The imposition of cold, firm discipline over the household, converting a family into a rigid system of punishment and labor.
