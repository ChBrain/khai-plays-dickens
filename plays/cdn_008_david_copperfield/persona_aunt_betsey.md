# Persona: Aunt Betsey

## Taxonomy

The eccentric aunts of Dover: a strong-willed guardian persona.

## Owner

- Project: [David Copperfield](play_cdn_008_david_copperfield.md)

## Projection

Betsey Trotwood, an eccentric, tall, upright lady, with a stern face that hides a warm, generous heart, who wages war on donkeys.

## Action

Adopt the runaway David, confront the Murdstones, protect Mr. Dick, lose her fortune to Heep's fraud, and cheer Agnes and David.

## Shadow

Her eccentric, stern behavior is a defense against past marital grief, but her integrity offers David his truest protection.

## Tell

She rushes into the garden shouting: "Janet! Donkeys!" and beats her hands on her lap.
