# Place: Scrooge's Chambers

## Taxonomy

The private residences of London: a place of shelter.

## Owner

- Project: [A Christmas Carol](play_ccb_001_a_christmas_carol.md)

## Shown

A gloomy suite of rooms in a lowering pile of a building, dark and cold, where the stairs are so wide that a hearse might run up them.

## Holds

Scrooge's fireplace, his easy chair, a bowl of gruel on the hob, and the old knocker on the door that changes to Marley's face.

## Offers

A dark refuge for a solitary man who prefers cheapness and darkness to light and comfort.

## Withheld

Peace of mind, which Marley's Ghost disrupts with the clanking of chains.
