# Persona: Tiny Tim

## Taxonomy

The children of the poor: a working-class child persona.

## Owner

- Project: [A Christmas Carol](play_ccb_001_a_christmas_carol.md)

## Projection

A little child, patient and mild, carrying a crutch and having his limbs supported by an iron frame, representing the innocent victim of social neglect.

## Action

Utter the blessing of Christmas: God bless us every one.

## Shadow

He is a passive figure who cannot save himself, whose life is entirely balanced on the ledger decisions of Scrooge's office.

## Tell

His steps are slow and heavy, matching the sound of his crutch on the stone floor.
