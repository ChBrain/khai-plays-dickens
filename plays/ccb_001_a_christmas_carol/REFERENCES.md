# A Christmas Carol: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's Christmas Book _A Christmas Carol_ (1843). The reclamation of a tight-fisted calculator under the audit of three spirits and a chain-bound ghost.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _A Christmas Carol_, published in 1843, one of The Christmas Books in the house catalog (`ccb_001`, [CC]). The text is in the public domain.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **The catalog is the index**: this warrant answers for one staged work; the full Dickens catalogue and its `ccb_001` numbering live in the house register, not here.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
