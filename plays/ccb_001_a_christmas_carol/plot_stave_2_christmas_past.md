# Plot: Christmas Past

## Taxonomy

The fytte of five staves: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [A Christmas Carol](play_ccb_001_a_christmas_carol.md)

## Cue

The first stroke of the bell at midnight, when the room is flooded with a supernatural light in [Scrooge's Chambers](place_scrooge_chambers.md) and shifts to [Past Scenes](place_past_scenes.md).

## Action

The [Ghost of Christmas Past](persona_ghost_past.md) initiates the [Spiritual Audit](process_spiritual_audit.md) under the [Spiritual Auditor](position_spiritual_auditor.md) position, taking [Scrooge](persona_scrooge.md) back to witness his boyhood solitude and the loss of Belle.

## Stage

The [Ghost of Christmas Past](persona_ghost_past.md) stands by Scrooge's bed, holding his hand, and guides him through the schoolroom and Fezziwig's warehouse where Scrooge once danced.

## Tension

[Scrooge](persona_scrooge.md) is filled with regret as he witnesses his early choices, attempting to extinguish the spirit's light with its cap to escape the pain of memory.
