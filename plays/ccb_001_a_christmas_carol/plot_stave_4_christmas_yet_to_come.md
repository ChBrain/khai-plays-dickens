# Plot: Christmas Yet to Come

## Taxonomy

The fytte of five staves: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [A Christmas Carol](play_ccb_001_a_christmas_carol.md)

## Cue

A solemn phantom, draped in black, approaching like a mist in [Scrooge's Chambers](place_scrooge_chambers.md) and shifting to [Churchyard](place_churchyard.md).

## Action

The [Ghost of Christmas Yet to Come](persona_ghost_yet_to_come.md) conducts the final stage of the [Spiritual Audit](process_spiritual_audit.md), leading [Scrooge](persona_scrooge.md) to witness his own death and the grief of Bob's family.

## Stage

The [Ghost of Christmas Yet to Come](persona_ghost_yet_to_come.md) stands silently, pointing with its hand, while Scrooge looks upon the untended [Scrooge's Grave](piece_scrooge_grave.md) and Bob's empty hearth.

## Tension

[Scrooge](persona_scrooge.md) is filled with terror as he realizes the neglected grave is his own, pleading with the spirit to let him sponge away the writing on the stone.
