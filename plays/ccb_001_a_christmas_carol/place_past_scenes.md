# Place: Past Scenes

## Taxonomy

The locations of memory: a place of history.

## Owner

- Project: [A Christmas Carol](play_ccb_001_a_christmas_carol.md)

## Shown

A solitary schoolroom where a boy sits neglected, followed by the bright warehouse of Fezziwig filled with music and dancing.

## Holds

The desk where young Scrooge read, the high desk of Fezziwig, and the ghosts of forgotten companions.

## Offers

A view of Scrooge's early years, showing when the seeds of his cold calculations were first sown and when joy was still cheap to him.

## Withheld

The power to speak to the figures of the past, who remain deaf to the old man's cries.
