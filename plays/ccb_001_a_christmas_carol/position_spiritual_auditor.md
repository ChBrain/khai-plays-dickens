# Position: Spiritual Auditor

## Taxonomy

The agencies of spiritual intervention: a supernatural office.

## Owner

- Project: [A Christmas Carol](play_ccb_001_a_christmas_carol.md)

## Has

The authority to control time and space, the mandate to review a mortal's life, and the light of truth that cannot be hidden.

## Orders

Present the scenes of memory, sympathy, or dread, and compel the mortal to look upon their own actions and their consequences.

## Loses

The capacity to alter the events they show, remaining strictly as witnesses to what has been, what is, or what must be.

## Drives

The moral audit of the mortal's soul, forcing the cold calculator to witness the human cost of his choices and initiate the process of reclamation.
