# Plan: Salvation Plan

## Taxonomy

The spiritual directives of Jacob Marley: a personal salvation mandate.

## Owner

- Owner: [Marley](persona_marley.md)

## Direction

To rescue Scrooge from forging a chain longer and heavier than the one Marley drags, by confronting him with three spirits of warning.

## Orders

The spirits are ordered to visit Scrooge in succession on three consecutive nights, beginning at the stroke of one.

## Implementation

Supernatural visitations that bend time and space, bypassing Scrooge's locked doors and shuttered windows.

## Targets

- [ ] Scrooge is audited by the Ghost of Christmas Past
- [ ] Scrooge is audited by the Ghost of Christmas Present
- [ ] Scrooge is audited by the Ghost of Christmas Yet to Come
- [ ] Scrooge's moral calculations are broken and he repents
