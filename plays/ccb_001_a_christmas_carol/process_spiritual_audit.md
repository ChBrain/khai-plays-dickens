# Process: Spiritual Audit

## Taxonomy

The moral mechanisms of reform: a spiritual audit.

## Owner

- Project: [A Christmas Carol](play_ccb_001_a_christmas_carol.md)

## Initiated by

The stroke of one, following Marley's warning, when the room is prepared for the arrival of the spirits.

## Direction

The progressive softening of Scrooge's cold heart by showing him scenes of his past joy, his clerk's present suffering, and his own lonely death.

## Lever

The spirits' displays of truth meeting Scrooge's stubborn resistance and defenses.

## Echo

Scrooge's moral stance is altered, paving the way for the next audit and eventually his complete reclamation.
