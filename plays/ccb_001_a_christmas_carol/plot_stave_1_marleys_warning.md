# Plot: Marley's Warning

## Taxonomy

The fytte of five staves: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [A Christmas Carol](play_ccb_001_a_christmas_carol.md)

## Cue

A cold, dark night on Christmas Eve, where the frost is biting and the fog is thick in the street, starting in [Counting-House](place_counting_house.md) and shifting to [Scrooge's Chambers](place_scrooge_chambers.md).

## Action

[Scrooge](persona_scrooge.md) calculates his profits and refuses [Fred](persona_fred.md)'s invitation, while [Marley](persona_marley.md) appears in the chambers to warn Scrooge of his impending fate and table the [Salvation Plan](plan_salvation.md) under the key of [Moral Reclamation](pitch_reclamation.md).

## Stage

[Scrooge](persona_scrooge.md) sits at his high desk with his [Account Ledger](piece_ledger.md), while [Bob Cratchit](persona_bob_cratchit.md) occupies his cold cell as the [Clerk](position_clerk.md). Later, in the chambers, Scrooge sits by his fireplace before [Marley](persona_marley.md) enters, dragging [Marley's Chain](piece_marleys_chain.md).

## Tension

[Scrooge](persona_scrooge.md) tries to dismiss the phantom as a humbug caused by a spot of undigested beef, while [Marley](persona_marley.md)'s presence forces him to confront the weight of the invisible chain he is forging himself.
