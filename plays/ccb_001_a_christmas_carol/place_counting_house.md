# Place: Counting-House

## Taxonomy

The commercial environments of London: a place of business.

## Owner

- Project: [A Christmas Carol](play_ccb_001_a_christmas_carol.md)

## Shown

A cold, dark office on a foggy Christmas Eve, where the cold without is matched by the cold within, and the gas lamps flare redly through the gloom.

## Holds

Scrooge's desk, Bob's cell, a low fire with a single coal, and the heavy iron safe.

## Offers

A place to trade, calculate, and store the receipts of interest and debt, isolated from the cheer of the street.

## Withheld

The warmth of human sympathy, which Scrooge refuses to release to his clerk or his nephew.
