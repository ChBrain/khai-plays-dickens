# Persona: Ghost of Christmas Past

## Taxonomy

The spirits of memory: a ghostly persona.

## Owner

- Project: [A Christmas Carol](play_ccb_001_a_christmas_carol.md)

## Projection

A strange figure, like a child, yet not so like a child as like an old man, holding a branch of fresh green holly, from whose head shines a bright jet of light, serving as a [Spiritual Auditor](position_spiritual_auditor.md).

## Action

Display the scenes of Scrooge's childhood, youth, and early manhood, showing when his heart first hardened.

## Shadow

It cannot alter any past choice or stave, representing only what has been, and must witness Scrooge's attempts to extinguish its light.

## Tell

The light from its head flares and dims as Scrooge's memory resists or yields to the truth.
