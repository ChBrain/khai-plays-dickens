# Piece: Account Ledger

## Taxonomy

The tools of commerce: a financial ledger.

## Owner

- Project: [A Christmas Carol](play_ccb_001_a_christmas_carol.md)

## Place

[Counting-House](place_counting_house.md)

## Load Bearing

Scrooge's identity as a cold, tight-fisted calculator. Without this ledger, his daily activity of checking balances and counting interests collapses.

## Apparent

A large, leather-bound volume with ink-stained pages, filled with columns of figures, credits, and debits, showing Marley's death and Scrooge's transactions.

## Yearbook

- Seven years ago, Marley died, and Scrooge wrote his name on the ledger.
- For seven years, Scrooge added the interest of his loans to the book.
- Yesterday, Scrooge reviewed the ledger before closing the office.
