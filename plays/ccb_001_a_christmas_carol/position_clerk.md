# Position: Clerk

## Taxonomy

The office staff of the counting-house: a professional role.

## Owner

- Project: [A Christmas Carol](play_ccb_001_a_christmas_carol.md)

## Has

A cold, cramped desk in a dismal cell, a single coal to keep warm, and a tankard of water, holding the expectation of absolute compliance and low wages.

## Orders

Copy the letters, keep the ledger current, and remain silent in the presence of the master.

## Loses

The freedom to speak out against the master, the comfort of warmth, and the ordinary hours of Christmas Day.

## Drives

The daily maintenance of the counting-house records, translating the master's transactions into ink, while carrying the weight of a family on a weekly salary of fifteen shillings.
