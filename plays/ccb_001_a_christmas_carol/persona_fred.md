# Persona: Fred

## Taxonomy

The nephews of Scrooge: a middle-class persona.

## Owner

- Project: [A Christmas Carol](play_ccb_001_a_christmas_carol.md)

## Projection

A handsome, hearty young man, with eyes that sparkled and breath that smoked, whose face was ruddy and warm.

## Action

Invite Scrooge to Christmas dinner, defend the holiday as a kind, forgiving, charitable time, and toast his uncle's health.

## Shadow

He cannot see why Scrooge clings so stubbornly to his cold calculations and isolation, representing only the active invitation Scrooge refuses.

## Tell

His laugh is hearty and infectious, ringing out to fill the room with cheer.
