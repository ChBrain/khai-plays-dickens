# Place: Churchyard

## Taxonomy

The final resting places: a place of death.

## Owner

- Project: [A Christmas Carol](play_ccb_001_a_christmas_carol.md)

## Shown

A dark, neglected churchyard overrun by weeds, walled in by houses, choked up with too much burying.

## Holds

Scrooge's grave, the untended grass, and the cold stone marker of a forgotten name.

## Offers

A terminal destination for Scrooge's selfish life, showing the end of a trajectory that carries no love or memory.

## Withheld

Comfort or hope, which the dark stone refuses to yield until Scrooge repents.
