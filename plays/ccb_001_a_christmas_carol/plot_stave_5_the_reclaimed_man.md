# Plot: The Reclaimed Man

## Taxonomy

The fytte of five staves: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [A Christmas Carol](play_ccb_001_a_christmas_carol.md)

## Cue

A bright, clear, cold morning on Christmas Day, with the bells ringing out for church, starting in [Scrooge's Chambers](place_scrooge_chambers.md) and shifting to [Counting-House](place_counting_house.md).

## Action

[Scrooge](persona_scrooge.md) wakes up to discover he is alive, resolves the [Salvation Plan](plan_salvation.md) with success, sends the turkey to [Bob Cratchit](persona_bob_cratchit.md), and visits [Fred](persona_fred.md).

## Stage

[Scrooge](persona_scrooge.md) stands in his room laughing and crying, then visits Fred's house. On the day after, Scrooge sits at his desk in the counting-house, waiting to catch Bob arriving late.

## Tension

[Bob Cratchit](persona_bob_cratchit.md) arrives late, fearing Scrooge's anger, while Scrooge plays the tyrant before raising Bob's salary and promising to help his family, resolving the tension with joy.
