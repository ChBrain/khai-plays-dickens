# Place: Cratchit Home

## Taxonomy

The poor households of Camden Town: a place of family.

## Owner

- Project: [A Christmas Carol](play_ccb_001_a_christmas_carol.md)

## Shown

A small, poor dwelling, active with children and smelling of sage and onion, where the family gathers in threadbare clothes.

## Holds

The small table, Bob's meager fire, the copper where the pudding is boiled, and Tim's active wooden crutch.

## Offers

A space of warmth, mutual love, and Christmas cheer, maintained on a weekly budget of fifteen shillings.

## Withheld
