# Pitch: Moral Reclamation

## Taxonomy

The tuning of tone for the whole play: a production directive.

## Owner

- Owner: [A Christmas Carol](play_ccb_001_a_christmas_carol.md)

## Tenor

A Victorian serial storyteller's register: humane, ironic, socially observant, with concrete circumstantial detail, showing the cold streets of London as a living machine.

## Undertow

The dark, persistent threat of spiritual stagnation: the possibility that Scrooge might remain hard and sharp as flint, refusing the audits.

## Nerve

The reader's sympathy and social conscience, struck by the contrast between Scrooge's locked coffers and the open warmth of the poor.

## Echo

A lingering sense of conversion, where the cold ledgers of calculation are replaced by the active bookkeeping of charity and common welfare.
