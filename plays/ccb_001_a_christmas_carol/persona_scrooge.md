# Persona: Scrooge

## Taxonomy

The partners of Scrooge and Marley: a merchant persona.

## Owner

- Project: [A Christmas Carol](play_ccb_001_a_christmas_carol.md)

## Projection

A squeezing, wrenching, grasping, scraping, clutching, covetous old sinner, hard and sharp as flint, from whom no steel had ever struck out generous fire.

## Action

Calculate the cost and cash value of all human interactions and transactions.

## Shadow

He cannot see that his accumulation of wealth is actually a systematic accumulation of debt to his fellow men, forging a chain he will drag in death.

## Tell

A cold frost shivers his chin, and his thin lips grow blue when he speaks of charity.
