# Plot: Christmas Present

## Taxonomy

The fytte of five staves: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [A Christmas Carol](play_ccb_001_a_christmas_carol.md)

## Cue

The second stroke of the bell, when a warm, bright light shines from the next room in [Scrooge's Chambers](place_scrooge_chambers.md) and shifts to [Cratchit Home](place_cratchit_home.md).

## Action

The [Ghost of Christmas Present](persona_ghost_present.md) conducts the second stage of the [Spiritual Audit](process_spiritual_audit.md), showing [Scrooge](persona_scrooge.md) the active Christmas Day and Bob's hearth.

## Stage

The [Ghost of Christmas Present](persona_ghost_present.md) sits on a throne of Christmas food, holding his torch, while [Bob Cratchit](persona_bob_cratchit.md) and [Tiny Tim](persona_tiny_tim.md) gather at the [Cratchit Hearth](piece_cratchit_hearth.md) to toast Scrooge.

## Tension

[Scrooge](persona_scrooge.md) asks if [Tiny Tim](persona_tiny_tim.md) will live, only to have the spirit quote his own words about decreasing the surplus population back to him, as the figures of Ignorance and Want are revealed.
