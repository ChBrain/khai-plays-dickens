# Persona: Ghost of Christmas Yet to Come

## Taxonomy

The spirits of dread: a ghostly persona.

## Owner

- Project: [A Christmas Carol](play_ccb_001_a_christmas_carol.md)

## Projection

A solemn phantom draped in a deep black garment, which conceals its head, its face, and its form, showing nothing but one outstretched hand, serving as a [Spiritual Auditor](position_spiritual_auditor.md).

## Action

Display the future consequences of Scrooge's choices, showing the unmourned death of a man and the grief of the Cratchits.

## Shadow

It never speaks or answers, representing the cold inevitability of time and the terminal vector of Scrooge's present course.

## Tell

The outstretched hand shivers and shrinks when Scrooge promises to change his ways and keep Christmas in his heart.
