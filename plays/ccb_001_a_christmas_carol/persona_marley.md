# Persona: Marley

## Taxonomy

The partners of Scrooge and Marley: a ghostly persona.

## Owner

- Project: [A Christmas Carol](play_ccb_001_a_christmas_carol.md)

## Projection

A transparent phantom in coat, tights, and boots, dragging a chain of ledgers, cash-boxes, and heavy purses, carrying the chilling air of the tomb.

## Action

Warn Scrooge of the heavy weight of his future punishment and announce the coming of the three spirits.

## Shadow

He did not see during life that his business should have been common humanity, charity, mercy, and benevolence, not the counting-house.

## Tell

He raises a frightful cry and shakes his chain, and his lower jaw drops down upon his breast when the bandage is removed.
