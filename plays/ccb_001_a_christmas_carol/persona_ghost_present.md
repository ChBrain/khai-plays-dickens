# Persona: Ghost of Christmas Present

## Taxonomy

The spirits of sympathy: a ghostly persona.

## Owner

- Project: [A Christmas Carol](play_ccb_001_a_christmas_carol.md)

## Projection

A majestic giant clad in a simple green robe, holding a glowing torch shaped like a horn of plenty, carrying a joyful air of abundance and charity, serving as a [Spiritual Auditor](position_spiritual_auditor.md).

## Action

Display the active, breathing world of Christmas Day, showing how the poorest houses and miners keep the feast.

## Shadow

It lives only for a single day, its life ending at midnight, and carries the hidden, clawed figures of Ignorance and Want beneath its robes.

## Tell

Its hair turns grey as the night advances, and its voice grows solemn when it quotes Scrooge's own words back to him.
