# Persona: Bob Cratchit

## Taxonomy

The clerks of London: a working-class persona.

## Owner

- Project: [A Christmas Carol](play_ccb_001_a_christmas_carol.md)

## Projection

A mild, humble [Clerk](position_clerk.md) in a threadbare comforter, carrying his crippled son on his shoulder, representing the moral center of the play.

## Action

Toil silently at his desk, return home to keep the feast, and toast the master as the founder of the feast despite his wife's anger.

## Shadow

He is powerless to change the economic conditions that grind him down, unable to buy medical aid for Tim without the master's grace.

## Tell

His voice cracks with tears when he speaks of Tim's possible death, and he rubs his cold hands together to warm them.
