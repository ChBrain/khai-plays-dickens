# Plot: The Windy Tower

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [The Chimes](play_ccb_002_the_chimes.md)

## Cue

Midnight on New Year's Eve, when the wind howls and the bells strike the hour in the dark [Church Tower](place_church_tower.md).

## Action

[Trotty](persona_trotty.md) ascends the tower, drawn by the bells, where the [Goblin of the Chimes](persona_goblin_chimes.md) initiates the [Goblin Audit](process_goblin_audit.md) to test his faith in humanity.

## Stage

[Trotty](persona_trotty.md) climbs the wooden steps to the rafters, standing before the [Great Bell](piece_bell.md) as the goblins rise from the metal of the bell.

## Tension

The [Goblin of the Chimes](persona_goblin_chimes.md) accuses [Trotty](persona_trotty.md) of believing the poor are bad by birth, sentencing him to look upon the shadow years to see the cost of his despair.
