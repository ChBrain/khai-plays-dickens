# Plot: The River Edge

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [The Chimes](play_ccb_002_the_chimes.md)

## Cue

A cold night by the dark river, where [Meg](persona_meg.md) stands in absolute despair, shifting back to the belfry tower.

## Action

[Meg](persona_meg.md) prepares to jump into the river to escape her destitution, while [Trotty](persona_trotty.md) cries out to the [Goblin of the Chimes](persona_goblin_chimes.md) that he was wrong, resolving the [Goblin Audit](process_goblin_audit.md) and waking on New Year's Day.

## Stage

[Trotty](persona_trotty.md) clings to the [Great Bell](piece_bell.md) in his dream, before waking up in [Trotty's Home](place_trotty_home.md) to see Meg and Richard celebrating.

## Tension

[Trotty](persona_trotty.md) must conquer his despair and reclaim his faith in the poor before Meg jumps, pleading with the spirits as the chimes ring out the old year.
