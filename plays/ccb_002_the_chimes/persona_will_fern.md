# Persona: Will Fern

## Taxonomy

The country laborers of England: a desperate persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Chimes](play_ccb_002_the_chimes.md)

## Projection

A tall, strong, country laborer in ruined clothes, who is hunted from parish to parish with his orphaned niece Lilian.

## Action

Flee from the law, seek work in London, and confront the magistrate with the desperate truth of the laborer's life.

## Shadow

He cannot find an honest way to live within a system that outlawed his migration, eventually driven to arson and prison.

## Tell

He clenches his fists and speaks with a rough, passionate voice when confronting the wealthy.
