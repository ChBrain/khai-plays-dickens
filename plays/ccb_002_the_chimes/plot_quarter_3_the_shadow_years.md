# Plot: The Shadow Years

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [The Chimes](play_ccb_002_the_chimes.md)

## Cue

The visions of the future years, showing the decay of hopes and the slide into poverty, shifting to [Fern's Hovel](place_fern_hovel.md).

## Action

The [Goblin of the Chimes](persona_goblin_chimes.md) displays the ruin of [Richard](persona_richard.md), who becomes a broken drunkard, while [Will Fern](persona_will_fern.md) is imprisoned, and [Meg](persona_meg.md) works to survive.

## Stage

[Trotty](persona_trotty.md) watches invisibly as [Meg](persona_meg.md) plies [Meg's Needle](piece_megs_needle.md) late into the night to support her child, while Richard slouches in the street.

## Tension

[Trotty](persona_trotty.md) witnesses [Meg](persona_meg.md)'s endless labor and Richard's decay, unable to comfort them or halt the downward slide of their lives.
