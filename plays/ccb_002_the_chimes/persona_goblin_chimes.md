# Persona: Goblin of the Chimes

## Taxonomy

The spirits of the bells: a ghostly persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Chimes](play_ccb_002_the_chimes.md)

## Projection

A weird, swarming goblin figure that rises from the metal of the bell, carrying the heavy vibration of the chime.

## Action

Accuse Trotty of losing faith in humanity and sentence him to witness the future.

## Shadow

It cannot alter the future it displays, representing only the voice of the bells and the cold consequence of Trotty's despair.

## Tell

The goblin's form dissolves into mist and reform when the bell rings out.
