# Plot: The Porter Badge

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [The Chimes](play_ccb_002_the_chimes.md)

## Cue

A cold New Year's Eve, where the wind blows through the streets, starting by the church wall where [Trotty](persona_trotty.md) waits.

## Action

[Trotty](persona_trotty.md) in his [Porter](position_porter.md) position waits for errands, while [Alderman Cute](persona_alderman_cute.md) in his [Magistrate](position_magistrate.md) position lectures [Meg](persona_meg.md) and [Richard](persona_richard.md) against marrying under the [Social Suppression Plan](plan_social_suppression.md).

## Stage

[Trotty](persona_trotty.md) wears his brass [Porter Badge](piece_porter_badge.md) and eats tripe brought by [Meg](persona_meg.md), before they are called to [Cute's Hall](place_cute_hall.md). Later, at [Trotty's Home](place_trotty_home.md), Trotty reads the [Newspaper](piece_newspaper.md) under the key of the [Goblin Sermon](pitch_goblin_sermon.md) and falls into despair.

## Tension

The magistrate's lecture that the poor have no right to exist convinces [Trotty](persona_trotty.md) that his daughter's marriage is a crime, filling him with despair as he reads of poverty in the news.
