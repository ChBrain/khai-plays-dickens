# The Chimes: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's Christmas Book _The Chimes_ (1844). A goblin story of bells that rang an old year out, auditing a ticket-porter's lost faith in humanity.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _The Chimes_, published in 1844, one of The Christmas Books in the house catalog (`ccb_002`, [C]). The text is in the public domain.
