# Position: Porter

## Taxonomy

The humble laborers of London: a ticket-porter role.

## Owner

- Project: [The Chimes](play_ccb_002_the_chimes.md)

## Has

A brass ticket-porter's badge, a corner to wait by in the freezing wind, and the expectation of running long errands for pennies.

## Orders

Wait for employment, carry any parcel or message without complaint, and remain humble in the presence of magistrates.

## Loses

The security of a fixed wage, the comfort of shelter, and the standing to argue with those who lecture the poor.

## Drives

The constant running of errands to buy daily bread, carrying the messages of the wealthy while enduring the cold streets.
