# Pitch: Goblin Sermon

## Taxonomy

The tuning of tone for the whole play: a production directive.

## Owner

- Owner: [The Chimes](play_ccb_002_the_chimes.md)

## Tenor

A Victorian storyteller's register of social indignation, observing the mechanical heartlessness of the lecturing classes and the chill of winter streets.

## Undertow

The deep, heavy resonance of the church bells, reminding the poor of their spiritual inheritance when the world tells them they have none.

## Nerve

The reader's sense of social justice, struck by the contrast between comfortable magistrates and the cold wind biting the ticket-porters.

## Echo

A persistent warning against losing faith in humanity and believing that poverty is a crime.
