# Piece: Newspaper

## Taxonomy

The sheets of news: a daily paper.

## Owner

## Place

## Load Bearing

Trotty's despair. Without the reports of crime and the lectures of Alderman Cute in this paper, Trotty's loss of faith in the poor collapses.

## Apparent

A folded sheet of print, damp and cheap, filled with reports of thefts, starvation, and the speeches of comfortable men.
