# Place: Church Tower

## Taxonomy

The structures of the church: a windy tower.

## Owner

- Project: [The Chimes](play_ccb_002_the_chimes.md)

## Shown

A cold, dark, windy church tower, where the wind howls through the stone arches, and the giant bells hang in the shadows.

## Holds

The great bells, the stone steps, and the spirits of the bells that watch over the city.

## Offers

A windy height from which to look down upon the streets, and a home for the voices of warning.

## Withheld

Comfort and shelter, which the stone walls refuse to give to the ticket-porter.
