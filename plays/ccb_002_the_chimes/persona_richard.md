# Persona: Richard

## Taxonomy

The young artisans of London: a working-class persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Chimes](play_ccb_002_the_chimes.md)

## Projection

A handsome, honest young smith, who works hard and hopes to marry Meg.

## Action

Work at his forge, plan for his marriage, and later lose his heart and become a broken drunkard when poverty beats him down.

## Shadow

He cannot withstand the magistrate's lecture that his marriage is a crime against political economy, losing his self-respect and hope.

## Tell

He hangs his head and his shoulders slump when Alderman Cute lectures him.
