# Process: Goblin Audit

## Taxonomy

The moral mechanisms of warning: a spiritual audit.

## Owner

- Project: [The Chimes](play_ccb_002_the_chimes.md)

## Initiated by

Trotty's despair on New Year's Eve, when he declares that the poor are bad by birth and have no right to exist.

## Direction

The progressive demonstration of Meg's ruin in the visions of the future, showing how despair drives the poor to the edge when hope is stripped away.

## Lever

The Goblins' visions of the future meeting Trotty's cries of agony and his eventual reclamation of faith.

## Echo

Trotty's faith in the poor is restored, and he wakes on New Year's Day with his spirit reclaimed.
