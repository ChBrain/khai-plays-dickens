# Persona: Meg

## Taxonomy

The daughters of the poor: a working-class persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Chimes](play_ccb_002_the_chimes.md)

## Projection

A beautiful, bright young woman, full of hope and affection, who loves her father and her fiancé Richard.

## Action

Bring Trotty his dinner of tripe, announce her wedding plans, and later work herself to the bone to support her child.

## Shadow

She is vulnerable to the systematic despair that poverty brings, eventually driven to the edge of the river in the visions.

## Tell

Her eyes fill with tears of joy when she speaks of her wedding, and her hands grow thin from sewing.
