# Persona: Trotty

## Taxonomy

The ticket-porters of London: a working-class persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Chimes](play_ccb_002_the_chimes.md)

## Projection

A weak, small, old man, who waiting for jobs in his [Porter](position_porter.md) position trotted to keep himself warm, representing the humble, patient poor of the city.

## Action

Wait by the church, trot to keep warm, run errands for pennies, and read the news of crime with despair.

## Shadow

He is easily discouraged by the lectures of the wealthy, coming to believe that the poor are born bad and have no right to exist.

## Tell

He shakes his head solemnly and checks his brass badge when the magistrate speaks.
