# Persona: Bounderby

## Taxonomy

The boastful mill owners of Coketown: a boastful capitalist persona.

## Owner

## Projection

Josiah Bounderby of Coketown, a loud, boastful man in his [Millowner](position_millowner.md) position, with a puffed-up face and a habit of bragging about his ditch-born rise.

## Action

Employ Stephen Blackpool, marry Louisa Gradgrind, boast of his wealth, demand Stephen's arrest, and die of a fit in Coketown streets.

## Shadow

His self-made ditch-born story is a complete lie designed to justify his greed, hiding a comfortable upbringing by a loving mother.
