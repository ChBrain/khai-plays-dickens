## Cue

A wet, dark Coketown night, starting outside the bank vault.

## Action

[Tom](persona_tom.md) steals the [Bank Key](piece_bank_key.md) and robs the vault, framing the innocent [Stephen](persona_stephen.md), which drives the desperate [Louisa](persona_louisa.md) to escape Harthouse's advances and collapse before her father.

## Stage

Tom bites his nails as he opens the vault, while Louisa collapses on the floor before her father, exposing the failure of the [Fact Grinding](process_fact_grinding.md) process.

## Tension

Tom's theft is successful, but the emotional collapse of Louisa forces Gradgrind to realize the failure of his system of Facts.
