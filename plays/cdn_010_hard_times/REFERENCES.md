# Hard Times: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's novel _Hard Times: For These Times_ (1854). A drama of industrial labor, staging the conflict between Fact and Fancy, school fact-grinding, and a circus escape.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _Hard Times: For These Times_, published in weekly parts in 1854, one of The Novels in the house catalog (`cdn_010`, [HT]). The text is in the public domain.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **The catalog is the index**: this warrant answers for one staged work; the full Dickens catalogue and its `cdn_010` numbering live in the house register, not here.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
