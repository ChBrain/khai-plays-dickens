# Persona: Gradgrind

## Taxonomy

The fact-obsessed schoolmasters of Coketown: an educator persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Hard Times](play_cdn_010_hard_times.md)

## Projection

Thomas Gradgrind, a square, dry man, with a square forefinger and a bald head, who demands Facts and suppresses all Fancy in his schoolroom.

## Action

Educate his children Louisa and Tom in Facts, adopt Sissy Jupe, arrange Louisa's marriage to Bounderby, and realize the ruin of his system.

## Shadow

His system of Facts is intended for good, but it starves Louisa's heart and makes Tom a selfish, bank-robbing thief.

## Tell

He declares in a dry voice: "Now, what I want is, Facts. Teach these boys and girls nothing but Facts."
