# Position: Millowner

## Taxonomy

The mill owners of Coketown: a mill owner role.

## Owner

- Project: [Hard Times](play_cdn_010_hard_times.md)

## Has

A factory mill, a bank vault, a private carriage, and a mandate to manage labor and accounts.

## Orders

Boast of self-made rise from poverty, impose harsh labor rules on weavers, dismiss workers who complain, and threaten lockouts.

## Loses

The credibility of his boasts when his mother is found, and the control of his household when his wife flees.

## Drives

The maximization of mill production and the suppression of workers' unions through harsh capitalist principles and boastful lies.
