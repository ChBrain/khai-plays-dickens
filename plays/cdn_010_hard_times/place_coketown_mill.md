# Place: Coketown Mill

## Taxonomy

The mills of Coketown: a power-loom mill.

## Owner

- Project: [Hard Times](play_cdn_010_hard_times.md)

## Shown

A large, red-brick mill with tall chimneys pouring out black coal-smoke, vibrating with the motion of steam engines.

## Holds

Power-looms, spinning frames, oil tubs, coal piles, and the weavers' workspace.

## Offers

A grueling, monotonous livelihood of industrial labor for the Coketown weavers.

## Withheld

Fresh air, rest, and green fields, which the smoke-blackened brick walls refuse to offer the workers.
