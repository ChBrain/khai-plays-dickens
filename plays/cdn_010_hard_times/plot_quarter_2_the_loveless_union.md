# Plot: The Loveless Union

## Taxonomy

## Owner

## Cue

A dark, smoky Coketown afternoon, starting inside [Coketown Mill](place_coketown_mill.md).

## Action

[Bounderby](persona_bounderby.md) in his [Millowner](position_millowner.md) position marries the passive [Louisa](persona_louisa.md) to secure [Tom](persona_tom.md)'s bank clerkship, while [Stephen](persona_stephen.md) in his [Weaver](position_weaver.md) position struggles against the harsh mill regulations.

## Stage

Louisa stares silently into the factory fires, while Stephen operates the power-loom and Bounderby barks orders.

## Tension

Louisa's emotional starvation is complete, and the weavers' union agitation increases Stephen's isolation.
