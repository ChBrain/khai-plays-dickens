# Process: Fact Grinding

## Taxonomy

The educational mechanisms of industrial England: a fact grinding.

## Owner

- Project: [Hard Times](play_cdn_010_hard_times.md)

## Initiated by

Mr. Thomas Gradgrind's stone schoolroom, requiring pupils to define horses in terms of quadrupeds and statistics.

## Direction

The progressive emotional starvation of children, showing how erasing fancy and play results in moral confusion and emotional breakdown.

## Lever

Gradgrind's wooden ruler of measurement and school blackboards, meeting the passive submission of Louisa and the selfishness of Tom.

## Echo

Louisa collapses in grief, declaring the failure of her education, while Tom becomes a bank robber, forcing Gradgrind to admit his ruin.
