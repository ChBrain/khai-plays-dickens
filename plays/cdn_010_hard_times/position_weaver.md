# Position: Weaver

## Taxonomy

The power-loom weavers of Coketown: a power-loom weaver role.

## Owner

- Project: [Hard Times](play_cdn_010_hard_times.md)

## Has

A loom key, a power-loom, a modest tenement room, and a mandate to spin cotton.

## Orders

Operate power-looms for twelve hours a day, suffer poverty, refuse to join the union when bound by a promise, and seek work in other towns.

## Loses

His employment when he offends the owner, his union fellowship when he refuses to strike, and his life when he falls into Old Hell Shaft.

## Drives

The honest, silent endurance of industrial labor, struggling against both the owners' greed and the union's pressure.
