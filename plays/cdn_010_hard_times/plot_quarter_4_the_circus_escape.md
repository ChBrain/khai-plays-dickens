# Plot: The Circus Escape

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Hard Times](play_cdn_010_hard_times.md)

## Cue

A bright circus morning, starting in [Sleary Circus](place_sleary_circus.md) and shifting to Coketown streets.

## Action

[Stephen](persona_stephen.md) dies after falling into Old Hell Shaft, while [Sissy](persona_sissy.md) and Sleary execute the [Circus Escape Plan](plan_circus_escape.md) to smuggle the exposed [Tom](persona_tom.md) out of the country, leaving [Bounderby](persona_bounderby.md) to die disgraced.

## Stage

Tom stands disguised as a clown beside the [Circus Peg](piece_circus_peg.md) as the circus horses gallop away, while Bounderby's mother exposes his lies in the street.

## Tension

The tragic death of Stephen is mourned, but Tom's escape represents the triumph of circus Fancy and affection over cold Coketown Facts.
