# Piece: Interest Ledger

## Taxonomy

The ledgers of usury: an account ledger book.

## Owner

- Project: [Nicholas Nickleby](play_cdn_003_nicholas_nickleby.md)

## Place

[Cheeryble Office](place_cheeryble_office.md)

## Load Bearing

Ralph Nickleby's financial operations. Without this interest ledger book representing his usurious loans and debtors' defaults, his usurer position collapses.

## Apparent

A thick, leather-bound ledger, with copperplate entries of loans, dates, and interest rates, resting in Ralph's safe.

## Yearbook

- For years, Ralph has recorded his usurious loans in this book.
- Today, he checks the entries for Madeline Bray's father's debt.
- The ledger falls into the hands of the executors after Ralph's suicide.
