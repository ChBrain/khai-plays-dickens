# Plot: The Retribution

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Nicholas Nickleby](play_cdn_003_nicholas_nickleby.md)

## Cue

A quiet evening in the country, shifting to Golden Square and Yorkshire.

## Action

[Smike](persona_smike.md) dies in the country, leading to the resolution of the [Smike Liberation Plan](plan_smike_liberation.md) when [Nicholas](persona_nicholas.md) confronts [Ralph Nickleby](persona_ralph.md) and Squeers, ending the [Usurious Squeeze](process_usurious_squeeze.md) process.

## Stage

Ralph stands in his dark office learning that Smike was his lost son, before he hangs himself in despair, and [Dotheboys Hall](place_dotheboys_hall.md) is dissolved.

## Tension

Ralph's discovery of Smike's identity destroys his cold calculations, bringing down his financial empire and resolving the family's fortunes.
