# Position: Schoolmaster

## Taxonomy

The masters of academies: a schoolmaster role.

## Owner

- Project: [Nicholas Nickleby](play_cdn_003_nicholas_nickleby.md)

## Has

A boarding school named Dotheboys Hall, a leather cane, a brimstone ladle, and a mandate to collect fees from parents who wish to abandon their children.

## Orders

Beat the pupils, feed them brimstone and treacle to spoil their appetites, write false letters to parents, and pocket the board fees.

## Loses

The capacity for simple humanity, the respect of honest educators, and the standing of a fair guardian of childhood.

## Drives

The extraction of profit from unwanted children, translating the schoolmaster role into starvation and physical abuse.
