# Persona: Smike

## Taxonomy

The neglected pupils of Yorkshire: a crippled runaway persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Nicholas Nickleby](play_cdn_003_nicholas_nickleby.md)

## Projection

A tall, thin youth, with a lame leg and a half-witted, timid face, wearing a child's outgrown coat and broken shoes.

## Action

Suffer under Squeers' cane, escape with Nicholas, work as Crummles' actor, flee Squeers' recaptures, and die peacefully in the country.

## Shadow

He is Ralph Nickleby's abandoned son, though he dies without ever knowing his father, acting as the unwitting lever of Ralph's ruin.

## Tell

He cowers at Squeers' name and looks at Nicholas with dog-like devotion.
