# Nicholas Nickleby: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's novel _The Life and Adventures of Nicholas Nickleby_ (1838). Fortunes of a young gentleman, staging Yorkshire school economy, theatrical diversion, and Ralph's usury.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _The Life and Adventures of Nicholas Nickleby_, published in monthly parts from 1838 to 1839, one of The Novels in the house catalog (`cdn_003`, [NN]). The text is in the public domain.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
