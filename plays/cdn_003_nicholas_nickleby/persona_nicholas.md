# Persona: Nicholas

## Taxonomy

The young gentlemen of England: a fiery young protagonist persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Nicholas Nickleby](play_cdn_003_nicholas_nickleby.md)

## Projection

A handsome, high-spirited young man, with a proud face and dark eyes, who is quick to anger in the face of injustice and oppression.

## Action

Work as Squeers' usher, beat Squeers to rescue Smike, join Vincent Crummles' troupe, seek Cheeryble employment, and defend his sister Kate from usurers.

## Shadow

His fiery temper and pride can lead him into conflicts before he secures the resources to back his declarations.

## Tell

He draws himself up to his full height and speaks with flashing eyes when defending the helpless.
