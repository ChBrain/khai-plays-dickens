# Persona: Ralph Nickleby

## Taxonomy

The moneylenders of London: a cold-hearted usurer persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Nicholas Nickleby](play_cdn_003_nicholas_nickleby.md)

## Projection

A grey-haired man in his [Usurer](position_usurer.md) position, with a cold, wrinkled face and dark, calculating eyes, who treats all human relationships as financial accounts.

## Action

Exploit his nephew Nicholas, use his sister Kate to please wealthy debtors, discount Bray's bills, and hang himself in despair.

## Shadow

He thinks he is financially secure and above family obligations, before discovering that the boy Smike he persecuted was his own abandoned son.

## Tell

He counts his gold coins and locks his iron safe with a heavy key.
