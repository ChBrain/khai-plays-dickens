# Position: Usurer

## Taxonomy

The moneylenders of London: a usurer role.

## Owner

- Project: [Nicholas Nickleby](play_cdn_003_nicholas_nickleby.md)

## Has

A dark office in Golden Square, account ledgers, discounted bills, and a network of wealthy clients and desperate debtors.

## Orders

Lend money at high interest rates, discount bills for desperate clients, exploit family members to please wealthy creditors, and foreclose on defaults.

## Loses

The right to family love, the peace of mind undisturbed by debt, and the respect of honest merchants.

## Drives

The accumulation of gold through usurious calculations, turning family ties and client distress into financial leverage.
