# Persona: Crummles

## Taxonomy

The theatrical managers of England: an provincial actor-manager persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Nicholas Nickleby](play_cdn_003_nicholas_nickleby.md)

## Projection

Vincent Crummles, a stout, pompous, but warm-hearted man, who walks with a theatrical stride and has a booming, stage-trained voice.

## Action

Manage the Portsmouth theatrical troupe, recruit Nicholas and Smike, perform sensational dramas, and eventually emigrate to America.

## Shadow

His constant search for theatrical sensation and eccentric novelties hides a genuine, warm kindness that offers shelter to the runaways.

## Tell

He embraces Nicholas theatrically and refers to Smike as: "a split-infant of tragedy."
