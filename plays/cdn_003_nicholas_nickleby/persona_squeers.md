# Persona: Squeers

## Taxonomy

The schoolmasters of Yorkshire: a cruel one-eyed tyrant persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Nicholas Nickleby](play_cdn_003_nicholas_nickleby.md)

## Projection

A one-eyed, harsh-featured man in his [Schoolmaster](position_schoolmaster.md) position, with a greasy coat and a squeaking voice, representing the Yorkshire school trade.

## Action

Run Dotheboys Hall, administer brimstone, beat Smike, conspire with Ralph to kidnap Smike, and face transportation.

## Shadow

His talk of morals and education hides a pure financial exploitation of unwanted children, whom he starves to save board costs.

## Tell

He taps his leather cane against his boot and squints his single eye when threatening a boy.
