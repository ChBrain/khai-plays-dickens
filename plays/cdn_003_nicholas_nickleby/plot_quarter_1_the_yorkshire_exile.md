# Plot: The Yorkshire Exile

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Nicholas Nickleby](play_cdn_003_nicholas_nickleby.md)

## Cue

A freezing winter morning in Yorkshire, starting in [Dotheboys Hall](place_dotheboys_hall.md).

## Action

[Nicholas](persona_nicholas.md) serves as usher under [Squeers](persona_squeers.md) in his [Schoolmaster](position_schoolmaster.md) position, witnessing the abuse of [Smike](persona_smike.md) under the key of the [Theatrical Fortune Pitch](pitch_theatrical_fortune.md).

## Stage

Squeers administers brimstone and treacle to the boys with the [Brimstone Spoon](piece_brimstone_spoon.md), before Nicholas rebels, beats Squeers, and flees with Smike.

## Tension

Nicholas's fiery temper is pushed to the limit by Squeers' cruelty, leading to a violent rebellion that makes them runaways.
