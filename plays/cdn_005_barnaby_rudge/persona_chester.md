# Persona: Sir John Chester

## Taxonomy

The polished gentlemen of England: a manipulative aristocrat persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Barnaby Rudge](play_cdn_005_barnaby_rudge.md)

## Projection

An elegant, middle-aged gentleman, dressed in the height of fashion, with a smooth, unwrinkled face and a soft, polite voice.

## Action

Scheme to prevent his son Edward's marriage, manipulate Lord George Gordon behind the scenes, and meet his death in a duel with Haredale.

## Shadow

His polished, polite behavior hides a cold, absolute selfishness, using religious riots to destroy his political enemies.

## Tell

He reads Lord Chesterfield's letters, smiles smoothly, and taps his snuff-box.
