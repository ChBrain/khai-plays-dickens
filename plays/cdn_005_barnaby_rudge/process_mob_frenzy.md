# Process: Mob Frenzy

## Taxonomy

The social mechanisms of fanaticism: a mob frenzy.

## Owner

- Project: [Barnaby Rudge](play_cdn_005_barnaby_rudge.md)

## Initiated by

Lord George Gordon's petition and Gashford's anti-Catholic propaganda, inciting London Protestant mobs to protest.

## Direction

The progressive expansion of riots from street protests to arson attacks on Catholic homes, churches, and London prisons.

## Lever

The coordination of mob leaders Sim Tappertit, Hugh, and Dennis, meeting the civic resistance of Gabriel Varden.

## Echo

The military suppresses the riots, the leaders are arrested and sentenced to the gallows, and Barnaby is pardoned.
