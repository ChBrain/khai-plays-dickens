# Persona: Barnaby

## Taxonomy

The simple-minded heroes of England: a simple protagonist persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Barnaby Rudge](play_cdn_005_barnaby_rudge.md)

## Projection

A young man of wild, wandering eyes, dressed in tattered clothes decorated with peacock feathers, carrying a large wicker cage on his back.

## Action

Wander the Chigwell woods, talk to his raven Grip, flee to London, join the rioters for glory, and escape the gallows by a last-minute pardon.

## Shadow

His lack of understanding makes him a passive tool for the rioters, who use his strength and courage to guard their outposts.

## Tell

He laughs wildly, flaps his arms like a bird, and points to his raven Grip.
