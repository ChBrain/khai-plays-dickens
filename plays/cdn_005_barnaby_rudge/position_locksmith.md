# Position: Locksmith

## Taxonomy

The craftsmen of London: a locksmith role.

## Owner

- Project: [Barnaby Rudge](play_cdn_005_barnaby_rudge.md)

## Has

An iron workshop, anvils and hammers, keys of London houses and prisons, and a mandate to defend civic order and pick locks.

## Orders

Forge iron keys, repair locks for the city, refuse to pick locks for rioters, and defend the house from apprentice mutineers.

## Loses

The right to live without civic danger, the peace of his domestic home, and the obedience of his vanity-filled apprentice.

## Drives

The defense of locksmith integrity and civic peace, maintaining the strength of iron locks against mob destruction.
