# Pitch: Gordon Frenzy

## Taxonomy

The tuning of tone for the whole play: a production directive.

## Owner

- Owner: [Barnaby Rudge](play_cdn_005_barnaby_rudge.md)

## Tenor

A Victorian historical register, contrasting the cozy country Maypole Inn and locksmith's domestic peace with the wild, chaotic roar of the London Protestant rioters.

## Undertow

The repetitive, metallic croaking of Grip the raven and the heavy clanking of locksmith keys, echoing through the burning streets of Newgate.

## Nerve

The reader's dread of the simple Barnaby being led to the gallows by the mob's frenzy, and the threat of Sim's mutinous apprentices.

## Echo

A persistent assurance that locksmith integrity, domestic loyalty, and simple sanity are the true defense against political and religious fanaticism.
