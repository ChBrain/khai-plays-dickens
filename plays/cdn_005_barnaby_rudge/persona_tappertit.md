# Persona: Sim Tappertit

## Taxonomy

The apprentices of London: a vain locksmith apprentice persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Barnaby Rudge](play_cdn_005_barnaby_rudge.md)

## Projection

Simon Tappertit, a small-statured apprentice with thin, legs, a large nose, and an immense belief in his own personal beauty and power.

## Action

Work under Gabriel Varden, organize the 'Prentice Knights mutiny, lead the rioters during the sackings, and lose his legs in the riots.

## Shadow

His vanity makes him easily manipulated by rioters, using the 'Prentice Knights to rebel against master authority.

## Tell

He squints his eyes, admires his legs, and declares: "Apprentice blood will have its day!"
