# Persona: Hugh

## Taxonomy

The hostlers of Chigwell: a wild hostler persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Barnaby Rudge](play_cdn_005_barnaby_rudge.md)

## Projection

A wild, muscular young man, with long, tangled black hair and a dark, sunburnt face, who serves as hostler at the Maypole.

## Action

Tend the horses, join the mob in his [Rioter](position_rioter.md) position under Chester's orders, lead the mob to burn the Warren, and die on the gallows.

## Shadow

He is Sir John Chester's abandoned natural son, operating as his father's brute tool without ever knowing their relationship.

## Tell

He laughs savagely, cracks his whip, and boasts of his horseman's strength.
