## Cue

A hot summer night in London, shifting to the gates of [The Warren](place_warren.md).

## Action

The mob executes the [Mob Frenzy](process_mob_frenzy.md) process, leading [Hugh](persona_hugh.md) and [Sim Tappertit](persona_tappertit.md) in their [Rioter](position_rioter.md) position to burn the estate, while [Gabriel Varden](persona_varden.md) in his [Locksmith](position_locksmith.md) position refuses to pick the prison lock.

## Stage

Hugh carries the blue [Gordon Flag](piece_gordon_flag.md) during the sacking, while Varden refuses to surrender the [Locksmith Key](piece_varden_key.md) at the Newgate Prison gate.

## Tension

[Barnaby](persona_barnaby.md) is caught guarding the mob's flag, while Varden's locksmith integrity resists all threats, and the city is consumed by fire.
