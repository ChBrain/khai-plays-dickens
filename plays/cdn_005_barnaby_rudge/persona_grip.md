# Persona: Grip

## Taxonomy

The talking ravens of England: a bird companion persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Barnaby Rudge](play_cdn_005_barnaby_rudge.md)

## Projection

A large, sleek black raven, with knowing, bead-like eyes, who rests on Barnaby's shoulder or in his wicker cage.

## Action

Accompany Barnaby in his travels, croak out phrases, hop on the tavern tables, and survive the Gordon Riots.

## Shadow

Its croaking phrases represent the repetitive, mindless slogans of the human rioters, acting as a comic commentary on religious frenzy.

## Tell

It cocks its head, taps its beak on Barnaby's shoulder, and croaks: "Polly put the kettle on, we'll all have tea."
