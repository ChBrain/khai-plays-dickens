# Barnaby Rudge: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's novel _Barnaby Rudge: A Tale of the Riots of Eighty_ (1841). Gordon Riots of Eighty, staging locksmith duty, apprentice mutiny, and mob frenzy.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _Barnaby Rudge: A Tale of the Riots of Eighty_, published in weekly parts in 1841, one of The Novels in the house catalog (`cdn_005`, [BR]). The text is in the public domain.
