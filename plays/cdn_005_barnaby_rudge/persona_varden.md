# Persona: Gabriel Varden

## Taxonomy

The locksmiths of London: an honest master craftsman persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Barnaby Rudge](play_cdn_005_barnaby_rudge.md)

## Projection

A stout, hearty man in his [Locksmith](position_locksmith.md) position, with a red, cheerful face and a leather apron, representing civic locksmith integrity.

## Action

Forge iron keys, refuse to pick the Newgate locks for the rioters, defend his home, and secure Barnaby's pardon.

## Shadow

His honest nature is the exact opposite of Sim Tappertit's apprentice mutiny, standing firm against mob threats.

## Tell

He chuckles heartily, hammers on his anvil, and shakes his head over his wife's Protestant pamphlets.
