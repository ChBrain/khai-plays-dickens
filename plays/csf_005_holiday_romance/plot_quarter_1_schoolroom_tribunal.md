# Plot: Schoolroom Tribunal

## Taxonomy

## Owner

## Cue

A secret schoolroom in London at dusk, starting inside [place_schoolroom_tribunal.md](place_schoolroom_tribunal.md).

## Action

The children in their [Child Romancer](position_child_romancer.md) position execute the [Childish Play](process_childish_play.md) process in [place_schoolroom_tribunal.md](place_schoolroom_tribunal.md) to hold a mock trial judging the dry, boring parents and teachers under the [Holiday Romance Pitch](pitch_holiday_romance.md).

## Stage

The children sit at school desks as a jury, while a child judge sits in the teacher's high chair and passes mock judgments on parents.

## Tension

The dry authority of the schoolroom is subverted by the playful solemnity of the children's court.
