# Persona: Grandmarina

## Taxonomy

The majestic fairies of fairy land: a fairy godmother persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Holiday Romance](play_csf_005_holiday_romance.md)

## Projection

Fairy Grandmarina, a grand, majestic, sharp-tongued fairy godmother who arrives in a coach drawn by peacocks.

## Action

Visit the fishmonger, give the magic fishbone to Watkins, lecture Watkins on self-help, and organize Alicia's wedding.

## Shadow

Her sharp lectures and demands for self-help cover her deep care for Alicia, using magic only when Alicia has exhausted all human efforts.

## Tell

She waves her fan, declaring: "Those who do not help themselves, Watkins, do not deserve fairy help!"
