# Position: Child Romancer

## Taxonomy

The young storytellers of the schoolroom: a child romancer role.

## Owner

- Project: [Holiday Romance](play_csf_005_holiday_romance.md)

## Has

A pocket notebook, a pencil stub, a wooden toy sword, and a mandate to write romantic stories.

## Orders

Write stories criticizing grown-ups, conduct mock trials in the schoolroom, play pirate adventures, and marry schoolroom partners.

## Loses

His boredom, when he leads his schoolroom friends in a mock trial to punish the unsympathetic grown-ups.

## Drives

The subversion of adult authority and the validation of childhood imagination.
