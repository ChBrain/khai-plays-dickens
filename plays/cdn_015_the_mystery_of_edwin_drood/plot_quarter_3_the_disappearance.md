# Plot: The Disappearance

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [The Mystery of Edwin Drood](play_cdn_015_the_mystery_of_edwin_drood.md)

## Cue

A cold, winter morning at the cathedral gate.

## Action

On Christmas morning, [Edwin Drood](persona_edwin_drood.md) is missing, prompting [Jasper](persona_jasper.md) to accuse [Neville Landless](persona_neville_landless.md) of murder in [place_cloisterham_cathedral.md](place_cloisterham_cathedral.md), while Neville is arrested and Jasper declares his love and threats to the terrified [Rosa Bud](persona_rosa_bud.md).

## Stage

Neville is escorted by guards, while Jasper confronts Rosa Bud by the sundial and the gold-and-ruby [Drood Ring](piece_drood_ring.md) remains hidden in the tomb.

## Tension

Edwin is gone and Neville is ruined, while Rosa's terror forces her to seek immediate escape.
