# Persona: Datchery

## Taxonomy

The secret observers of Cloisterham: a secret inquirer persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Mystery of Edwin Drood](play_cdn_015_the_mystery_of_edwin_drood.md)

## Projection

Dick Datchery, a mysterious gentleman with a huge head of white hair in his [Inquirer](position_inquirer.md) position, who behaves with dry, formal politeness.

## Action

Arrive in Cloisterham, rent a lodging near Jasper, watch Jasper's movements, and score chalk marks behind his cupboard door.

## Shadow

His false identity and white wig hide a secret inquirer, whose quiet investigations threaten to expose Jasper's double life.

## Tell

He looks at his cupboard door, marking a thick white chalk line to record his discoveries.
