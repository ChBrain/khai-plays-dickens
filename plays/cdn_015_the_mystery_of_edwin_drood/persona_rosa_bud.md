# Persona: Rosa Bud

## Taxonomy

The beautiful orphans of Cloisterham: an orphan heroine persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Mystery of Edwin Drood](play_cdn_015_the_mystery_of_edwin_drood.md)

## Projection

Rosa Bud (Rosebud), a beautiful, sweet young music student at the Nuns' House, whose charming face reflects a deep fear of John Jasper.

## Action

Study music, break off her engagement with Edwin, flee to London, and seek Mr. Grewgious's protection.

## Shadow

Her sweet, childish exterior hides a sharp intuition, recognizing Jasper's dark obsession and fleeing his threats to save herself.

## Tell

She shrinks away when Jasper approaches the piano, looking at him with terrified eyes.
