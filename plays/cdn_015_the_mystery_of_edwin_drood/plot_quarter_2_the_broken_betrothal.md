# Plot: The Broken Betrothal

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [The Mystery of Edwin Drood](play_cdn_015_the_mystery_of_edwin_drood.md)

## Cue

A quiet, cold dusk in the cathedral close, shifting to a stormy Christmas Eve night.

## Action

[Edwin Drood](persona_edwin_drood.md) and [Rosa Bud](persona_rosa_bud.md) break off their engagement, while [Jasper](persona_jasper.md) executes the [Crypt Reconnaissance Plan](plan_crypt_reconnaissance.md) in [place_cloisterham_cathedral.md](place_cloisterham_cathedral.md) and hosts a dinner to reconcile Edwin and [Neville Landless](persona_neville_landless.md) before the Christmas storm.

## Stage

Edwin and Rosa walk in the cathedral garden, while Jasper steals the [Cathedral Key](piece_cathedral_key.md) to explore the crypts, and the Christmas wind tears the hands off the cathedral clock.

## Tension

The betrothal is ended, but Jasper continues his preparations, and the dinner sets the stage for the disappearance.
