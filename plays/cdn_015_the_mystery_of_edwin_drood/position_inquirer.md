# Position: Inquirer

## Taxonomy

The secret observers of Cloisterham: a secret inquirer role.

## Owner

- Project: [The Mystery of Edwin Drood](play_cdn_015_the_mystery_of_edwin_drood.md)

## Has

A white wig, a rented lodging, a chalk slate, and a mandate to investigate suspects.

## Orders

Rent rooms near suspects, adopt a false identity, watch John Jasper's movements, and mark chalk scores to record discoveries.

## Loses

The comfort of his true identity, but never his patience or his silent, systematic watch.

## Drives

The silent, systematic collection of evidence to expose the truth behind the disappearance of Edwin Drood.
