# Pitch: Drood Shadows

## Taxonomy

The tuning of tone for the whole play: a production directive.

## Owner

- Owner: [The Mystery of Edwin Drood](play_cdn_015_the_mystery_of_edwin_drood.md)

## Tenor

A solemn, shadowy atmosphere of a Cathedral town: the cold dust of medieval vaults, the sweet, heavy fumes of London opium dens, the howling of a Christmas storm, and the sharp clicking of heels on stone pathways.

## Undertow

The low chanting of cathedral choir hymns, the bubbling of the opium pipe, the scratching of stone-mason tools in the crypts, and the quiet scoring of chalk marks behind a door.

## Nerve

The terror of John Jasper's silent, drug-fueled obsession and the mystery of Edwin Drood's sudden Christmas Eve disappearance.

## Echo

A persistent warning that respectability can mask a dark, double life, and that the shadows of the past will eventually be tracked down by silent inquiry.
