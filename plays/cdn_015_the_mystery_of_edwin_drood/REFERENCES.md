# The Mystery of Edwin Drood: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's novel _The Mystery of Edwin Drood_ (1870). A drama of mystery and double lives, staging cathedral town shadows, John Jasper's drug-dreams, and Datchery's inquiry.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _The Mystery of Edwin Drood_, published in monthly parts in 1870; unfinished, one of The Novels in the house catalog (`cdn_015`, [MED]). The text is in the public domain.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
