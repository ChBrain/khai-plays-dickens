# Persona: Edwin Drood

## Taxonomy

## Owner

Edwin Drood, a handsome, light-hearted young engineering student, betrothed to Rosa Bud, who is unaware of the dark shadows around him.

## Shadow

His light-hearted, naive nature makes him blind to his uncle Jasper's dark obsession, leading him into a fatal trap.
