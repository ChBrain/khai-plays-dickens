# Persona: Brokers Man

## Taxonomy

The debt collectors of London: a broker's assistant persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Sketches by Boz](play_csf_001_sketches_by_boz.md)

## Projection

Albert, a quiet, poor broker's man who is placed in houses to guard seized goods until debts are paid.

## Action

Sit in kitchens, watch family possessions, carry pawn tickets, and quietly witness the ruin of families.

## Shadow

His quiet, passive role hides his own poverty and shame, performing a cruel task to earn a small daily wage.

## Tell

He sits quietly in a corner, looking at a small pawn ticket in his hand.
