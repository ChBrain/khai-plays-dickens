# Position: Beadle

## Taxonomy

The parish officials of London: a parish beadle role.

## Owner

- Project: [Sketches by Boz](play_csf_001_sketches_by_boz.md)

## Has

A gold-laced cocked hat, a brass-headed cane, a parish vestry office, and a mandate to enforce local order.

## Orders

Maintain order in the churchyard, announce vestry elections, visit widows, and manage local parish charity distributions.

## Loses

His dignity, when the parish boys tease him or when vestry politics collapse into petty arguments.

## Drives

The preservation of his own pompous dignity and authority within the parish boundaries.
