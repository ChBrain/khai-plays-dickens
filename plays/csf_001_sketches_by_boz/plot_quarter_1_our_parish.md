# Plot: Our Parish

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

## Cue

A quiet, dry vestry room in a London parish, starting inside [place_parish_vestry.md](place_parish_vestry.md).

## Action

[Beadle](persona_beadle.md) in his [Beadle Position](position_beadle.md) position announces the local parish vestry elections, wearing the [Cocked Hat](piece_cocked_hat.md) and managing local parochial charity while [Boz](persona_boz.md) records the events in [place_london_streets.md](place_london_streets.md) under the [Boz Sketches Pitch](pitch_boz_sketches.md).

## Stage

The Beadle Mr. Bung announces the parish election candidates, while vestrymen debate and local boys tease the Beadle in the churchyard.

## Tension

The Beadle tries to preserve his pompous dignity while parish politics collapse into petty arguments.
