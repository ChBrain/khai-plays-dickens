# Persona: Tibbs

## Taxonomy

The boarding-house keepers of London: a boarding-house keeper persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Sketches by Boz](play_csf_001_sketches_by_boz.md)

## Projection

Mrs. Tibbs, a busy, respectable boarding-house keeper in Great Coram Street, who manages her house with constant energy.

## Action

Prepare meals, assign rooms, ring the house bell, receive new lodgers, and try to resolve resident conflicts.

## Shadow

Her respectable, busy business is a response to her husband's laziness, forcing her to run the boarding-house to avoid debt.

## Tell

She rings the dinner bell, complaining of the price of beef and the laziness of her servant.
