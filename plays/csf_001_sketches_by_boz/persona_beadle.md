# Persona: Beadle

## Taxonomy

The parish officials of London: a beadle persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Sketches by Boz](play_csf_001_sketches_by_boz.md)

## Projection

Mr. Bung, a pompous, stout parish beadle in his [Beadle Position](position_beadle.md) position, whose gold-laced cocked hat represents local vestry power.

## Action

Announce vestry elections, visit local widows, carry the parish mace, and argue with parochial candidates.

## Shadow

His pompous, self-important manner hides a fear of being mocked by the parish boys, making him defend his cocked hat with constant pride.

## Tell

He taps his brass-headed cane on the stones, adjusting his gold-laced cocked hat.
