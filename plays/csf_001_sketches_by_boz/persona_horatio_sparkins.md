# Persona: Horatio Sparkins

## Taxonomy

The social climbers of London: a fraudulent gentleman persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Sketches by Boz](play_csf_001_sketches_by_boz.md)

## Projection

Horatio Sparkins, a fashionable, mysterious young gentleman who quotes poetry and dresses in the height of fashion.

## Action

Attend suburban parties, quote Byron, charm the Malderton family, and work secretly as a common clerk in a London linen-draper's shop.

## Shadow

His fashionable, poetic identity is a fraudulent mask to hide his low employment, which collapses when he is spotted in the shop.

## Tell

He quotes a line of poetry, passing his hand through his curled hair with a mysterious sigh.
