# Plot: Middle Class Tales

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Sketches by Boz](play_csf_001_sketches_by_boz.md)

## Cue

A middle-class drawing room in a London suburb and a silent room in debt.

## Action

[Horatio_Sparkins](persona_horatio_sparkins.md) executes his [Social Climbing Plan](plan_social_climbing.md) to deceive families, while [Boz](persona_boz.md) witnesses the exposure of Sparkins's true identity, and the poor [Brokers Man](persona_brokers_man.md) Albert guards seized goods in a ruined home using his [Pawn Ticket](piece_pawn_ticket.md).

## Stage

Sparkins quotes poetry at a party, while Albert sits in a silent kitchen with his pawn ticket, and families face the reality of financial ruin.

## Tension

Middle-class social ambitions collapse into comic exposure, while the poor face the silent tragedy of debt.
