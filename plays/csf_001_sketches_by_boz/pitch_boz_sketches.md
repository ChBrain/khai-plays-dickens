# Pitch: Boz Sketches

## Taxonomy

The tuning of tone for the whole play: a production directive.

## Owner

- Owner: [Sketches by Boz](play_csf_001_sketches_by_boz.md)

## Tenor

A bustling, clamorous London street atmosphere: the damp coats and steam of passengers inside stagecoaches, the bright gas-lamps of gin-shops, the dry vestry dust of parish halls, and the noisy chattering of Vauxhall crowds.

## Undertow

The rattling of carriage wheels on uneven cobblestones, the calling of street sellers, the quiet scratch of a pencil in a notebook, and the low gossip of boarding-house tables.

## Nerve

The comic energy of Boz's sharp observation of character and the quiet tragedy of London poverty behind respectable doors.

## Echo

A persistent warning that social respectability is often a thin mask for vanity, and that the true pulse of the city beats in its humblest streets.
