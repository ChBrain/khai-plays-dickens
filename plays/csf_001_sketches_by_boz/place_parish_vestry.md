# Place: Parish Vestry

## Taxonomy

The parish rooms of London: a vestry room.

## Owner

- Project: [Sketches by Boz](play_csf_001_sketches_by_boz.md)

## Shown

A dry, respectable parish hall with wooden tables, official ledgers, and maps of local boundaries.

## Holds

Parish ledgers, ballot boxes, vestry desks, and Mr. Bung's cocked hat.

## Offers

A quiet sanctuary for administrative business and beadle election debates.

## Withheld

Modern, democratic progress, which the conservative parish vestry refuses to allow.
