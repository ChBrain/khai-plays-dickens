# Persona: Cochran

## Taxonomy

The boarding-house residents of London: an eccentric lodger persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Sketches by Boz](play_csf_001_sketches_by_boz.md)

## Projection

Mr. Cochran, an eccentric boarding-house resident at Mrs. Tibbs's, who behaves with extreme politeness and minor vanity.

## Action

Attend the boarding-house dinners, converse with residents, argue over politics, and participate in Mrs. Tibbs's drawing-room gatherings.

## Shadow

His polite, grand manner is a cover for his small, lonely life, relying on Mrs. Tibbs's parlor for social contact.

## Tell

He bows formally, declaring: "It is a most remarkable day, indeed."
