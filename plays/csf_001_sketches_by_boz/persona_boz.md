# Persona: Boz

## Taxonomy

The observers of London streets: a reporter persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Sketches by Boz](play_csf_001_sketches_by_boz.md)

## Projection

Boz, a young, observant street reporter in his [Reporter](position_reporter.md) position, whose sharp eyes watch the moving crowds of London.

## Action

Walk the streets, write sketches in his pocket notebook, describe coach passengers, and observe boarding-house conflicts.

## Shadow

His constant search for humor in the streets is a response to the city's dark poverty, which he records with quiet sympathy.

## Tell

He opens his pocket notebook, writing rapidly as he watches passengers on the omnibus.
