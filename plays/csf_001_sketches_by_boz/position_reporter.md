# Position: Reporter

## Taxonomy

The street reporters of London: a street reporter role.

## Owner

- Project: [Sketches by Boz](play_csf_001_sketches_by_boz.md)

## Has

A pocket notebook, a pencil, walking shoes, and a mandate to observe and record London life.

## Orders

Walk the busy streets of London, watch passenger behaviors inside coaches, describe current fashions, and record character dialogues.

## Loses

His detachment, when he is moved by the quiet tragedies of poor families in pawn-shops.

## Drives

The relentless, sympathetic pursuit of truth in the ordinary lives of Londoners.
