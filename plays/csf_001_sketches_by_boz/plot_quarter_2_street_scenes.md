# Plot: Street Scenes

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Sketches by Boz](play_csf_001_sketches_by_boz.md)

## Cue

A busy, noisy London street corner, shifting to the bright gas-lamps of a gin-shop.

## Action

[Boz](persona_boz.md) in his [Reporter](position_reporter.md) position executes the [Street Observation](process_street_observation.md) process in [place_london_streets.md](place_london_streets.md), recording stagecoach passenger behaviors, visiting Vauxhall Gardens, and documenting London life with his [Reporters Notebook](piece_reporters_notebook.md).

## Stage

An omnibus rattles over the cobblestones, while Boz watches passengers struggle with damp coats, and families enter a bright, noisy gin-shop.

## Tension

The chaotic social energy of the busy streets is balanced against the quiet poverty Boz observes.
