# Process: Street Observation

## Taxonomy

The systematic observation of London street life: a street observation process.

## Owner

- Project: [Sketches by Boz](play_csf_001_sketches_by_boz.md)

## Initiated by

Boz's walks through London, where he observes scenes and records them in his notebook.

## Direction

The progressive description of streets, coaches, shops, and crowds, showing how the street scenes are collected.

## Lever

Boz's reporters notebook and walking shoes, meeting Mr. Bung's announcements and boarding-house gossip.

## Echo

Boz publishes sketches of these scenes, which record the social energy and poverty of Victorian London.
