# Sketches by Boz: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's shorter work _Sketches by Boz_ (1836). A panoramic observation of London street life, parish beadle charity, and middle-class boarding houses.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _Sketches by Boz_, published in 1836, one of Shorter Fiction and Sketches in the house catalog (`csf_001`, [SB]). The text is in the public domain.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **The catalog is the index**: this warrant answers for one staged work; the full Dickens catalogue and its `csf_001` numbering live in the house register, not here.
