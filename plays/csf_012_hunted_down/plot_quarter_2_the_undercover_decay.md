# Plot: The Undercover Decay

## Taxonomy

The fytte of the actuary's disguise: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Hunted Down](play_csf_012_hunted_down.md)

## Cue

A dingy street corner in London, shifting to the dark rooms of Gravel Lane.

## Action

[Alfred Meltham](persona_meltham.md) disappears from the insurance world and adopts the alias of Alfred Beckwith to launch the [Undercover Trap Plan](plan_undercover_trap.md), establishing himself in the cluttered parlour of [Gravel Lane](place_gravel_lane.md).

## Stage

[Alfred Meltham](persona_meltham.md) feigns a public collapse of health and reputation, drinking heavily at the local taverns to lure [Julius Slinkton](persona_slinkton.md) into making contact, while [Mr. Sampson](persona_sampson.md) coordinates from the office.

## Tension

Meltham's realistic performance of drunken ruin is so convincing that observers believe he is truly destroying himself, raising the stakes of his survival.
