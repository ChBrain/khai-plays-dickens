# Persona: Alfred Meltham

## Taxonomy

The undercover avengers of fraud: a grieving, determined insurance actuary persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Hunted Down](play_csf_012_hunted_down.md)

## Projection

Alfred Meltham, a brilliant young actuary driven by grief over a past victim of Slinkton, who adopts the alias of Alfred Beckwith to serve as bait in the trap.

## Action

Disguise himself as a degraded drunkard, rent rooms in Gravel Lane, drink the poisoned brandy to trick Slinkton, and confront the murderer.

## Shadow

His obsessive pursuit of revenge consumes his health and spirit, leading to his own early death shortly after the case concludes.

## Tell

He feigns a shaky hand and slurred speech when in character as Beckwith, but reveals a cold, sharp gaze when Slinkton is trapped.
