# Plan: Undercover Trap Plan

## Taxonomy

The design of a counter-plot to trap a poisoner: an undercover trap plan.

## Owner

- Owner: [Alfred Meltham](persona_meltham.md)

## Direction

To expose Julius Slinkton's murderous pattern of insurance fraud and secure justice by using Meltham as bait.

## Orders

Meltham is ordered to adopt the alias of Alfred Beckwith, rent lodgings in Gravel Lane, feign severe physical decay and heavy drinking, and allow Slinkton to believe he is successfully poisoning him.

## Implementation

The Gravel Lane lodgings, the bottle of brandy laced with poison, and coordination with Sampson at the Life Assurance Office.

## Targets

- [ ] Meltham disappears from public life and adopts the identity of Alfred Beckwith
- [ ] Beckwith rents lodgings in Gravel Lane and performs a public show of physical decline
- [ ] Beckwith accepts Slinkton's false friendship and allows him to administer the poisoned brandy
- [ ] Sampson and Meltham confront Slinkton in the Gravel Lane parlour when the trap is sprung
