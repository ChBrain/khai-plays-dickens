# Persona: Julius Slinkton

## Taxonomy

The calculating insurance murderers: a suave, hypocritical poisoner persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Hunted Down](play_csf_012_hunted_down.md)

## Projection

Julius Slinkton, a murderer who presents himself as a devoted uncle and social moralist, operating in his [Poisoner](position_poisoner.md) position to secure fortunes by poisoning his relatives.

## Action

Propose fraudulent insurance policies, visit Gravel Lane to slowly administer poison, and attempt to insure his niece Margaret Niner before being confronted.

## Shadow

His vanity and belief in his own intellectual superiority make him blind to the trap being laid around him by the insurance office.

## Tell

He smooths his whiskers and speaks in a soft, moralizing tone while calculating the financial value of a target's life.
