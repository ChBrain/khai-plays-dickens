# Plot: The Suspicious Proposal

## Taxonomy

The fytte of the insurance proposal: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Hunted Down](play_csf_012_hunted_down.md)

## Cue

A respectable morning in London, inside the offices of the Life Assurance Office.

## Action

[Julius Slinkton](persona_slinkton.md) in his [Poisoner](position_poisoner.md) position visits the [Life Assurance Office](place_assurance_office.md) to propose a [Life Assurance Policy](piece_assurance_policy.md) on his niece, drawing the immediate suspicion of [Mr. Sampson](persona_sampson.md) in his [Insurer](position_insurer.md) position, who initiates the [Insurance Audit](process_insurance_audit.md) under the direction of the [Insurance Audit Pitch](pitch_insurance_audit.md).

## Stage

[Mr. Sampson](persona_sampson.md) watches from his desk as [Julius Slinkton](persona_slinkton.md) smooths his whiskers and presents the false paperwork, while the innocent [Margaret Niner](persona_margaret.md) waits quietly in the hall.

## Tension

The contrast between Slinkton's moralizing language and the cold calculations of the insurance office creates a silent, suspicious atmosphere.
