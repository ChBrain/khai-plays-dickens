# Persona: Margaret Niner

## Taxonomy

The targets of calculating avarice: an innocent, endangered niece persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Hunted Down](play_csf_012_hunted_down.md)

## Projection

Margaret Niner, the innocent niece of Julius Slinkton, who is kept in dependency and targeted for insurance fraud.

## Action

Accompany Slinkton to the Assurance Office, rely on his false care, and receive warnings from Sampson before being rescued.

## Shadow

Her initial loyalty to her uncle makes it difficult for her to see the danger she is in until the conspiracy is fully exposed.

## Tell

She moves with a fragile, nervous grace and looks to Slinkton for permission before speaking.
