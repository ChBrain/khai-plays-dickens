# Hunted Down: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's shorter work _Hunted Down_ (1859). A drama of insurance fraud, staging calculating poisoner Julius Slinkton audited by an insurance counter-trap.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _Hunted Down_, published in 1859, one of Shorter Fiction and Sketches in the house catalog (`csf_012`, [HD]). The text is in the public domain.
