# Plot: The Trap Sprung

## Taxonomy

The fytte of the final confrontation: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Hunted Down](play_csf_012_hunted_down.md)

## Cue

A cold morning inside the Gravel Lane lodgings.

## Action

[Mr. Sampson](persona_sampson.md) and the healthy [Alfred Meltham](persona_meltham.md) spring the [Undercover Trap Plan](plan_undercover_trap.md) by confronting [Julius Slinkton](persona_slinkton.md) with his crimes and the unsigned [Life Assurance Policy](piece_assurance_policy.md) on Margaret's life, ending the poisoner's career.

## Stage

[Alfred Meltham](persona_meltham.md) stands up fully recovered and pours the remains of the [Poisoned Brandy](piece_poisoned_brandy.md) onto the floor, while [Julius Slinkton](persona_slinkton.md), realizing his ruin and the arrest that awaits him, swallows his own poison.

## Tension

The dramatic reversal as the hunter is hunted down, exposing Slinkton's shock and ultimate self-destruction.
