# Position: Poisoner

## Taxonomy

The perpetrators of fraudulent murder: a poisoner role.

## Owner

- Project: [Hunted Down](play_csf_012_hunted_down.md)

## Has

A supply of slow poison, life assurance policies, a suave social reputation, and a calculating mind.

## Orders

Select wealthy or insurable relatives, secure policies on their lives, slowly administer poison, and claim the payouts.

## Loses

His freedom when confronted by his own victim, and his life when his schemes are turned against him.

## Drives

The pursuit of easy wealth through the cold calculation of human mortality and corporate vulnerability.
