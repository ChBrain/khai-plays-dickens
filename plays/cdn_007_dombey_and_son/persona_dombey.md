# Persona: Dombey

## Taxonomy

The proud merchants of England: a cold patriarch persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Dombey and Son](play_cdn_007_dombey_and_son.md)

## Projection

Mr. Dombey, a cold, stiff, handsome man in his [Merchant](position_merchant.md) position, who wears a heavy watch and holds himself with rigid, unbending pride.

## Action

Ignore his daughter Florence, educate his son Paul, marry Edith Granger, strike Florence in a rage, and face bankrutpcy alone.

## Shadow

His mercantile pride makes him blind to domestic affection, leading him to sacrifice his family's happiness for the sake of the firm.

## Tell

He consults his watch, stands with hands behind his back, and speaks of the firm Dombey and Son.
