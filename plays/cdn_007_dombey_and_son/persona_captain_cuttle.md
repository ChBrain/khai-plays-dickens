# Persona: Captain Cuttle

## Taxonomy

The retired sailors of London: a warm companion persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Dombey and Son](play_cdn_007_dombey_and_son.md)

## Projection

Ned Cuttle, a retired ship's captain, with a red, weather-beaten face, a hook instead of a right hand, and a very hard glaze hat.

## Action

Keep the Wooden Midshipman shop, shelter Florence during her flight, and cheer Walter Gay upon his return.

## Shadow

His warm, simple heart offers a direct moral contrast to Dombey's cold, calculating pride, acting as Florence's true guardian.

## Tell

He adjusts his hook, taps his glaze hat, and declares: "When found, make a note of."
