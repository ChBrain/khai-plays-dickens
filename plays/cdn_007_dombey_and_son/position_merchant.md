# Position: Merchant

## Taxonomy

The directors of London commerce: a proud merchant role.

## Owner

- Project: [Dombey and Son](play_cdn_007_dombey_and_son.md)

## Has

A grand London office, a leather ledger book, merchant vessels, and a mandate to direct the wholesale shipping firm Dombey and Son.

## Orders

Manage shipping ledgers, direct office clerks, demand absolute submission from family and employees, and secure an heir to continue the firm.

## Loses

The affection of his loving daughter, the life of his fragile heir, and the stability of his commercial firm through prideful neglect.

## Drives

The expansion of the mercantile house Dombey and Son, treating all human relationships as transactions subordinate to the firm.
