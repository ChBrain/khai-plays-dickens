# Persona: Paul Dombey

## Taxonomy

The fragile heirs of London: a delicate child persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Dombey and Son](play_cdn_007_dombey_and_son.md)

## Projection

A small, sickly, old-fashioned child, with wide, thoughtful eyes and a weak physical frame, who seems too wise for his years.

## Action

Study at Dr. Blimber's academy under his father's plan, grow weaker in Brighton, and die in Florence's arms.

## Shadow

His fragile health is audited by the heavy classical lessons and his father's ambitious expectations, leading to his early death.

## Tell

He asks: "Papa, what's money after all?" and looks out at the sea waves.
