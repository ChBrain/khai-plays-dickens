# Persona: Edith

## Taxonomy

The proud widows of England: a proud protagonist persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Dombey and Son](play_cdn_007_dombey_and_son.md)

## Projection

Edith Granger, a beautiful, stately young widow, with a proud, flashing eye and a cold, haughty demeanor that refuses to submit.

## Action

Marry Mr. Dombey, protect Florence, refuse to submit to her husband's demands, flee to France with Carker, and reject Carker to ruin Dombey.

## Shadow

Her pride is the exact match for Dombey's, leading to a domestic cold war that destroys their home and drives Carker to flight.

## Tell

She flashes her dark eyes, speaks in cold, measured tones, and draws Florence to her side.
