# Persona: Florence

## Taxonomy

The neglected daughters of London: a loving daughter persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Dombey and Son](play_cdn_007_dombey_and_son.md)

## Projection

A sweet, gentle young girl, whose face reflects a deep, unrequited love for her cold father, and whose spirit remains pure despite neglect.

## Action

Comfort her fragile brother Paul, seek her father's affection in vain, flee to the Wooden Midshipman, marry Walter, and rescue her ruined father.

## Shadow

Her absolute devotion is the moral balance to Dombey's cold pride, eventually rescuing him from suicide and despair.

## Tell

She speaks in a soft, imploring voice and stretches out her hands to comfort others.
