# Place: Midshipman Shop

## Taxonomy

The old shops of London: a nautical instrument shop.

## Owner

- Project: [Dombey and Son](play_cdn_007_dombey_and_son.md)

## Shown

A small, cozy, old-fashioned shop in London, filled with brass sextants, wooden chronometers, telescopes, and sea charts.

## Holds

The wooden midshipman sign outside, Sol Gills's workbench, Captain Cuttle's tea-tray, and old sea chests.

## Offers

A warm, simple sanctuary of maritime safety, old-fashioned gossip, and shelter for Florence in her flight.

## Withheld

Cold commercial calculations and proud ledgers, which have no place among the brass instruments.
