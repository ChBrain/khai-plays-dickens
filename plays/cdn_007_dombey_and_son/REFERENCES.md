# Dombey and Son: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's novel _Dombey and Son_ (1846). Staging mercantile pride, corporate treachery, railway fate, and family reconciliation.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _Dombey and Son_, published in monthly parts from 1846 to 1848, one of The Novels in the house catalog (`cdn_007`, [DS]). The text is in the public domain.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **The catalog is the index**: this warrant answers for one staged work; the full Dickens catalogue and its `cdn_007` numbering live in the house register, not here.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
