# Position: Manager

## Taxonomy

The administrators of firms: a corporate manager role.

## Owner

- Project: [Dombey and Son](play_cdn_007_dombey_and_son.md)

## Has

An office desk next to the merchant, correspondence files, bank accounts, and a mandate to audit accounts and manage clerks.

## Orders

Audit firm accounts, manage foreign correspondence, execute the merchant's domestic commands, and plot secret financial frauds.

## Loses

The trust of his merchant employer, the protection of the laws, and his life when his treachery is exposed.

## Drives

The secret exploitation of the firm's assets and his employer's pride, smiling with white teeth while plotting financial ruin.
