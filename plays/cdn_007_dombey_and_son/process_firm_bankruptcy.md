# Process: Firm Bankruptcy

## Taxonomy

The financial collapse of shipping houses: a firm bankruptcy.

## Owner

- Project: [Dombey and Son](play_cdn_007_dombey_and_son.md)

## Initiated by

James Carker's secret embezzlements and speculations, combined with Mr. Dombey's prideful neglect of his business affairs in his grief.

## Direction

The progressive insolvency of the great shipping house Dombey and Son, leading to the closing of offices, sale of assets, and bankruptcy.

## Lever

The audits of the executors and the demands of the creditors, meeting the empty safe left by Carker's flight.

## Echo

The firm is dissolved, the grand mansion is stripped, and the humbled Mr. Dombey is left destitute and alone in the empty rooms.
