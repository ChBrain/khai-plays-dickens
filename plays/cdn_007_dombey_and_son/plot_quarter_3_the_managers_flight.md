# Plot: The Manager's Flight

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Dombey and Son](play_cdn_007_dombey_and_son.md)

## Cue

A stormy autumn night in France, moving to the platforms of [Railway Station](place_railway_station.md).

## Action

[Edith](persona_edith.md) flees to France, prompting [James Carker](persona_carker.md) in his [Manager](position_manager.md) position to run from [Dombey](persona_dombey.md), who strikes [Florence](persona_florence.md) in his rage.

## Stage

Carker flees in terror back to London, falling under the wheels of the speeding [Iron Train](piece_iron_train.md) at the railway station.

## Tension

Edith's rejection of Carker ruins Dombey's pride, and Carker's gruesome death under the train resolves his treachery in a sudden industrial horror.
