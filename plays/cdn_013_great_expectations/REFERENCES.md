# Great Expectations: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's novel _Great Expectations_ (1860). A drama of social ambition and class snobbery, staging Satis House decay, a convict benefactor, and the forge sanctuary.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _Great Expectations_, published in weekly parts from 1860 to 1861, one of The Novels in the house catalog (`cdn_013`, [GE]). The text is in the public domain.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
