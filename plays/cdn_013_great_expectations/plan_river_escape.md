# Plan: River Escape Plan

## Taxonomy

The flight by river: a plan of river escape.

## Owner

- Owner: [Pip](persona_pip.md)

## Direction

To smuggle the convict Abel Magwitch down the River Thames to meet a departing steamer, escaping Jaggers's warnings and police arrest.

## Orders

Pip is ordered to hire a rowboat, keep it at a dock near London Bridge, practice rowing with Herbert Pocket, and convey Magwitch to the steamer.

## Implementation

The River Thames and marshes near Gravesend, utilizing Herbert Pocket's rowing help.

## Targets

- [ ] Pip hires a rowboat and practices rowing on the Thames
- [ ] He hides Magwitch in a riverside lodging near the docks
- [ ] They row Magwitch down the river to board the steamer
