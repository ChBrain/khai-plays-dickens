# Persona: Estella

## Taxonomy

The cold beauties of Kent: a cold pride persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Great Expectations](play_cdn_013_great_expectations.md)

## Projection

Estella, a beautiful, proud young woman raised by Miss Havisham, whose cold face reflects a complete lack of sympathy or heart.

## Action

Insult young Pip, study in Europe, return to break hearts, marry Bentley Drummle, suffer his cruelty, and meet Pip again at Satis House.

## Shadow

Her cold, heartless behavior is the result of her upbringing, leaving her unable to love even the devoted Pip until she is broken by suffering.

## Tell

She looks at Pip with a cold, mocking smile, declaring: "I have no heart, Pip."
