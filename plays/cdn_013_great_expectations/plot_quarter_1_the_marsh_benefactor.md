# Plot: The Marsh Benefactor

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Great Expectations](play_cdn_013_great_expectations.md)

## Cue

A cold, misty morning on the Kent marshes, starting inside [place_gargery_forge.md](place_gargery_forge.md).

## Action

[Pip](persona_pip.md) lives with [Joe Gargery](persona_joe_gargery.md) in his [Blacksmith](position_blacksmith.md) position at the forge, helps an escaped convict, and is summoned to [Satis House Place](place_satis_house.md) where he meets [Miss Havisham](persona_miss_havisham.md) and the proud [Estella](persona_estella.md) under the [Great Expectations Pitch](pitch_great_expectations.md).

## Stage

Joe hammers at the [Gargery Anvil](piece_gargery_anvil.md), while the convict files his [Magwitch Legiron](piece_magwitch_legiron.md) in the marsh grass, and Miss Havisham sits before her cobwebbed [Wedding Cake](piece_wedding_cake.md).

## Tension

Pip falls in love with Estella and becomes ashamed of the simple forge, longing to become a gentleman.
