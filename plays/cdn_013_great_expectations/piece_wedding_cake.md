# Piece: Wedding Cake

## Taxonomy

The symbols of frozen grief: a decayed wedding cake.

## Owner

## Place

## Load Bearing

Miss Havisham's frozen grief. Without this decayed wedding cake covered in cobwebs and spiders representing her abandonment on her wedding day, Satis House decay collapses.

## Apparent

A large, multi-tiered wedding cake, completely covered in grey cobwebs, dust, and running spiders, sitting on a long table.

## Yearbook

- Twenty years ago, the cake was placed on the feast table.
- Today, Miss Havisham points to the cake as her future grave.
- The room catches fire, destroying the table and the cake.
