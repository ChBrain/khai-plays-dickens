# Plot: The Great Expectations

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Great Expectations](play_cdn_013_great_expectations.md)

## Cue

A busy, dusty morning in London, starting inside [Jaggers Chambers](place_jaggers_chambers.md).

## Action

[Jaggers](persona_jaggers.md) announces Pip's fortune, sending [Pip](persona_pip.md) to London where he undergoes the [Expectations Corruption](process_expectations_corruption.md) process, becoming a snobbish gentleman and neglecting Joe Gargery.

## Stage

Jaggers washes his hands in his dark office, while Pip spends money in his London chambers and ignores letters from the forge.

## Tension

Pip's social ambition is fulfilled, but his moral character is corrupted by unearned wealth and snobbery.
