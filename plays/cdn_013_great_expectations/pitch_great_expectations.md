# Pitch: Great Expectations

## Taxonomy

The tuning of tone for the whole play: a production directive.

## Owner

- Owner: [Great Expectations](play_cdn_013_great_expectations.md)

## Tenor

A damp, misty marsh landscape in Kent, smelling of mud and river-weed, contrasted with the dark, cobwebbed rooms of Satis House, the dusty London offices of Mr. Jaggers, and the warm, bright sparks of Joe's forge.

## Undertow

The rhythmic clinking of Joe's anvil hammer, the clatter of the blacksmith's forge, and the heavy iron scraping of Magwitch filing his leg-iron in the marsh grass.

## Nerve

The emotional tension of Estella's cold pride, Miss Havisham's frozen grief, and the danger of Magwitch's illegal return to London.

## Echo

A persistent warning that unearned wealth and social ambition rot the moral character, while the simple, honest labor of the forge remains the only true sanctuary.
