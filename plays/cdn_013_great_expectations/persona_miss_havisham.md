# Persona: Miss Havisham

## Taxonomy

The decaying brides of Kent: a decaying jilted bride persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Great Expectations](play_cdn_013_great_expectations.md)

## Projection

Miss Havisham of Satis House, an eccentric lady dressed in a faded, yellowed bridal dress and veil, living in candle-lit darkness.

## Action

Summon young Pip, command him to play, raise Estella to break men's hearts, confess her deception, and die after her dress catches fire.

## Shadow

Her frozen grief is a tool of revenge against the male sex, but she realizes too late that she has ruined Estella's capacity for love.

## Tell

She points with her withered hand to the stopped clock, whispering: "Play, play, play!"
