# Persona: Joe Gargery

## Taxonomy

The gentle blacksmiths of Kent: a blacksmith persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Great Expectations](play_cdn_013_great_expectations.md)

## Projection

Joe Gargery, a giant, fair-haired man in his [Blacksmith](position_blacksmith.md) position, with a face of child-like goodness and mild blue eyes.

## Action

Work at the forge, sing the Old Clem song, receive Pip in London, pay Pip's debts, nurse him back to health, and marry Biddy.

## Shadow

His gentle, passive nature makes him a victim of his wife's hot temper, but his quiet integrity remains Pip's truest moral guide.

## Tell

He pats Pip on the head, saying in his mild voice: "What larks, Pip, old chap!"
