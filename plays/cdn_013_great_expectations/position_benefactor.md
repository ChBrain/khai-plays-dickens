# Position: Benefactor

## Taxonomy

The secret benefactors of England: a secret benefactor role.

## Owner

- Project: [Great Expectations](play_cdn_013_great_expectations.md)

## Has

A sheep-farming fortune in Australia, a secret agent (Jaggers), a list of expectations, and a mandate to support the protégé.

## Orders

Earn fortunes in exile, send agents with expectations to London, return in secret at risk of life, and seek to see the gentleman he made.

## Loses

The right to live in England, his fortune to state confiscation, and his life to injuries sustained during escape.

## Drives

The creation of a gentleman out of a poor boy who once helped him on the marshes, using his fortune to buy status.
