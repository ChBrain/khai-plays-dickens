# Persona: Pip

## Taxonomy

The ambitious orphans of Kent: a protagonist persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Great Expectations](play_cdn_013_great_expectations.md)

## Projection

Philip Pirrip (Pip), a young man of earnest, reflective face, who grows from a simple forge apprentice to a snobbish, debt-ridden gentleman.

## Action

Steal a file for the convict, visit Miss Havisham, receive Jaggers's announcement, study in London, neglect Joe Gargery, and rescue Magwitch.

## Shadow

His unearned expectations make him snobbish and ashamed of the forge, until his ruin and Magwitch's sacrifice teach him true values.

## Tell

He writes his memories in a large ledger, reflecting on the illusions of his youth.
