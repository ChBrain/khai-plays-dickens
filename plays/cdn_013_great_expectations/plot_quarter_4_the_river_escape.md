# Plot: The River Escape

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Great Expectations](play_cdn_013_great_expectations.md)

## Cue

A cold, misty dawn on the River Thames, shifting to the quiet forge on the marshes.

## Action

[Pip](persona_pip.md) executes the [River Escape Plan](plan_river_escape.md) to smuggle [Magwitch](persona_magwitch.md) out, but Magwitch is captured and dies, and the ruined Pip is nursed and saved by [Joe Gargery](persona_joe_gargery.md) at [place_gargery_forge.md](place_gargery_forge.md).

## Stage

Magwitch lies dying in the prison cell, while Joe pats Pip on the head beside the [Gargery Anvil](piece_gargery_anvil.md) and Pip returns to work.

## Tension

The expectations are gone and Magwitch is dead, but Pip finds redemption and moral sanctuary back at the blacksmith forge.
