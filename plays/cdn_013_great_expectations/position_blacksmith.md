# Position: Blacksmith

## Taxonomy

The blacksmiths of Kent: a blacksmith role.

## Owner

- Project: [Great Expectations](play_cdn_013_great_expectations.md)

## Has

A forge, an anvil, a hammer, a modest house, and a mandate to weld iron.

## Orders

Work at the forge, sing anvil songs, pay the debts of loved ones, and offer simple domestic sanctuary to runaways.

## Loses

The companionship of his apprentice when he goes to London, but never his patience or love.

## Drives

The honest, simple endurance of manual labor, remaining true to his friends and his forge despite their snobbish neglect.
