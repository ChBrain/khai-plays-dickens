# Plot: The True Benefactor

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Great Expectations](play_cdn_013_great_expectations.md)

## Cue

A stormy, wet London night, with wind howling up the stairs.

## Action

The convict [Magwitch](persona_magwitch.md) in his [Benefactor](position_benefactor.md) position returns to London and reveals himself to [Pip](persona_pip.md) as the true source of his expectations, while [Estella](persona_estella.md) marries Bentley Drummle.

## Stage

Magwitch holds out his rough hands to Pip, while Pip recoils in horror and Jaggers washes his hands in [Jaggers Chambers](place_jaggers_chambers.md).

## Tension

Pip's illusions are shattered: his fortune comes from a criminal, not Miss Havisham, leaving him ruined and heart-broken.
