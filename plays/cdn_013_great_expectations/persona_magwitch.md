# Persona: Magwitch

## Taxonomy

The escaped convicts of the Kent marshes: a secret benefactor persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Great Expectations](play_cdn_013_great_expectations.md)

## Projection

Abel Magwitch, a fierce, rough-cast convict in his [Benefactor](position_benefactor.md) position, who has made a sheep-farming fortune in Australia.

## Action

Escape from the Hulks, demand food and file from Pip, send Jaggers with the fortune, return to London, and die in the prison infirmary.

## Shadow

His rough, criminal exterior hides a deep, simple gratitude, prompting him to risk his life to see the gentleman he made.

## Tell

He eats roughly, stroking his grey hair, and barks: "I made you a gentleman, Pip!"
