# Process: Expectations Corruption

## Taxonomy

The moral decay of social ambition: an expectations corruption.

## Owner

- Project: [Great Expectations](play_cdn_013_great_expectations.md)

## Initiated by

Mr. Jaggers's announcement of Pip's great expectations, sending the simple blacksmith boy to study in London as a gentleman.

## Direction

The progressive social snobbery and moral decay, showing how unearned wealth makes Pip ashamed of Joe Gargery and his forge life.

## Lever

Mr. Jaggers's law desk and hand-washing basin, meeting Pip's debts and his snobbish rejection of his old friends.

## Echo

Pip's fortune is confiscated when Magwitch is arrested, and the ruined Pip is rescued and nursed by the faithful Joe Gargery.
