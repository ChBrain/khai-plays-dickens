# Persona: Edward

## Taxonomy

The returned sons of England: a traveler persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Cricket on the Hearth](play_ccb_003_the_cricket_on_the_hearth.md)

## Projection

A young, handsome traveler who returns from South America, first appearing disguised as a deaf old stranger.

## Action

Travel in John's cart, stay at the cottage in disguise, and eventually reveal himself to claim his love May Fielding.

## Shadow

His secret return and disguise are what create the misunderstanding between John and Dot, nearly wrecking their home.

## Tell

He cup his hand to his ear when playing the deaf stranger, and stands tall when he throws off the disguise.
