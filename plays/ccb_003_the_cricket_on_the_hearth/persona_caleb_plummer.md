# Persona: Caleb Plummer

## Taxonomy

The poor artisans of London: a working-class persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Cricket on the Hearth](play_ccb_003_the_cricket_on_the_hearth.md)

## Projection

A poor, shivering old toy-maker, who in his [Toy-Maker](position_toy_maker.md) position works tirelessly to support his blind daughter Bertha.

## Action

Carve wooden figures, paint staring eyes on dolls, and construct a beautiful, imaginary world of comfort for Bertha.

## Shadow

He hides their real poverty and Tackleton's coldness from Bertha, creating a beautiful lie that must eventually confront the truth.

## Tell

He wears a thin sack coat and rubs his cold fingers together before picking up the carving knife.
