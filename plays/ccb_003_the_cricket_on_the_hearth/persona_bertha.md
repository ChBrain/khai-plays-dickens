# Persona: Bertha

## Taxonomy

The children of the poor: a blind persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Cricket on the Hearth](play_ccb_003_the_cricket_on_the_hearth.md)

## Projection

A sweet, blind young woman, who lives in a beautiful world of imagination created by her father's loving descriptions.

## Action

Dress dolls, feel the shapes of the toy horses, and love the generous "friend" Tackleton whom her father has invented.

## Shadow

She is cut off from the physical truth of her environment, making her love the cold master Tackleton until the truth is revealed.

## Tell

She tilts her head to listen and touches the toys with delicate fingers.
