# Persona: The Cricket

## Taxonomy

The spirits of the hearth: a ghostly persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Cricket on the Hearth](play_ccb_003_the_cricket_on_the_hearth.md)

## Projection

A tiny, insect figure that resides in the brickwork of the hearth, rising as a swarm of domestic fairies to defend the home.

## Action

Chirp to establish domestic peace, comfort John in his night of doubt, and summon the spirits of home to counsel him.

## Shadow

It has no physical power to alter the characters' choices, acting only through moral resonance and the comfort of its song.

## Tell

Its sharp, clear chirp rings out whenever the home is threatened or celebrated.
