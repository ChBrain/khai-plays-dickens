# Position: Carrier

## Taxonomy

The trades of transport: a carrier role.

## Owner

- Project: [The Cricket on the Hearth](play_ccb_003_the_cricket_on_the_hearth.md)

## Has

A sturdy horse, a large delivery cart, and a mandate to deliver parcels safely and reliably through all weathers.

## Orders

Deliver packages to the town, bring back the mail and goods, and protect the home cottage with steady industry.

## Loses

The luxury of leisure, the freedom from weather constraints, and the youthful agility of those who live in towns.

## Drives

The reliable transport of parcels and people, providing a steady connection between country cottages and town markets.
