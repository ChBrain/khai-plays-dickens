# Persona: John Peerybingle

## Taxonomy

The carriers of England: a working-class persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Cricket on the Hearth](play_ccb_003_the_cricket_on_the_hearth.md)

## Projection

A large, honest, slow-spoken man, who in his [Carrier](position_carrier.md) position drove the cart between the town and the cottages, representing the reliable heart of the country.

## Action

Drive the cart, deliver parcels, love his young wife Dot, and sit by the hearth in silent grief when he suspects her of betrayal.

## Shadow

He is slow to understand Dot's feelings and quick to believe that his age has made him a burden to her, nearly sacrificing his marriage to her supposed happiness.

## Tell

He strokes his dog Boxer and smokes his pipe slowly when deep in thought.
