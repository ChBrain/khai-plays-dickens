# Plot: The Shadow on the Hearth

## Cue

A winter day, moving from the cottage to [Tackleton's Counting-House](place_tackleton_counting_house.md).

## Action

[Tackleton](persona_tackleton.md) visits the cottage and plants seeds of doubt in [John Peerybingle](persona_john_peerybingle.md)'s mind, later showing him [Dot](persona_dot.md) secretly embracing the young [Edward](persona_edward.md) who has removed his disguise.

## Stage

John looks through the window of Tackleton's workshop, watching his young wife help the traveler hide, while Tackleton stands beside him with a cold chuckle.

## Tension

John is filled with a terrible jealousy and grief, believing his wife has betrayed him with a younger man, while Dot must keep the secret of Edward's return to protect him.
