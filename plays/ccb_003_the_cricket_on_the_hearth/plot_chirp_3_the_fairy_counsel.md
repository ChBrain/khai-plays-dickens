# Plot: The Fairy Counsel

## Owner

## Cue

A quiet, dark night in the cottage, where the fire burns low on the [Cricket Hearth](piece_cricket_hearth.md).

## Action

[John Peerybingle](persona_john_peerybingle.md) sits by the hearth and resolves the [Marital Sacrifice Plan](plan_marital_sacrifice.md), before [The Cricket](persona_cricket.md) chirps and summons the spirits to restore [Domestic Harmony](process_domestic_harmony.md) and reconcile John with [Dot](persona_dot.md) when [Edward](persona_edward.md) reveals himself.

## Stage

John sits in his large chair by the hearth at [Peerybingle Cottage](place_peerybingle_cottage.md), while the spirits of the home appear to him in his thoughts. The next morning, [Tackleton](persona_tackleton.md) joins the celebration.

## Tension

John must conquer his own feelings of betrayal and choose to sacrifice his own happiness for Dot's, before the morning sun brings the true explanation and resolves the tension with joy.
