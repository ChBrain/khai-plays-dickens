# Position: Toy-Maker

## Taxonomy

The offices of toy-making: an artisan role.

## Owner

- Project: [The Cricket on the Hearth](play_ccb_003_the_cricket_on_the_hearth.md)

## Has

A workbench, paint-pots, wood-carving tools, and the mandate to construct dolls and toy horses for a master merchant.

## Orders

Carve wooden figures, paint staring eyes on dolls, and color wooden horses to match Tackleton's orders.

## Loses

The right to keep his own profits, the comfort of a decent wage, and the freedom to tell his daughter the truth of their poverty.

## Drives

The creation of toys that bring joy to others, while living in a cold, damp workshop himself.
