# The Cricket on the Hearth: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's Christmas Book _The Cricket on the Hearth_ (1845). A fairy tale of home, staging the testing of a carrier's trust and the protective illusions of a poor toy-maker.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _The Cricket on the Hearth_, published in 1845, one of The Christmas Books in the house catalog (`ccb_003`, [CH]). The text is in the public domain.
