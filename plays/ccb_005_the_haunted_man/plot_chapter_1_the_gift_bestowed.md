# Plot: The Gift Bestowed

## Taxonomy

The fytte of three chapters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

## Cue

A cold, dark winter night, where the snow falls on the college arches, starting in [Laboratory](place_laboratory.md).

## Action

[Redlaw](persona_redlaw.md) in his [Lecturer](position_lecturer.md) position sits alone with his sorrowful thoughts, before [The Ghost](persona_ghost_bargain.md) appears to seal the [Ghost's Bargain Plan](plan_ghosts_bargain.md) under the key of the [Green Memory Pitch](pitch_green_memory.md).

## Stage

Redlaw sits by his cold fireplace surrounded by [Chemistry Glass](piece_chemistry_glass.md), while the ghastly double steps from the shadows, initiating the [Memory Infection](process_memory_infection.md) process.

## Tension

Redlaw tries to resist the phantom's bargain of oblivion, before his self-torment drives him to accept the gift of forgetting all past sorrows and wrongs.
