# Process: Memory Infection

## Taxonomy

The moral contagion of oblivion: a memory infection process.

## Owner

- Project: [The Haunted Man](play_ccb_005_the_haunted_man.md)

## Initiated by

Redlaw's bargain with the ghost, which strips him of his sorrowful memories and turns his touch into a contagion of oblivion.

## Direction

The progressive diffusion of memory loss to the Tetterby family and others Redlaw meets, turning their warm affections into cold calculation.

## Lever

Redlaw's presence/contact acting as the infection carrier, and Milly's simple kindness acting as the reversing force.

## Echo

The affected characters recover their memories and affections, and Redlaw resolves to keep his memory green.
