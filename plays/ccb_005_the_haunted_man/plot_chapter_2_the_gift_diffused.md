# Plot: The Gift Diffused

## Taxonomy

The fytte of three chapters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [The Haunted Man](play_ccb_005_the_haunted_man.md)

## Cue

A winter night, moving through the streets from the college to [Tetterby Home](place_tetterby_home.md).

## Action

[Redlaw](persona_redlaw.md) goes about the town and spreads the [Memory Infection](process_memory_infection.md) to [Tetterby](persona_tetterby.md) and [Philip Swidger](persona_philip_swidger.md) in his [Custodian](position_custodian.md) position, while the [Street Boy](persona_street_boy.md) remains immune.

## Stage

Redlaw visits the crowded home where Tetterby rocks the [Tetterby Cradle](piece_tetterby_cradle.md), and walks with the wild street child who follows him back to the dark college.

## Tension

Redlaw's presence strips the poor family of their moral memories, turning their warm domestic cooperation into cold, grumbling selfishness and strife.
