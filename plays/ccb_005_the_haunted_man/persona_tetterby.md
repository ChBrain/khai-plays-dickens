# Persona: Tetterby

## Taxonomy

The newsmen of London: a working-class news-seller persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Haunted Man](play_ccb_005_the_haunted_man.md)

## Projection

Adolphus Tetterby, a poor, hardworking newsman, carrying the heavy burden of supporting a large family in crowded rooms.

## Action

Sell newspapers, rock the baby, love his wife, and later fall into cold calculation and domestic strife when infected by Redlaw.

## Shadow

He is normally patient and loving, but the loss of his memories of shared struggle makes him selfish and bitter toward his family.

## Tell

He sighs heavily and counts his small change on the news-shop counter.
