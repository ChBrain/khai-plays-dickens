# Persona: The Ghost

## Taxonomy

The phantoms of memory: a ghostly persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Haunted Man](play_ccb_005_the_haunted_man.md)

## Projection

An exact double of Redlaw, but hollow-eyed and ghastly, representing the dark shadow of his past wrongs and painful memories.

## Action

Haunt Redlaw's study, offer him the bargain of oblivion, and bestow the power of spreading memory loss.

## Shadow

It has no physical existence outside Redlaw's mind, acting only as the echo of his own self-torment.

## Tell

It mimics Redlaw's movements and speaks with a hollow, whispering voice.
