# Position: Custodian

## Taxonomy

The servants of the college: a custodian role.

## Owner

- Project: [The Haunted Man](play_ccb_005_the_haunted_man.md)

## Has

A cozy lodge at the college gates, a bunch of keys for the rooms, and the mandate to serve the chemistry professors and maintain the fireplaces.

## Orders

Keep the keys, sweep the rooms, light the fires for the lecturers, and care for the old college buildings.

## Loses

The right to live without heavy physical work, and the wealth of the scholars he serves.

## Drives

The daily maintenance of college life, keeping the gates and fireplaces working for the scholars.
