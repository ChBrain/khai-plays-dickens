# Position: Lecturer

## Taxonomy

The scholars of the college: a chemistry lecturer role.

## Owner

- Project: [The Haunted Man](play_ccb_005_the_haunted_man.md)

## Has

A dark chemistry laboratory, shelves of chemical reagents, a lecture hall, and the mandate to teach student scholars the science of elements.

## Orders

Analyze the composition of matter, lecture the college students, and maintain a quiet, scholarly isolation from the town.

## Loses

The warmth of family life, the joy of simple companionship, and the peace of mind undisturbed by past sorrows.

## Drives

The pursuit of academic truth and chemical formulas, living in dark chambers surrounded by old memories.
