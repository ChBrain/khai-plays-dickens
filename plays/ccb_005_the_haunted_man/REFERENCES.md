# The Haunted Man: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's Christmas Book _The Haunted Man and the Ghost's Bargain_ (1848). The ghost's bargain, staging a chemist's flight from memory and the moral infection of oblivion in a college town.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _The Haunted Man and the Ghost's Bargain_, published in 1848, one of The Christmas Books in the house catalog (`ccb_005`, [HM]). The text is in the public domain.
