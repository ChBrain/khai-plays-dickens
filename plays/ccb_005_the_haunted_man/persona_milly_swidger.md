# Persona: Milly Swidger

## Taxonomy

The wives of custodians: a domestic helper persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Haunted Man](play_ccb_005_the_haunted_man.md)

## Projection

A warm, cheerful, good-natured woman who assists in the [Custodian](position_custodian.md) duties, representing pure love, gratitude, and moral memory.

## Action

Tend the sick, comfort the old custodian Philip, bring food to the poor, and restore the memories of those affected by Redlaw's touch.

## Shadow

She is immune to the contagion of oblivion because her heart is filled with selfless love, making her the natural doctor of this moral sickness.

## Tell

She speaks with a gentle, soothing voice and carries a basket of simple comfort goods.
