# Pitch: Green Memory

## Taxonomy

The tuning of tone for the whole play: a production directive.

## Owner

- Owner: [The Haunted Man](play_ccb_005_the_haunted_man.md)

## Tenor

A Victorian moral register, contrasting the dark, mechanical coldness of the chemistry lab with the green, warm, light of memory and domestic affection.

## Undertow

The deep, solemn whisper of the phantom double, contrasted with the cheerful bustle of the Custodian's lodge and Milly's laugh.

## Nerve

The reader's dread of the moral contagion of oblivion, where simple gratitude and sympathy vanish under the touch of cold intellect.

## Echo

A persistent, prayerful plea to keep our memories green, acknowledging that our sorrows are what nurture our capacity for love.
