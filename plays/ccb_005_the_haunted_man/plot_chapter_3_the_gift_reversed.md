# Plot: The Gift Reversed

## Taxonomy

The fytte of three chapters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [The Haunted Man](play_ccb_005_the_haunted_man.md)

## Cue

A bright, clear Christmas Day morning, starting in [Swidger Lodge](place_swidger_lodge.md).

## Action

[Milly Swidger](persona_milly_swidger.md) uses her gentle, loving presence to reverse the [Memory Infection](process_memory_infection.md), restoring memory and love to [Tetterby](persona_tetterby.md) and [Philip Swidger](persona_philip_swidger.md), while [Redlaw](persona_redlaw.md) begs [The Ghost](persona_ghost_bargain.md) to recall the gift.

## Stage

The family gathers in the custodian's lodge under the Christmas [Holly Wreath](piece_holly_wreath.md), as Milly's simple charity heals the cold bitterness and reconciles everyone.

## Tension

Redlaw must learn to value the memory of sorrow as the source of human sympathy, pleading with the spirits as the restoration of memory returns joy to the home.
