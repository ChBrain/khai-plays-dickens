# Persona: Street Boy

## Taxonomy

The children of the streets: a wild, homeless persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Haunted Man](play_ccb_005_the_haunted_man.md)

## Projection

A wild, nameless street child, dirty, ragged, and fierce, who has never known home or affection.

## Action

Prowl the streets, seek warmth, curl up by Redlaw's fire, and remain immune to the contagion of memory loss.

## Shadow

He is immune to Redlaw's touch because he has no memories of love or sorrow to lose, representing the horror of a human soul stripped of moral memories from birth.

## Tell

He growls like a wild animal and shrinks from any hand raised to touch him.
