# Persona: Philip Swidger

## Taxonomy

The old servants of the college: a retired custodian persona.

## Owner

- Project: [The Haunted Man](play_ccb_005_the_haunted_man.md)

## Projection

An eighty-seven-year-old custodian in his [Custodian](position_custodian.md) position, with silver hair and a happy old face, full of gratitude for a long life.

## Action

Sit by his fire, praise the college lecturers, and later lose all his gratitude and become grumbling and selfish when touched by Redlaw.

## Shadow

His happy old age is built on the memories of the past, which Redlaw's touch strips away, leaving him cold.

## Tell

He strokes his silver hair and talks of the ancient college history with a shaking voice.
