# Piece: Railway Signal

## Taxonomy

The symbols of railway transit: a railway signal lamp.

## Owner

- Project: [Christmas Stories](play_csf_006_christmas_stories.md)

## Place

[Mugby Junction](place_mugby_junction.md)

## Load Bearing

Mugby Junction operations. Without this railway signal lamp representing station safety and transit directions, the Mugby Junction station operations collapse.

## Apparent

A large metal railway signal lamp with red and green glass lenses, mounted on a wooden post.

## Yearbook

- For years, Mugby railwaymen operated this signal to direct trains.
- Today, the signal director turns the lamp to direct the night mail train.
- The signal remains a symbol of station safety and transit control.
