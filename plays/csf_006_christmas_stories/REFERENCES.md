# Christmas Stories: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's shorter work _Christmas Stories_ (1850). A festive collection of winter charity, lodging-house adoptions, street auctions, and railway junction redemptions.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _Christmas Stories_, published from 1850 to 1867, one of Shorter Fiction and Sketches in the house catalog (`csf_006`, [CS]). The text is in the public domain.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **The catalog is the index**: this warrant answers for one staged work; the full Dickens catalogue and its `csf_006` numbering live in the house register, not here.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
