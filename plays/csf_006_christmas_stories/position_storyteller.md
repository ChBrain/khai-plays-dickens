# Position: Storyteller

## Taxonomy

The travellers and storytellers of Christmas: a storyteller role.

## Owner

- Project: [Christmas Stories](play_csf_006_christmas_stories.md)

## Has

A travel flask, a collection of stories, a pocket notebook, and a mandate to share Christmas cheer.

## Orders

Visit local charities, host festive dinners for travellers, listen to personal stories of soldiers and guests, and write them down.

## Loses

His loneliness, when he shares a warm fire and a story with poor travellers on Christmas Eve.

## Drives

The cultivation of Christmas charity and the collection of stories of human reconciliation.
