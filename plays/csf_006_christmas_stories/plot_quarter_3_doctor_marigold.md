# Plot: Doctor Marigold

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Christmas Stories](play_csf_006_christmas_stories.md)

## Cue

A colorful cheap jack cart parked in a London street market.

## Action

[Doctor Marigold](persona_marigold.md) operates his [Cheap Jacks Cart](piece_cheap_jacks_cart.md) to auction goods, while executing the [Marigold Education](process_marigold_education.md) process in [place_lirriper_lodgings.md](place_lirriper_lodgings.md) to teach the adopted deaf-and-dumb [Sophy](persona_sophy.md) to read and communicate using his [Handwritten Book](piece_handwritten_book.md).

## Stage

Doctor Marigold hawking goods on his cart footboard, while Sophy sits inside the cart studying the handwritten book and practicing hand signs.

## Tension

Marigold's public auction humor is balanced by his private, fatherly devotion to the silent child.
