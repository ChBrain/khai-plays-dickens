# Plot: The Golden Thread

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [A Tale of Two Cities](play_cdn_012_a_tale_of_two_cities.md)

## Cue

A quiet London courtroom, shifting to a leafy house garden in Soho.

## Action

[Charles Darnay](persona_darnay.md) is tried for treason but acquitted thanks to [Sydney Carton](persona_carton.md)'s resemblance, and Darnay marries [Lucie](persona_lucie.md), who serves as the golden thread of the household.

## Stage

Sydney Carton looks dissolutely at his reflection, while Lucie sits under the plane-tree in Soho and Jarvis Lorry manages assets at [Tellsons Bank](place_tellsons_bank.md).

## Tension

Carton's vow of devotion to Lucie is recorded, while Darnay's marriage is celebrated, but the dark storm in France begins to loom.
