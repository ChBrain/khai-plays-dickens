# Pitch: Two Cities

## Taxonomy

The tuning of tone for the whole play: a production directive.

## Owner

- Owner: [A Tale of Two Cities](play_cdn_012_a_tale_of_two_cities.md)

## Tenor

A duality of atmosphere: the quiet, dark, musty halls of Tellson's Bank and the leafy domestic sanctuary of London, contrasted with the wine-spilled streets, the clanking blade of the Guillotine, and the roaring mobs of Paris.

## Undertow

The relentless, mechanical march of the revolution, the rhythmic click of Madame Defarge's knitting needles, and the clanking iron of prison gates.

## Nerve

The terror of the revolutionary tribunal trials and the desperation of Carton's silent prison switch.

## Echo

A persistent assurance that death is conquered by self-sacrificing love, and that a willing sacrifice brings the truest resurrection.
