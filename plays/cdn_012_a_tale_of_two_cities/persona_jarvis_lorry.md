# Persona: Jarvis Lorry

## Taxonomy

The loyal clerks of Tellson's Bank: a bank clerk persona.

## Owner

- Project: [A Tale of Two Cities](play_cdn_012_a_tale_of_two_cities.md)

## Projection

Jarvis Lorry, a neat, elderly gentleman in his [Bankclerk](position_bankclerk.md) position, who claims to be a man of business but has a warm, fatherly heart.

## Action

Convey Dr. Manette to London, support Lucie's household, travel to Paris for Tellson's, and arrange the family's flight from Paris.

## Shadow

His insistence that he is a "mere man of business" is a defense against emotional involvement, though his actions show deep devotion.

## Tell

He adjusts his wig, pats his pockets, and declares: "Tellson's is a business house, my dear."
