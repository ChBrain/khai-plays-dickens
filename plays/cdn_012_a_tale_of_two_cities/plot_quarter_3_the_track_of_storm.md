# Plot: The Track of Storm

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [A Tale of Two Cities](play_cdn_012_a_tale_of_two_cities.md)

## Cue

A stormy, blood-red morning in Paris, starting inside [Bastille Prison](place_bastille_prison.md).

## Action

The Bastille is stormed, prompting [Madame Defarge](persona_madame_defarge.md) in her [Knitter](position_knitter.md) position to lead the mob, while [Charles Darnay](persona_darnay.md) returns to France to save Gabelle, only to be arrested under the [Revolutionary Justice](process_revolutionary_justice.md) process.

## Stage

Madame Defarge knits names into the [Defarge Register](piece_defarge_register.md) at the wine shop counter, while Darnay is marched between armed guards.

## Tension

Darnay's arrest is complete, exposing him to the Tribunal, while Dr. Manette uses his status as a former prisoner to protect him.
