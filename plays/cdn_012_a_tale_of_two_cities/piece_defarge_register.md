# Piece: Defarge Register

## Taxonomy

The symbols of revolutionary vengeance: a knitted register.

## Owner

## Place

## Load Bearing

Madame Defarge's register of vengeance. Without this knitted register representing the names of the condemned encoded in her wool yarn, the knitter position collapses.

## Apparent

A long, wool knit strip, with complex patterns of stitches representing the names of those marked for death by the revolution.

## Yearbook

- For years, Madame Defarge knitted names while watching the wine shop.
- Today, she knits Darnay's name into the register as he is arrested.
- The register is left behind when she goes to her final duel with Miss Pross.
