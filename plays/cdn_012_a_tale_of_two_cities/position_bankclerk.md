# Position: Bankclerk

## Taxonomy

The clerks of Tellson's Bank: a bank clerk role.

## Owner

- Project: [A Tale of Two Cities](play_cdn_012_a_tale_of_two_cities.md)

## Has

A Tellson's desk, ledgers, travel clearances, and a mandate to manage deposits and messages.

## Orders

Manage bank deposits, convey messages across the English Channel, arrange flights for clients, and protect old business records.

## Loses

The comfort of business routine, and the safety of London when sent to revolutionary Paris to protect assets.

## Drives

The loyal, business-like service to the bank and its clients, risking safety to fulfill duty.
