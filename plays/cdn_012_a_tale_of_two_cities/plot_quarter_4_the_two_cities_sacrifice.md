# Plot: The Two Cities Sacrifice

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [A Tale of Two Cities](play_cdn_012_a_tale_of_two_cities.md)

## Cue

A cold, grey morning at the Conciergerie cell, shifting to the public square in Paris.

## Action

[Charles Darnay](persona_darnay.md) is condemned by the [Revolutionary Justice](process_revolutionary_justice.md) process, but [Sydney Carton](persona_carton.md) executes the [Prison Switch Plan](plan_prison_switch.md) to take his place, going to his death under the [Guillotine Blade](piece_guillotine_blade.md) while Darnay and Lucie escape.

## Stage

Carton stands on the scaffold looking up, while Madame Defarge is killed in a duel with Miss Pross, and the carriage containing Darnay speeds away.

## Tension

The mechanical violence of the Guillotine claims Carton, but his sacrifice achieves his own resurrection and Darnay's safety.
