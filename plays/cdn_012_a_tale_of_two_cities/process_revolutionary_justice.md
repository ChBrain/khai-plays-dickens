# Process: Revolutionary Justice

## Taxonomy

The mechanical trials of Paris: a revolutionary justice.

## Owner

- Project: [A Tale of Two Cities](play_cdn_012_a_tale_of_two_cities.md)

## Initiated by

The Revolutionary Tribunal, condemning aristocrats, emigrants, and Gabelle's defenders to execution under the Law of suspects.

## Direction

The progressive arrest and execution of former nobles, showing how personal vengeance replaces trial and law.

## Lever

## Echo
