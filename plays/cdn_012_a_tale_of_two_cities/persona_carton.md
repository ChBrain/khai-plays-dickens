# Persona: Carton

## Taxonomy

The dissolute advocates of London: a self-sacrificing advocate persona.

## Owner

- Project: [A Tale of Two Cities](play_cdn_012_a_tale_of_two_cities.md)

## Projection

Sydney Carton, a brilliant but dissolute English lawyer, who looks physically identical to Charles Darnay but bears a careworn, wasted face.

## Action

Assist Stryver at the trial, confess his love to Lucie, travel to Paris, execute the prison switch, and go to the Guillotine in Darnay's place.

## Shadow

His dissolute, alcohol-fueled life is a waste of talent, but his physical resemblance to Darnay becomes the tool of his ultimate redemption.

## Tell

He looks at his reflection, mutters of his wasted life, and says: "It is a far, far better thing that I do..."
