# Persona: Dr Manette

## Taxonomy

The shoemaking prisoners of the Bastille: a father persona.

## Owner

- Project: [A Tale of Two Cities](play_cdn_012_a_tale_of_two_cities.md)

## Projection

Dr. Alexandre Manette, a white-haired gentleman whose face bears the marks of eighteen years of unjust confinement in the Bastille.

## Action

Hammer shoes obsessively in Paris, return to London, recover his sanity through Lucie's care, and use his prisoner status to protect Darnay.

## Shadow

His shoe-making habit returns whenever he faces extreme stress, representing the psychological scar of his Bastille captivity.

## Tell

He hammers leather at a small bench, muttering: "One Hundred and Five, North Tower."
