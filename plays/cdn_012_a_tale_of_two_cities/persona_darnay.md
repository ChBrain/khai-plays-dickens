# Persona: Darnay

## Taxonomy

The renouncing aristocrats of France: an emigrant persona.

## Owner

- Project: [A Tale of Two Cities](play_cdn_012_a_tale_of_two_cities.md)

## Projection

Charles Darnay, a handsome, noble-featured young man, who has renounced his French aristocratic name (Evrémonde) to live as a tutor in London.

## Action

Be tried for treason, marry Lucie Manette, return to Paris to save Gabelle, be condemned by the Tribunal, and be switched out of prison.

## Shadow

His honorable return to Paris is a noble duty, but it ignores the danger of revolutionary vengeance, exposing his family to immediate ruin.

## Tell

He speaks in an earnest, noble tone, declaring his renunciation of his family's estates.
