# A Tale of Two Cities: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's novel _A Tale of Two Cities_ (1859). A drama of resurrection and violence, staging the dual cities, Madame Defarge's register, and Carton's sacrifice.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _A Tale of Two Cities_, published in weekly parts in 1859, one of The Novels in the house catalog (`cdn_012`, [TTC]). The text is in the public domain.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **The catalog is the index**: this warrant answers for one staged work; the full Dickens catalogue and its `cdn_012` numbering live in the house register, not here.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
