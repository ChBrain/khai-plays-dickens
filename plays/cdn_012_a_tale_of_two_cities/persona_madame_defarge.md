# Persona: Madame Defarge

## Taxonomy

The vengeful knitters of Saint Antoine: a revolutionary knitter persona.

## Owner

- Project: [A Tale of Two Cities](play_cdn_012_a_tale_of_two_cities.md)

## Projection

Madame Thérèse Defarge, a strong-featured, cold-eyed French woman in her [Knitter](position_knitter.md) position, who constantly knits and watches.

## Action

Knit the names of the condemned, lead the Saint Antoine mobs, demand Darnay's execution, seek Lucie's death, and die in a duel with Miss Pross.

## Shadow

Her cold, relentless drive for vengeance is fueled by the past crimes of the Evrémonde brothers against her peasant family.

## Tell

She knits silently at the wine shop counter, looking up with a cold, expressionless stare.
