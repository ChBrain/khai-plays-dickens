# Plot: Recalled to Life

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [A Tale of Two Cities](play_cdn_012_a_tale_of_two_cities.md)

## Cue

A dark, foggy evening in Dover and a wine-spilled street in Saint Antoine, starting inside [Wine Shop](place_wine_shop.md).

## Action

[Jarvis Lorry](persona_jarvis_lorry.md) in his [Bankclerk](position_bankclerk.md) position travels to Paris with [Lucie](persona_lucie.md) to retrieve [Dr Manette](persona_manette.md) who has been released from the Bastille, and they cross back to London under the key of the [Two Cities Pitch](pitch_two_cities.md).

## Stage

Dr. Manette sits in the dark attic room hammering at the [Shoemakers Bench](piece_shoemakers_bench.md), recognizing no one until Lucie's golden hair recalls him to life.

## Tension

Dr. Manette's fragile sanity is threatened by the sudden transition, but Lucie's care secures his initial recovery.
