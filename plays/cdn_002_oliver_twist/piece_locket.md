# Piece: Gold Locket

## Taxonomy

## Load Bearing

Oliver's birth identity and inheritance. Without this gold locket containing his mother's hair and name, Monks's plot and the proof of Oliver's parentage collapse.

## Apparent

A small gold locket, worn and tarnished, containing two locks of hair and the name "Agnes" engraved on the inside.

## Yearbook
