# Process: Pickpocketing

## Taxonomy

The criminal operations of Fagin's den: a pickpocketing process.

## Owner

- Project: [Oliver Twist](play_cdn_002_oliver_twist.md)

## Initiated by

Fagin's instruction in the garret, where the Artful Dodger and Charley Bates demonstrate the game of picking Fagin's pockets without being caught.

## Direction

The execution of thefts in the crowded London streets, showing how the boys mark a target, extract the handkerchief or watch, and hand it off.

## Lever

The coordination of Fagin's boys and the distraction of the crowd, meeting Oliver's first real exposure at the book-stall.

## Echo

Oliver is arrested, Fagin's gang flees, and the boy is rescued by Mr. Brownlow.
