# Persona: Mr. Bumble

## Taxonomy

The officers of the parish: a beadle persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Oliver Twist](play_cdn_002_oliver_twist.md)

## Projection

A large, stout man in his [Beadle](position_beadle.md) position, with a red face and a gold-laced coat, representing the self-important authority of the parish.

## Action

Apprentice Oliver to Sowerberry, sell the mother's locket to Monks, marry the workhouse matron, and end up as a poor workhouse lodger himself.

## Shadow

His self-importance hides his greed and cowardice, easily falling into corruption when offered money for secrets.

## Tell

He taps his parish cane on the ground and puffs out his chest to show his authority.
