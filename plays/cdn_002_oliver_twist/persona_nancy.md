# Persona: Nancy

## Taxonomy

The girls of the streets: an underworld persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Oliver Twist](play_cdn_002_oliver_twist.md)

## Projection

A young woman in a shabby red gown and a dirty bonnet, whose face is worn by the street life but retains the capacity for deep affection.

## Action

Help Fagin recapture Oliver, later shield Oliver from Sikes's anger, and meet Mr. Brownlow in secret to reveal the plot.

## Shadow

She is bound to Bill Sikes and the thieves by habits of fear and love, unable to escape the gang herself even while saving Oliver.

## Tell

She rocks back and forth and wrings her hands when struggling with her conscience.
