# Persona: Oliver

## Taxonomy

The parish orphans of England: a child persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Oliver Twist](play_cdn_002_oliver_twist.md)

## Projection

A pale, thin child, who had been hovered over by the workhouse board and beaten by undertakers, but whose face remains clear and innocent.

## Action

Ask for more gruel, run away to London, resist Fagin's lessons, and recover his true name under Mr. Brownlow's protection.

## Shadow

He is a passive target for the social and criminal machinery around him, unable to defend himself without the help of Nancy and Brownlow.

## Tell

He clasps his hands and speaks with a soft, pleading voice: "Please, sir, I want some more."
