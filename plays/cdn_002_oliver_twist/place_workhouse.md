# Place: Workhouse

## Taxonomy

The institutions of the poor law: a parish workhouse.

## Owner

- Project: [Oliver Twist](play_cdn_002_oliver_twist.md)

## Shown

A cold, grey brick workhouse, where the wind blows through the narrow windows, and the copper vat stands in the hall.

## Holds

The copper vat, the wooden tables, the board-room, and the sleeping wards for the orphans.

## Offers

A cold, starvation shelter for the parish poor, where hunger is regulated by the board.

## Withheld

Comfort, warmth, and any sense of home or family affection.
