# Oliver Twist: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's novel _Oliver Twist; or, The Parish Boy's Progress_ (1837). Staging the workhouse economy, Fagin's pickpocket academy, and Monks's plot to destroy Oliver's inheritance.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _Oliver Twist; or, The Parish Boy's Progress_, published in monthly parts from 1837 to 1839, one of The Novels in the house catalog (`cdn_002`, [OT]). The text is in the public domain.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **The catalog is the index**: this warrant answers for one staged work; the full Dickens catalogue and its `cdn_002` numbering live in the house register, not here.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
