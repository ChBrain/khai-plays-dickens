# Place: Fagin's Den

## Taxonomy

The retreats of London thieves: a dark den.

## Owner

A dark, dirty back garret, with a greasy table and a low fire, where the smell of frying sausages fills the air.

A secret shelter for pickpockets and thieves, where Fagin trains his gang in the shadows.
