## Action

## Stage

Nancy stands by the river steps telling what she knows to protect [Oliver](persona_oliver.md), before Sikes returns to the den in a brutal rage.

## Tension

Nancy risks her life to save the boy from Fagin's gang, leading to her murder by Sikes and the flight of the criminals.
