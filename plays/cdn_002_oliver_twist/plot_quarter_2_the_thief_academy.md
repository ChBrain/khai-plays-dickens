# Plot: The Thief Academy

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Oliver Twist](play_cdn_002_oliver_twist.md)

## Cue

A smoke-filled London morning, starting in [Fagin's Den](place_fagin_den.md) and moving to [Brownlow Home](place_brownlow_home.md).

## Action

[Fagin](persona_fagin.md) in his [Fence](position_fence.md) position trains [Oliver](persona_oliver.md) in the [Pickpocketing](process_pickpocketing.md) process, leading to Oliver's capture and rescue by Mr. Brownlow.

## Stage

Fagin place stolen pocket-handkerchiefs on the line, letting the boys pick pockets with the [Silk Handkerchief](piece_handkerchief.md) as practice.

## Tension

Oliver is shocked to discover the boys are thieves, before he is caught by the crowd at the book-stall and rescued by the benevolent master.
