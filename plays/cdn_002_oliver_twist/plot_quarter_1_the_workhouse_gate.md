# Plot: The Workhouse Gate

## Taxonomy

The fytte of four quarters: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [Oliver Twist](play_cdn_002_oliver_twist.md)

## Cue

A cold winter night in the parish, starting in [Workhouse](place_workhouse.md).

## Action

[Oliver](persona_oliver.md) is born in the workhouse, where [Mr. Bumble](persona_bumble.md) in his [Beadle](position_beadle.md) position regulates the poor under the key of the [Innocence Squeeze Pitch](pitch_innocence_squeeze.md).

## Stage

Oliver sits at the long wooden tables, holding his empty [Gruel Bowl](piece_gruel_bowl.md) and asking the master for more, which leads to his apprenticeship and flight to London.

## Tension

Oliver's simple request for food is treated as a rebellion by the board, forcing him to flee to the city to escape starvation.
