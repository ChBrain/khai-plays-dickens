# Position: Beadle

## Taxonomy

The officers of the parish: a beadle role.

## Owner

- Project: [Oliver Twist](play_cdn_002_oliver_twist.md)

## Has

A gold-laced cocked hat, a brass-headed cane, a parish registry of births, and the mandate to regulate the workhouse budget.

## Orders

Lecture the parish orphans, starve the workhouse boys to save costs, apprentice troublesome boys to undertakers, and maintain the majesty of the parish.

## Loses

The capacity for simple compassion, the warmth of domestic respect, and the standing of a true guardian of the poor.

## Drives

The cheap regulation of poverty, translating the parish laws into systematic starvation and cold threats.
