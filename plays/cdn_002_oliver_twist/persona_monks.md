# Persona: Monks

## Taxonomy

The plotters of inheritance: a dark brother persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Oliver Twist](play_cdn_002_oliver_twist.md)

## Projection

A young man named Edward Leeford, with a dark, fit-throwing look and a red mark on his throat, who goes by the name Monks.

## Action

Hire Fagin to destroy Oliver's reputation, destroy the mother's gold locket, and eventually confess his plot to Mr. Brownlow under threat of exposure.

## Shadow

His hatred of Oliver is driven by greed and a vow to his mother to destroy his half-brother's reputation and inheritance.

## Tell

He hides in a dark cloak and starts violently at the sound of thunder.
