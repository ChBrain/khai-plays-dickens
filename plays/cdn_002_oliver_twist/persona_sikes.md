# Persona: Sikes

## Taxonomy

The housebreakers of London: a brutal thief persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Oliver Twist](play_cdn_002_oliver_twist.md)

## Projection

Bill Sikes, a stout, broad-shouldered man in a black velvet coat and dirty trousers, who speaks with a gruff voice and is followed by a white dog.

## Action

Plan and execute burglaries, force Oliver to assist him, murder Nancy in a rage, and accidentally hang himself while fleeing the mob.

## Shadow

He represents the raw, unpolished brutality of the criminal underworld, operating by threats and violence without Fagin's calculations.

## Tell

He swears and kicks his dog Bull's-eye when angry.
