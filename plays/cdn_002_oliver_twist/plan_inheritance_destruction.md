# Plan: Inheritance Destruction Plan

## Taxonomy

The suppression of birth right: a plan of inheritance destruction.

## Owner

- Owner: [Monks](persona_monks.md)

## Direction

To destroy all evidence of Oliver's true identity, and to force him into a life of crime so that he is convicted as a thief, thereby forfeiting his share of the father's estate.

## Orders

Monks is ordered to pay Fagin to keep Oliver in his gang, recover and destroy the mother's gold locket, and arrange for Oliver to be caught in a burglary.

## Implementation

The London underworld and Fagin's den, utilizing Fagin's pickpockets and Sikes's housebreaking operations.
