# Piece: Gruel Bowl

## Taxonomy

The utensils of the workhouse: a wooden bowl.

## Owner

- Project: [Oliver Twist](play_cdn_002_oliver_twist.md)

## Place

[Workhouse](place_workhouse.md)

## Load Bearing

The workhouse starvation economy. Without this wooden bowl Oliver holds when he asks for more gruel, the initial trigger of his flight and the parish scandal collapses.

## Apparent

A small, rough wooden bowl, scraped clean of all food, held in a child's thin hands.

## Yearbook

- For three years, Oliver has eaten his daily pint of gruel from this bowl.
- Today, he holds the bowl and walks up to the master, asking for more.
- The bowl is left behind when Oliver is apprenticed.
