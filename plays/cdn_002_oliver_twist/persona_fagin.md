# Persona: Fagin

## Taxonomy

The receivers of London: an old fence persona.

## Owner

- Project: [Oliver Twist](play_cdn_002_oliver_twist.md)

## Projection

A shrivelled old man in a greasy flannel gown, with matted red hair and a villainous face, who in his [Fence](position_fence.md) position stands over his fire.

## Action

Train young pickpockets, hide stolen watches, plot with Monks to destroy Oliver, and face execution in Newgate.

## Shadow

He acts as a benevolent protector to homeless boys, while actually training them for the gallows to line his own pockets.

## Tell

He chuckles and addresses the boys with a sinister, wheedling tone: "my dear."
