# Position: Fence

## Taxonomy

The receivers of stolen goods: a fence role.

## Owner

- Project: [Oliver Twist](play_cdn_002_oliver_twist.md)

## Has

A dark back-room den, a collection of stolen pocket-handkerchiefs, paint to mark out initials, and a network of young pickpockets.

## Orders

Train young boys to steal handkerchiefs and watches, turn stolen goods into cash, keep secrets from the officers, and threaten any boy who wavers.

## Loses

The security of a legal trade, the right to walk in the daylight without fear, and the peace of a clean conscience.

## Drives

The training and exploitation of homeless street children, converting their thefts into profit while shielding his own skin.
