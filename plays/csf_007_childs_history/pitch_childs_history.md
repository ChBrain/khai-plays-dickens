# Pitch: Child's History Pitch

## Taxonomy

The tuning of tone for the whole play: a production directive.

## Owner

- Owner: [A Child's History of England](play_csf_007_childs_history.md)

## Tenor

A cozy, firelit Victorian study where the quiet rustle of manuscript pages and the crackle of coal fires contrast with the dark, stone coldness of ancient castles and battlefields.

## Undertow

The steady, repetitive march of centuries, the distant echo of iron swords clashing on shields, the scratching of monastic pens, and the heavy thud of the headsman axe.

## Nerve

The determined effort of a father to strip away the false glamour of royalty and teach his children that true nobility lies in justice, mercy, and the defense of common liberty.

## Echo

A persistent reminder that history is not a playground of glorious kings, but a long struggle of ordinary people seeking light and freedom from royal oppression.
