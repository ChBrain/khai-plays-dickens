# Persona: Child Reader

## Taxonomy

The children of Gads Hill: a young listener persona.

## Owner

- Project: [A Child's History of England](play_csf_007_childs_history.md)

## Projection

The Child Reader, a quiet listener in his [Pupil](position_pupil.md) position, who absorbs the history of England through the dramatic stories read by the fire.

## Action

Listen to the father's reading, ask about ancient customs, and imagine the castles and battlefields.

## Shadow

He can get sleepy from the warmth of the hearth or lose track of the many kings who share the same name.

## Tell

He leans forward with his elbows on his knees, watching his father's face with quiet concentration.
