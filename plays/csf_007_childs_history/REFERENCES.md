# A Child's History of England: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging Charles Dickens's shorter work _A Child's History of England_ (1851). A narrative retelling of English history, educating children through moral audits of monarchs.

## Origin

Charles Dickens, _A Child's History of England_, published from 1851 to 1853, one of Shorter Fiction and Sketches in the house catalog (`csf_007`, [CHE]). The text is in the public domain.
