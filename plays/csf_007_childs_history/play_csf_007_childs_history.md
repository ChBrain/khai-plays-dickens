[khai-plays-dickens](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-plays-dickens)

## Name

A Child's History of England

## Arc

A moral and narrative journey through the history of England, leading a young listener from the early Roman conquest through the struggles of medieval kings, the tyranny of the Tudors, and the final establishment of constitutional liberty in the Revolution of 1688.
