# Position: Instructor

## Taxonomy

The educators of youth: a history instructor role.

## Owner

- Project: [A Child's History of England](play_csf_007_childs_history.md)

## Has

A written manuscript, reference books, and a quiet hearthside seat.

## Orders

Read the history pages to the children, explain the context of ancient battles, and point out the moral failings of past rulers.

## Loses

His academic detachment when describing historical acts of cruelty, and his patience with defenders of corrupt kings.

## Drives

The desire to foster a love of liberty and moral integrity in the minds of young listeners.
