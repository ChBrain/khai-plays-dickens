# Plot: Tudor Tyranny

## Taxonomy

The fytte of royal absolute rule: a narrative plot.

## Owner

- Project: [A Child's History of England](play_csf_007_childs_history.md)

## Cue

The candles are trimmed as the narrative enters the sixteenth century.

## Action

[Charles Dickens](persona_dickens.md) reads from the [History Manuscript](piece_history_manuscript.md) to describe the marriages and executions of King Henry the Eighth, conducting a moral audit under the process of [Chronological Narration](process_chronological_narration.md).

## Stage

[Charles Dickens](persona_dickens.md) describes the prisoners locked behind the walls of the [Tower of London](place_tower_of_london.md) who watched the sparkles of the [Royal Crown](piece_royal_crown.md) from their cell windows, explaining this to the [Child Reader](persona_child_reader.md).

## Tension

The religious persecutions under Queen Mary and the absolute authority of the Tudors create a tense discussion about the preservation of individual conscience.
